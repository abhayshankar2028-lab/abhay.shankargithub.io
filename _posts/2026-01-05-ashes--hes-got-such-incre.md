In an exclusive interview, England cricket team captain Joe Root praised Australian opener Travis Head, praising his incredible hand-eye coordination. "He's got such incredible hand-eye coordination that he's always looking to throw punches," Root said in an interview. Head has emerged as a key player in England's Ashes campaign, hitting crucial hundreds against Australia. Root's comments highlight the impressive batting skills of Head, who has already scored crucial runs in the series. With Root at the helm, England is fighting back in the Ashes, and Head's batting prowess has been a significant factor. The praise is also a testament to Root's admiration for his team's resilience and determination. As the Ashes continue, one thing is clear – Head's impressive batting skills have earned him a special place in the England captain's affections.
