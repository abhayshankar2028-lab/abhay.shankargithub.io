**Neena Gupta Opens Up About Her Biggest Regret: Saath Saath**

In a recent interview, renowned Bollywood actress Neena Gupta shared her candid thoughts on one of her most iconic films - Saath Saath. Speaking about the movie, Neena revealed that her biggest mistake was choosing to star alongside Salman Khan. "That film took away my chance of being seen as a heroine," she candidly stated. Neena, who was known for her tough on-screen persona, felt that Saath Saath marked a turning point in her career, where she was typecast as a damsel in distress. The actress was initially hesitant about the role, but ultimately had to adapt to her character. Neena's candid confession serves as a reminder that even the biggest stars can have regrets, and it's never too late to make a change.
