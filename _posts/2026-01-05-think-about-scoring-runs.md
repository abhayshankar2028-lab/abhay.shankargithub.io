![Think about scoring runs and not getting out: Ricky Ponting’s advice to India T20 skipper Suryakumar Yadav](https://images.indianexpress.com/2025/12/Surya-14.jpg)

As India's T20 skipper Suryakumar Yadav navigates the intricacies of the game, his captain Ricky Ponting offers valuable advice to guide him towards success. Ponting emphasizes the importance of focusing on scoring runs rather than getting out. "You can't control what's happening on the field, but you can control how you react to it," Ponting shared. "If you can get your run-scoring skills sharp and your defensive skills solid, you'll be in a much better position to succeed. Don't get frustrated if you don't get a run, just focus on being calm and collected." Ponting's words serve as a timely reminder that staying focused, adaptable, and composed are key to success in T20 cricket. By applying these principles, Suryakumar Yadav can set himself up for a successful tournament and propel India to their desired T20 glory.
