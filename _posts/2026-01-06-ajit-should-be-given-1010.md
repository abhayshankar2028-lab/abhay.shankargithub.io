In a recent interview, renowned Indian cricket commentator and spin legend Ajit Agarkar expressed his immense admiration for India's T20 World Cup squad selection. Agarkar praised Team India's decision to include the 10/10 batsman in their squad, stating that his batting skills are the reason behind this selection. Agarkar highlighted that his ability to score big hundreds has been a crucial factor in the team's success. He pointed out that Agarkar's exceptional batting skills and experience made him a shoo-in for the squad. Agarkar also praised the team's management for making the right call, stating that it's a strategic move that will benefit the team in the long run. With Agarkar's endorsement, it's clear that India is confident about their chances in the T20 World Cup.
