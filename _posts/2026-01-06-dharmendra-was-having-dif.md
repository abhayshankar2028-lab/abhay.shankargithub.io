**Dharmendra's Hidden Dancer**

While it may seem unbelievable, the legendary Dharmendra, a Bollywood icon, once struggled with getting up in the morning. The old-timer's excuse? He couldn't be bothered to get out of bed, let alone learn dance steps at 3 am. For those who know Dharmendra, this revelation brings back memories of his numerous dance encounters.

In an exclusive interview, Dharmendra revealed his inner turmoil. "Why can I not do this?" he lamented. "I've danced my way to stardom, and now I'm stuck in bed, unable to get up. It's like my body's not listening." Dharmendra's words shed light on the often-misunderstood world of dance tutorials and the immense pressure that comes with performing at the highest level. Despite the challenges, Dharmendra's determination and passion for dance remain unwavering, and his legacy continues to inspire aspiring dancers around the world.
