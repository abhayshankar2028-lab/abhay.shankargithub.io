**Jana Nagad Star Jason Shah Opens Up on Indian Actors' Insecurity**

In a recent interview, Bollywood star Jason Shah, known for his roles in Jana Nagad, shared his candid thoughts on the insecurity prevalent among Indian actors. When asked about the common perception that 90% of Indian actors are insecure, Shah expressed his skepticism.

"I think that's just a stereotype, and there are many talented actors who are comfortable with their roles and don't let their insecurity affect their work," Shah said. "As for coming into work, I'm not sure if that's true or not," he added with a hint of humor. Shah's candid remarks come as a refreshing perspective, highlighting the reality of the acting industry. His words resonate with many, who feel that the pressure to constantly produce content can take a toll on an actor's mental health. Shah's refreshing take offers a glimpse into the complexities of the Indian acting world.
