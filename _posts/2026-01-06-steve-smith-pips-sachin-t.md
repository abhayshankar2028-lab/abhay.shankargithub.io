**Steve Smith's Historic Test Breakthrough**

In a thrilling match at the SCG on March 14, 2015, the Australian captain, Steve Smith, shattered the Ashes record, notching his 37th Test century. The milestone victory helped Australia secure a 2-0 lead in the series. However, what made this achievement truly remarkable was that it came just days before Sachin Tendulkar's Test retirement.

As the 95-year-old icon bid adieu to international cricket, Smith's remarkable milestone marked a new chapter in the Ashes rivalry. This historic breakthrough not only propelled Australia to new heights but also shattered the record for the most Test centuries in the Ashes. Smith's heroics left fans and cricket enthusiasts alike in awe, cementing his place in cricket history.
