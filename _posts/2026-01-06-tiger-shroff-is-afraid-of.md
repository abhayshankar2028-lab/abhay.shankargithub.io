**Tiger Shroff's Flying Fears: The Ultimate Showdown**

In a recent interview, Bollywood superstar Tiger Shroff opened up about his fear of flying. The Dabangg star confessed that he is terrified of air travel, which only adds to the mystique of his on-screen daredevil persona.

However, when it comes to flying, Tiger Shroff has a different story to tell. According to him, his fear of flying is just one aspect of a very responsible and meticulous approach to his craft. The actor claims that he doesn't fear jumping off a building, instead, he has a trusty body double to take his place. By doing so, he still gets paid, ensuring that his elaborate stunts remain a thrilling experience for the audience. This unique approach to his profession is sure to leave fans in awe.
