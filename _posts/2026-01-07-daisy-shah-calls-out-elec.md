In a shocking display of voter activism, Bollywood actress Daisy Shah took to social media to call out election campaigners over a firecracker-induced fire near her apartment. The actress, known for her sharp wit and outspoken personality, stated that the incident highlighted a concerning "lack of civic sense" among some voters. "People must start realizing that their civic sense is very fragile and can be easily shattered," she tweeted. The incident occurred when a firecracker went off near her apartment, causing a nearby fire. The actress then went on to tag election campaigners who allegedly contributed to the situation. The hashtag #CivicSenseMatters trended on social media as Daisy Shah shared her experience, bringing attention to the importance of responsible campaigning. It's clear that this incident has sparked a conversation about voter responsibility and the need for campaign organizers to prioritize voter safety.
