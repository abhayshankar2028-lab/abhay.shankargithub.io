Nasser Hussain expressed his admiration for the outstanding performance of Jacob Bethell at Sydney Ashes Test. Bethell scored a remarkable 124 runs, including his maiden Ashes ton, to help England secure a dominant victory.

In an exclusive interview, Nasser Hussain praised Bethell's calm demeanor, stating, "Innings of a proper No 3, was calm, composed." This assessment highlights Bethell's ability to handle the pressure and maintain a steady approach, which contributed significantly to his team's success.

Hussain further praised Bethell's partnership with Ben Stokes, stating, "They knew each other so well, they just clicked. Their partnership was the backbone of our innings." Bethell's outstanding display showcased his potential as a top-order batsman, and his performance will undoubtedly contribute to England's overall tally in the ongoing Ashes series.
