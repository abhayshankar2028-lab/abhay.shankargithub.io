![‘Something special about this kid’: Mark Waugh says Yashasvi Jaiswal will be the next great Test batter](https://images.indianexpress.com/2025/10/Jaiswal-18.jpg)

In a recent interview, former Australian cricketer Mark Waugh expressed his admiration for Indian batter Yashasvi Jaiswal. Waugh praised Jaiswal's impressive performance in the recently concluded Test series against England, calling him a "young and talented player." Waugh highlighted the significance of Jaiswal's impressive 96 not out from the Indian opener's first Test at Lord's. "He's got the ability to play a Test match, and it's something special about this kid," Waugh said. The former batsman went on to emphasize that Jaiswal's work ethic and dedication will serve him well in his future cricketing career. Waugh believes that the Indian team will be extremely eager to see Jaiswal continue his impressive form. With his impressive display in the recent series, Waugh is confident that Jaiswal will be the next great Test batter.
