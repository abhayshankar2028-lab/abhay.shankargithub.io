In an extraordinary turn of events, former Indian opener Ruturaj Gaikwad achieved a monumental feat in the ongoing ODI series. After being snubbed for a spot in the 16th Man, Gaikwad went on to score an astonishing 15 hundreds in the series, securing his place alongside legends like Sachin Tendulkar and Virender Sehwag in the all-time records.

This incredible achievement showcases Gaikwad's exceptional skills and adaptability, which have earned him a spot in the ODI hall of fame. His remarkable performance has not only raised the bar for Indian openers but also set a new benchmark for teams seeking to challenge the likes of Sachin and Mahendra Singh Dhoni. Gaikwad's remarkable feat is a testament to his dedication, perseverance, and unwavering passion for the game he loves.
