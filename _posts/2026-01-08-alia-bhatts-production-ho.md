Alia Bhatt's production house, Dharma Productions, has taken a strong stance against the ban imposed by the Indian government on Ranveer Singh's film, 'Devadasi'. The film, directed by Anurag Kashyap, is set to release in the Middle East despite facing international censorship. Alia Bhatt's production house has lashed out at the government for not taking a stronger stance against the ban, stating that it is a betrayal to the Indian film industry. The cine body has also extended its support to Ranveer Singh, expressing its disappointment and frustration with the government's decision. The industry body has urged Prime Minister Narendra Modi to intervene and lift the ban, citing the potential loss of revenue for Indian cinemas. This is a clear indication of the industry's deep-seated frustration with the government's handling of the situation.
