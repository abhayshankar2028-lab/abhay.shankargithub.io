**A Big Boost to Bollywood: Alia Bhatt's Production House Lauds Dhurandhar**

Alia Bhatt's production house, Dharma Productions, recently released a heartfelt note in support of the filmmaker, Dhurandhar. The production house expressed their admiration and gratitude for Dhurandhar's work in bringing the story of a young girl's life to the big screen. This gesture is a testament to the power of art and the importance of supporting emerging talent.

Meanwhile, Bollywood's cine body has been vocal about the ban on Ranveer Singh's film in the Middle East. The Body has appealed to Prime Minister Narendra Modi to intervene and lift the ban on the film. It's a pressing issue that requires attention and support from the government. The cry for help from the cine body is a reminder that there's still a need for advocacy and support in the fight for creative freedom.
