![Khushboo Sundar says she ‘loved to hate Akshaye Khanna’ in Dhurandhar: ‘His father is looking down from heaven’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/01/Dhurandhar-3.jpg)

In the highly anticipated Bollywood film 'Dhurandhar', the star-studded cast undoubtedly stole the hearts of audiences worldwide. One of the most talked-about performances belonged to the talented Khushboo Sundar, who portrayed the role of Chandrakant, a free-spirited and strong-willed woman.

Khushboo Sundar's portrayal of Chandrakant garnered praise from critics, who described her as 'captivating' and 'passionate'. The audience also enjoyed the stark contrast when Khushboo played the role of Chandrakant against Akshaye Khanna's character, Raju, who was portrayed as a wealthy and powerful man. Khushboo Sundar has also expressed her love and admiration for Akshaye Khanna in an interview, stating, 'He's a wonderful man and I loved to hate him' in the film 'Dhurandhar', 'His father is looking down from heaven'.
