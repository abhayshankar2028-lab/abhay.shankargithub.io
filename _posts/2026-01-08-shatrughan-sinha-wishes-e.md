**Shatrughan Sinha's Heartfelt Birthday Wishes for Reena Roy**

It's that time of the year – birthday season! Bollywood's talented actor, Shatrughan Sinha, recently expressed his affection for his ex-girlfriend, Reena Roy, on her special day. The 65-year-old veteran actor took to social media to wish Reena a 'loving' birthday, referring to her as 'best actress'. It's clear that Shatrughan has a soft spot for Reena, and we can't help but feel the same way. Reena Roy has made a significant mark in the Indian film industry with her memorable performances in films like 'Kudrat' and 'Prem Ki Guggu'. Shatrughan's words of admiration and affection only add to the excitement surrounding her birthday celebration. Here's to many more years of love, laughter, and talented performances from Reena Roy!
