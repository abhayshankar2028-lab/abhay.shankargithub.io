In a candid conversation with a renowned Bollywood journalist, veteran actor Rekha Bachchan revealed her unapologetic approach to media scrutiny. Unlike her 'due respect' towards the paparazzi, Rekha has not hesitated to court them, flaunting her glamorous life and numerous endorsement deals. The veteran actress drew inspiration from her idol Amitabh Bachchan, stating, 'She can be me if she wants.' Rekha's bold stance on the media has sparked conversations about the changing dynamics of the entertainment industry. As the media landscape continues to evolve, it will be interesting to see how Rekha's unflappable attitude towards paparazzi impacts the way she navigates her public image. Her willingness to push boundaries may be a sign of a new era in Bollywood, where celebrities are no longer afraid to bare their souls for the sake of fame.
