**Unforgettable Moments from Aditya Dhar's Early Days**

In an exclusive interview, Bollywood director Aditya Dhar recalled the memorable moments from his early days in the film industry. He shared a heartwarming anecdote about how his protégé, Priyadarshan, believed in him and treated him like an equal, despite being a newcomer in the industry. Dhar expressed his gratitude to Priyadarshan for giving him a second chance, which ultimately led to his success in Bollywood. Dhar also mentioned his admiration for Priyadarshan's dedication and perseverance, which inspired him to work even harder. Dhar expressed his deep appreciation for Priyadarshan's guidance and mentorship, which has made him the director he is today. As Dhar wrapped up his conversation, it was clear that his gratitude to Priyadarshan will forever be etched in his heart.
