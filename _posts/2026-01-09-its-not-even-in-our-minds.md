In an exclusive interview with Cricket Addict, British singer and former One Direction member Will Young shared his thoughts on the upcoming 3-match ODI series against New Zealand. When asked if they are thinking about avenging their Champions Trophy loss to India, Young expressed a sense of relief and expressed his team's determination to bounce back from the disappointment.

"I don't think we're thinking about revenge," he said. "We know we could have done better, but we're focusing on the present and the next series. We're a team that believes in each other and that's what will help us turn the situation around."

With a resolute tone, Young emphasized that they are focused on performing at their best and winning the series. Will Young's candid response suggests that New Zealand will not be letting their Champions Trophy loss get them down, and their determination to perform will be the key to success against the All Blacks.
