![Priyadarshan congratulates his ‘disciple’ Aditya Dhar on Dhurandhar success, director says, ‘I’ll forever be your student first’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/01/dhurandhar-priyadarshan.jpg)

In a heartwarming gesture, Bollywood veteran director Priyadarshan has congratulated his protégé, Aditya Dhar, on the massive success of Dhurandhar. Dhar's critically acclaimed directorial debut, which features Kriti Sanon, Varun Dhawan, and Nawazuddin Siddiqui, has not only broken box office records but also garnered immense critical acclaim. Priyadarshan, known for his prolific output, praised Dhar for his "unbridled passion" and "unrelenting work ethic." When asked how he felt about his protégé's success, Priyadarshan revealed that Dhar was his "disciple" first, stating, "I'll forever be your student, first and foremost. I'm grateful for the opportunity to watch you grow as a filmmaker." This newfound appreciation from Priyadarshan only adds to the excitement surrounding Dhurandhar, solidifying its place as one of Bollywood's most promising new releases.
