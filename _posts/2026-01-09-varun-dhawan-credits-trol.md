In a surprising turn of events, actor Varun Dhawan has credited trolls for making the hit song "Border 2" from his film Dhoom 3 go viral. The song, which features Varun Dhawan in a dramatic showdown, was initially released without much fanfare but gained massive traction after it was flooded with comments on Twitter. Many fans and trolls alike started making fun of the song's lyrics, using hashtags like #GoVirilization and #Border2Challenge. Varun Dhawan took to social media to express his gratitude towards the trolls, stating, "God is great" in a post that was met with numerous likes and comments. The actor's response has sparked a lively debate, with some hailing him as a hero for embracing the trolls and turning a negative into a positive. The response has once again proved that fans and trolls can have a special bond.
