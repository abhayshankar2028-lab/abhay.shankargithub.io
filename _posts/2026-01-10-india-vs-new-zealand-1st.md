**India vs New Zealand 1st ODI Match Predicted: A Test of Strength**

The highly anticipated 1st ODI match between India and New Zealand is set to take place on July 23, 2023. The match will be played at the Eden Park in Auckland, New Zealand, which boasts a lively atmosphere. 

**India Squad:** Rohit Sharma (c), Shikhar Dhawan, Rishabh Pant, Ishan Kishan, Ravi Chopra, Jai Pandya, Shivam Singh, Sandeep Warrier, Jasprit Bumrah, Bhuvneshwar Kumar, and Shardul Thakur.

**New Zealand Squad:** Kane Williamson (c), Ross Taylor, Martin Guptill, Mark Watson-Brown, James Neesham, Ben Mitchell, Kyle Jamieson, Trent Boult, Mitchell Santner, Adam Zampa, and BlairTodd.

The match is expected to be a closely contested 3-1 match for New Zealand, who aim to exploit India's weaknesses. India's fast bowlers, particularly Jasprit Bumrah and Bhuvneshwar Kumar, will be crucial in containing New Zealand's attack. A win for New Zealand would secure a crucial 1-0 lead in the series.
