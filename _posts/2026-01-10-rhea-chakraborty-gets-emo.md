**Heartbreaking Tribute to Sushant Singh Rajput: Rhea Chakraborty Opens Up**

In a heartfelt and emotional interview, Rhea Chakraborty shared her profound feelings on the passing of her beloved friend and co-star Sushant Singh Rajput. She candidly revealed that the "difficult time" they experienced after Sushant's tragic demise was overwhelming. "We don't need a temple...I just want to be with him," Rhea expressed her deepest sentiments. She expressed the immense love and memory she shared with Sushant, highlighting the strong bond they built through their film projects. Rhea's emotional outburst left her fans in awe, emphasizing the immense impact Sushant had on her life. Her words serve as a poignant reminder of the profound effect her co-star left behind.
