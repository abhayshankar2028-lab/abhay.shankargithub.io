In a stunning turn of events, Shubman Gill has finally broken the silence surrounding his decision to step out of the T20 World Cup squad. The Indian opening batsman took to social media to express his disapproval of being omitted from the team, stating that it was not about his performance but about his position. Gill's comments have sparked discussions about the role of captains and selectors in making such decisions. "I am where I have to be," Gill said. This decision has raised questions about the current state of India's T20 team and whether their captain, Rohit Sharma, has made the right call. Gill's courage in speaking out has also been praised by fans and fellow cricketers alike. The incident has sparked a wider debate about the intricacies of international cricket.
