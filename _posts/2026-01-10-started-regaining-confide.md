Axar Patel, the Indian all-rounder, is sharing his remarkable journey of regaining confidence through his successful batting career. Recently, he opened up about the pivotal moment that led to his batting transformation. According to Patel, he was told by MS Dhoni, the legendary Indian captain, that he needed to start believing in himself. The encouragement from Dhoni was instrumental in Axar's resurgence as a batsman. "MS Dhoni's words of encouragement were a game-changer for me. He instilled in me the confidence to take risks and play my natural game," Axar revealed. The advice from Dhoni not only helped Axar regain his batting form but also led to a more assertive and aggressive approach to the game. Patel's story serves as an inspiration to players worldwide, demonstrating the impact of a supportive mentor on one's growth.
