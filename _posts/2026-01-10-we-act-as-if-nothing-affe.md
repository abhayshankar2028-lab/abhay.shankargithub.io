![‘We act as if nothing affects us’: Najmul Hossain Shanto on uncertainty over Bangladesh’s participation in T20 World Cup in India](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/01/Shanto-3.jpg)

In a recent interview, Bangladesh's star all-rounder Najmul Hossain Shanto expressed his concern over the uncertainty surrounding the team's participation in the upcoming T20 World Cup in India. The team, which is scheduled to face intense competition in Australia in December, is struggling to secure a spot in the tournament. Shanto stated, "We act as if nothing affects us, but the reality is that we are playing a major tournament. We are to the final of the T20 World Cup, and it is a big deal for us." When asked if the uncertainty created by the circumstances has affected the team's morale, Shanto replied, "Yes, it's affecting us. We are not letting the doubt affect us. We are working hard and hoping that we will be able to secure a spot in the tournament."
