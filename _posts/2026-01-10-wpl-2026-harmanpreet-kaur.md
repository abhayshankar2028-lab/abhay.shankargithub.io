**WPL 2026: MI Defeat DC, Harmanpreet Kaur shines for the T20 Blast**

The Women's Premier League (WPL) 2026 has reached its climax, with the Mumbai Indians (MI) securing a thrilling victory over the Delhi Capitals (DC). In a highly anticipated contest, Harmanpreet Kaur and Nat Sciver-Brunt led the charge for MI, with the duo combining their skills to deliver a championship-winning performance.

Kaur, in particular, made a memorable return to the WPL, showcasing her class with a blistering 77 runs from just 50 balls. Sciver-Brunt, meanwhile, impressed with her versatility and cunning, scoring 66 off 52 deliveries. The winning single from Kaur sealed the deal, as MI secured their fifth WPL title. A fitting end to the tournament, the victory cemented MI's status as one of the WPL's top teams.
