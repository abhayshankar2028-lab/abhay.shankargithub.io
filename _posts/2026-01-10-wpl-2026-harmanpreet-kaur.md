WPL 2026: A Thrilling Showdown Erupts!

In a stunning comeback, the Mumbai Indians (MI) have bounced back strongly to defeat the Delhi Capitals (DC) in the Women's Premier League (WPL) 2026. The match on Saturday witnessed a 10-wicket victory for MI in a thrilling contest that saw both teams put up a fierce fight. Harmanpreet Kaur, India's record-breaking captain, put on an incredible show, scoring an impressive 64 runs off just 38 balls. Nat Sciver-Brunt also played a crucial role, scoring 54 runs in 24 balls. The Indian spinners' partnership was instrumental in MI's victory. With 2 wickets conceded, DC's chance to chase down MI's massive total was extremely low. This stunning comeback has propelled MI to the top of the WPL 2026 table.
