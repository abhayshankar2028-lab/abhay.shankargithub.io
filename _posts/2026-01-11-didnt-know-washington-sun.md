**KL Rahul Shocks Fans: 'Didn't know Washington Sundar couldn't run'**

In a stunning turn of events, India's skipper KL Rahul has taken to social media to express his surprise at Washington Sundar's display on the field. During their thrilling victory over New Zealand in the Asia Cup, Sundar's valiant efforts on the field left fans stunned. 

Rahul couldn't help but react to Sundar's remarkable display, joking that he didn't know the young batter couldn't run. "Didn't know Washington Sundar couldn't run," he quipped, leaving fans in stitches. Sundar's impressive display, including a crucial 53-run partnership with Ishan Kishan, has earned him a spot in the 'Best of the Series' award. The victory has sent India to the top of the Asia Cup standings, and fans are eagerly awaiting the next match.
