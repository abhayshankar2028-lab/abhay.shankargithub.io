**Karan Johar's Unspoken Struggle: "Fainting as a Teenager"**

In a candid conversation, Karan Johar opened up about his difficult college days when he was severely dieting. The actor shared that he often fainted due to dehydration, while also expressing his frustration with the societal pressure to maintain an ideal physique. Johar revealed that his mother would often scold him for being 'fat' and 'large', stating that he had to undergo surgery to shed weight. Not surprisingly, Johar has since taken a different approach to body image, crediting his mother's influence and expressing his gratitude towards her for her unwavering support. "I'm not on Ozempic... I just want to live a normal life!" Johar confessed, emphasizing the importance of self-acceptance and mental well-being.
