It seems like Karan Johar is sharing a heartwarming story about his childhood experiences. In an interview, the renowned filmmaker recalls a poignant incident while he was in college. During a dieting phase, Karan allegedly fainted in his hostel room. To his shock and disappointment, his mother called him 'fat' and 'large'. The following year, Karan visited his mother in her home, and she referred to him as an 'emotional eater', not a dieter. It seems like Karan has learned to temper his emotions and not label himself as a 'fat' person. The story highlights Karan's humble beginnings, showcasing the struggles he faced during his formative years.
