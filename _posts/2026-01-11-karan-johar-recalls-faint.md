Karan Johar shared an amusing story on his social media platforms about his college days. During his time, he was diligently dieting, which led to fainting episodes. The incident left him with a distinct memory - his mother, who was worried about his health, called him 'fat' and 'large'. What made the situation even more peculiar was that Karan Johar denied ever taking any medications, including Ozempic, to aid in his weight loss journey. He explained that his mother's criticism motivated him to work harder and maintain a healthy lifestyle. The incident has left many of his fans in stitches, appreciating the humility and dedication that Karan Johar has displayed over the years. The unassuming actor's journey serves as an inspiring reminder that overcoming health issues begins with self-awareness and a strong support system.
