In a thrilling match at Jio Mallya Cup Arena, Gujarat Giants secured a thrilling victory over Delhi Capitals in the Women's Premier League (WPL). Sophie Devine, the Giants' star batter, played a crucial role in their triumph, guiding her team to a high-scoring win. Devine, who scored 66 runs from 64 balls, helped her team to post a total of 133/8 after Delhi Capitals chased it down to secure a score of 134/8.

The Giants' bowling attack, led by Devine, Arti Singh, and Mithali Raj, restricted Delhi Capitals to a competitive score. The Capitals' captain, Harleen Munhara, narrowly missed a century but still managed to post a respectable total. The Giants' victory marked their 4th win in the WPL, cementing their position at the top of the table.
