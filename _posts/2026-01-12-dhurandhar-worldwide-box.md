**Dhurandhar Worldwide Box Office Collection Day 38: Ranveer Singh’s Film Mints Rs 1,255 crore Globally**

In a spectacular performance, Dhurandhar Worldwide, the Indian drama directed by Rajiv Mehra and starring Ranveer Singh, has achieved a monumental milestone - reaching a global box office collection of Rs 1,255 crore. This stunning feat surpasses the impressive collection of Bollywood blockbuster The Raja Saab, starring Prabhas, and solidifies Ranveer Singh's status as one of India's leading actors. The film, which explores themes of love, family, and social issues, has received widespread critical acclaim for its authentic portrayal of rural life. With its talented cast, captivating storyline, and stunning cinematography, Dhurandhar Worldwide has undoubtedly left a lasting impression on the global audience.
