Irfan Pathan recently shed light on the intriguing reason behind KL Rahul's impressive batting form in ODIs. As a veteran specialist, Irfan Pathan credits Rahul's unique ability as a key factor in his consistent performance.

Rahul's ability to play at the right end, coupled with his exceptional footwork, makes him a perfect choice for the No. 6 role. His aggressive strokeplay and ability to control the game, even when facing pressure, are traits that have made him a favorite among fans and coaches alike.

Irfan Pathan, a former Indian captain, believes that Rahul's batting style is a perfect blend of technique, power, and finesse. The combination of his strengths has enabled him to thrive in the ODI format, making him a valuable asset to the Indian team.
