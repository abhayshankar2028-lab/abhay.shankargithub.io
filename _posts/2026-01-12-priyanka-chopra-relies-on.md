According to sources, Priyanka Chopra turned to her grandmother's expert opinion before walking the red carpet for the Golden Globes. 'Hamari daadiyon ne accha sikhaya hai,' which roughly translates to 'Grandmothers know best.' Priyanka has spoken fondly about her grandmother's guidance, which she said helped her choose the perfect outfit. She's often mentioned how she would rely on her grandmother's advice on everything from fashion to life. Priyanka's grandmother's wisdom seems to have paid off, as she looked stunning at the Golden Globes. With her grandmother's blessing, Priyanka Chopra truly knew she had the perfect look.
