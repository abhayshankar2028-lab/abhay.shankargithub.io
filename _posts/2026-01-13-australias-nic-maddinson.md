Australia's Nic Maddinson has made a triumphant return to the cricket scene after battling cancer. The 29-year-old former Australian opener made his comeback in the Big Bash League (BBL) for the Sydney Thunder. Maddinson, who was diagnosed with acute myeloid leukemia (AML) in 2019, has been on an incredible journey of recovery and rehabilitation.

Since making his comeback, Maddinson has been displaying his exceptional batting skills and showcasing his unwavering dedication to his craft. His impressive performances have caught the attention of cricket enthusiasts and fans alike. As Maddinson continues to rehabilitate and grow stronger, he remains focused on his ultimate goal – to return to the international stage and play cricket at the highest level.

With his incredible resilience and determination, Nic Maddinson is an inspiration to many, proving that 'just want to play' is indeed the right attitude.
