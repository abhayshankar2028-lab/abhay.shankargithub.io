**Parasakthi Amidst Online Storm: Sudha Kongara Speaks Out**

In a poignant display of solidarity, renowned Bollywood star Sudha Kongara has come forward to support Parasakthi, who has been facing intense online harassment. The actress, known for her roles in the films 'Dangal' and 'Baahubali', claims that she was subjected to targeted attacks on social media platforms. Kongara expressed her shock and disappointment at the blatant disregard for her personal safety, stating that she had no time to challenge the censor cuts that fueled these attacks. "As an actress, I've faced my fair share of criticism, but this is something else," Kongara said. Her words serve as a powerful reminder of the importance of protecting our online presence and the need for greater accountability in the entertainment industry.
