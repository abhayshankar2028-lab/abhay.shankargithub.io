![Suniel Shetty breaks down recalling son Ahan’s struggles after debut film flopped, says ‘he went through a lot in life’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/01/Suniel.jpg)

In a heartwarming conversation, Bollywood actor Suniel Shetty recently shared his genuine thoughts on his son Ahan's struggles after the debut film of his son flopped. The veteran actor emphasized that his child has indeed faced numerous challenges since then. Shetty expressed his pride in Ahan's resilience, stating, "He went through a lot in life, but that's what makes him strong. A lot of people doubted him, but his talent is genuine. He's a hard worker and has worked tirelessly to prove himself." The actor also commended Ahan's perseverance in overcoming doubts and criticism. This candid remark from Suniel Shetty has indeed warmed the hearts of many Ahan's fans and those who have witnessed his journey.
