India's very own Harmanpreet Kaur once again proved her dominance on the cricket pitch, securing a thrilling seven-wicket win for the Mumbai Indians over Gujarat Giants. In an incredible display of bowling prowess, Kaur took the prized wicket of Jordan Duffield, and then went on to deliver 3 crucial wickets in quick succession. 

Kaur's impressive performance helped her Mumbai Indians secure a commanding lead, and a 7-wicket victory in just over a couple of overs. The win marked the third consecutive victory for the Mumbai Indians in the WPL, solidifying their position at the top of the league table. With Harmanpreet Kaur at the helm, it's clear that the Mumbai Indians are once again a force to be reckoned with in the Women's Premier League.
