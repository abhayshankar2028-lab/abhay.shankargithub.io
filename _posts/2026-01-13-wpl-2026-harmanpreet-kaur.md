WPL 2026: India's explosive Harmanpreet Kaur secures crucial seven-wicket win for Mumbai Indians.

In a thrilling encounter, Mumbai Indians secured an impressive seven-wicket victory over Gujarat Giants, thanks in large part to a phenomenal performance from Harmanpreet Kaur. The all-rounder dominated the pitch with an elegant 2/33 figures and 3/15, which proved instrumental in her team's triumph. Mumbai Indians' bowlers also performed well, with AnujaPatel taking 2/25 and Deepti Chand picking up 2/22 to restrict Gujarat Giants' scoring.

The win puts Mumbai Indians in pole position, with 3 points from 2 matches, while Gujarat Giants sit second with 0 points. Harmanpreet Kaur's impressive display once again showcased her exceptional all-round skills, cementing her status as one of the best players in the Women's Premier League.
