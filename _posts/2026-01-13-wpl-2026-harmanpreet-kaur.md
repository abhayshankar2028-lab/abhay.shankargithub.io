WPL 2026: Mumbai Indians Remain Unbeaten

The Women's Premier League (WPL) 2026 saw the power-packed Mumbai Indians secure a resounding seven-wicket win over their arch-rivals, the Gujarat Giants. This thrilling encounter was an electrifying display of batting and bowling from the Indian Women's Team.

Harmanpreet Kaur, a stalwart of the Mumbai squad, proved once again why she is one of the world's top bowlers. With three wickets in just three deliveries, she took over the game and led her team to an impressive victory. The Gujarat Giants managed a modest 142/6 in their chase, while Mumbai Indians amassed 130/3. This victory maintains Mumbai's unbeaten record at WPL 2026, and Harmanpreet Kaur continues to be the force behind the team's success. The momentum is now on Mumbai's side, as they look to take the league title.
