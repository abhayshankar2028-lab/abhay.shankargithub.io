**WPL 2026: Mumbai Indians Gear Up to Take Down Gujarat Giants**

The Women's Premier League (WPL) is well underway, with the Mumbai Indians securing a resounding seven-wicket victory over the Gujarat Giants. Harmanpreet Kaur, the Indian captain, has once again proven her dominance in the 50-over format. In a thrilling contest, Mumbai Indians posted a formidable total of 181/5, thanks in large part to Kaur's explosive batting. She was instrumental in the team's victory, taking 2 crucial wickets to send the Giants' bowlers reeling. The win sees Mumbai Indians maintain their top spot in the league standings, while Gujarat Giants fall to 3rd place. Kaur's impressive display will undoubtedly lead to renewed interest in the WPL, making her a highly anticipated player for future tournaments. The Mumbai Indians will now look to continue their winning ways in the final leg of the WPL 2026.
