In a recent press conference, renowned Bollywood actor Emraan Hashmi addressed concerns regarding his upcoming film Awarapan 2, sparking rumors of a sequel to his 2015 film Ba***ds of Bollywood. Emraan Hashmi has been tight-lipped regarding the project's status, fueling speculation that he's cashing in on the nostalgia of the original.

In an interview, Emraan Hashmi explicitly denied any involvement with Awarapan 2, stating that the project is indeed in the works, but it has been seven years in the making. He reassured fans that the film is an authentic continuation of the original story, exploring new themes and characters. Emraan's dedication to the project reassures fans that Awarapan 2 is more than just a cash-in on the Ba***ds of Bollywood.
