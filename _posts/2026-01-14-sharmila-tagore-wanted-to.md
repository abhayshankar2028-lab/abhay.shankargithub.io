It's a fascinating tale of Bollywood legends and their infamous on-set antics. The story revolves around the iconic 'Sharmila Tagore wanting to wear a bikini,' a moment that left everyone in awe. According to Shammi Kapoor's son, she thought it would be a great idea to wear a bikini during a shoot for 'Shakti Samanta's 'An Evening in Paris.' 

But things took a remarkable turn when Shakti Samantha was not comfortable with Kapoor's suggestion. Instead, he suggested filming an entire scene on a helicopter. The combination of Sharmila's unapologetic brazenness and Shakti's innovative and unconventional approach to filmmaking left the crew in stitches. This offbeat approach to filmmaking has become a cherished memory in the world of Bollywood, cementing the legend of these two iconic actresses.
