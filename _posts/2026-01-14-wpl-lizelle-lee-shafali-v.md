In a thrilling World Premiere League (WPL) match, Delhi Capitals secured a last-ball win over the UP Warriorz. The game witnessed a nail-biting finish as Delhi's Lizelle Lee and Shafali Verma delivered the decisive blows. With the score tied at 2-2 in the 19th over, Pherazar Shukla struck a crucial six to put UP on the back foot. However, Delhi's strong batting duo then seized the opportunity to close out the match. With 19 balls remaining, Shafali Verma achieved a memorable half-century, followed by Lizelle Lee's aplenty. The big-hitting opener then contributed a crucial six, securing the win. Delhi's success marked their second win in the tournament. As the season draws to a close, UP Warriorz will look to bounce back and exact revenge against their opponents.
