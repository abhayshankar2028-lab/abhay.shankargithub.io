**Delhi Capitals Thrash UP Warriorz in WPL: Lizelle Lee and Shafali Verma Power the Win**

In a thrilling match, Delhi Capitals defeated the UP Warriorz by a last-ball run, securing their fifth win in the Women's Premier League (WPL). The powerful opener, Lizelle Lee, played a crucial role in Delhi's victory, scoring 52 not out off 51 balls. Shafali Verma also contributed with an impressive 53, and together, they formed a formidable batting partnership that kept UP Warriorz at bay. The Delhi Capitals' victory marks a significant milestone in their WPL campaign, and the team now looks to pull off a clean sweep of matches. With their energetic batting lineup and solid support staff, Delhi seems poised to make a deep run in the tournament.
