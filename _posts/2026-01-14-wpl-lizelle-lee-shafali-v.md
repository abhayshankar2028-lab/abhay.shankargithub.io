**WPL Highlights: Delhi Capitals Power Past UP Warriorz**

In an electrifying match at the DY Patil Stadium, Delhi Capitals ultimately emerged victorious, securing their third win of the Women's Premier League (WPL). Lizelle Lee was instrumental in Delhi's triumph, taking 2 wickets in 10 deliveries and claiming 5 crucial runs. Shafali Verma, meanwhile, hit an unbeatable 54 runs off 67 deliveries, showcasing her incredible batting skills. The star-studded Delhi squad proved to be too much for UP Warriorz, who struggled to build on a promising start. With a total of 133/5, Delhi capitalized on UP's 125/3, securing a last-ball victory. The win propels Delhi to the top of the WPL table, while UP Warriorz falls to third place. Lizelle Lee and Shafali Verma are now among the top run-scorers in the tournament, with 42 and 51 runs respectively.
