Mohammed Siraj has been appointed as the captain of Hyderabad cricket team in a surprise move. This choice is significant not only for the team but also for the city of Hyderabad, which has a long-standing passion for cricket. Siraj, an experienced cricketer, has been instrumental in Hyderabad's resurgence in the Ranji Trophy. As the captain, he will lead the team with a focus on developing the young talent and strengthening the squad.

The appointment is seen as a boost for the city's cricket scene, and fans are eagerly anticipating the captain's leadership. Siraj's experience in international cricket will undoubtedly bring a fresh perspective to the team. As the new captain, he will be expected to perform under pressure and lead his team to success. The appointment marks a significant milestone in Hyderabad's cricket journey, and fans are eagerly waiting to see the team perform under Siraj's leadership.
