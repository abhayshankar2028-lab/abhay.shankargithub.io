In an exclusive interview, renowned Indian cricketer Sunil Gavaskar shared his thoughts on the 2008-09 Indian Premier League match between India and New Zealand. During the match, India managed to chase down 285 runs in a low-scoring contest. Gavaskar expressed his surprise at how easily the New Zealanders were able to chase down the target.

The Indian batting lineup, comprising of MS Dhoni, Virender Sehwag, and Harbhajan Singh, performed admirably, with Dhoni scoring an impressive 111 runs off 112 deliveries. Sehwag also scored a valuable 64 runs off 50 deliveries. Meanwhile, Harbhajan Singh took 4 wickets at just 25 runs, showcasing his exceptional bowling skills.

Gavaskar praised the New Zealanders for their exceptional batting, saying they showed immense maturity and skill, which was too good for India to handle.
