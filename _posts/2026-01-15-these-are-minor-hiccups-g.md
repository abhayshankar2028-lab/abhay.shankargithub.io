![‘These are minor hiccups’: Gujarat Titans COO backs Shubman Gill to return ‘stronger’ after T20 World Cup snub](https://images.indianexpress.com/2025/12/Gill-66.jpg)

After the T20 World Cup snub, fans have been left wondering if Shubman Gill will return to the Gujarat Titans lineup. In an exclusive interview, the Titans' COO, Sachin Nagra, has been quite reassuring, stating, "These are minor hiccups." Nagra further emphasized that Gill's comeback is not a matter of if, but when. The Titans are indeed eager to welcome the young wicketkeeper back to the squad. With the upcoming Indian Premier League season, the team is looking to strengthen their lineup, and Nagra is confident that Gill will be included in the journey. "Gill's a crucial part of our team, and we're looking forward to his return," he added. This reassurance from Nagra might just give fans hope for their beloved player to make a comeback.
