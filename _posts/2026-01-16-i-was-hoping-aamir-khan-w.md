Mithila Palkar, the talented young actress, recently had the opportunity to attend an open casting session for Bhoot Bangla and Happy Patel's upcoming comedy project. She got a chance to showcase her comedic skills alongside her friends in this hilarious new venture. During the session, she shared her experience of participating in the audition with us.

"It was an amazing experience to be a part of this project with such talented artists," Mithila expressed in an exclusive interview. "I was hoping Aamir Khan would like my audition, but I'm glad he did! It was an incredible experience working with Happy and Bhoot Bangla. The energy was electric and we all had a blast."

The project promises to be a laugh-out-loud comedy, and it seems like Mithila's enthusiasm is contagious! We can't wait to see the final product and get ready for a hilarious ride.
