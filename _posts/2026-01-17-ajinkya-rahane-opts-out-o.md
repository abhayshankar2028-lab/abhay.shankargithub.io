**Ajinkya Rahane opts out of Mumbai's remaining Ranji Trophy matches**

In a shocking turn of events, Indian batter Ajinkya Rahane has chosen to withdraw from the remaining Ranji Trophy fixtures in Mumbai. This decision comes as a surprise, given that many top Indian players have already committed to participating in the tournament.

Rahane, who has been instrumental in India's recent cricket resurgence, has reportedly called for a change in the tour schedule. With the tournament now set to take place in the southern state, many of India's top players have opted out of participating. This move has raised concerns about the potential impact on India's chances in the tournament. The decision by Rahane has left fans and cricket enthusiasts wondering about the future of India's cricketers at the domestic level.
