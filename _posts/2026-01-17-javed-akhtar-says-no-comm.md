**Bollywood Buzz: A Glimpse into the World of Celebrities**

The latest news has ignited intense discussions about the working relationship between AR Rahman and Javed Akhtar. It has been reported that the renowned music composer has expressed his disappointment over not receiving a single project from Javed Akhtar in recent times.

In a recent conversation, Javed Akhtar has defended himself in saying, "I do not believe in the concept of communal elements in our industry." On the other hand, Indian playback singer Shaan shared his thoughts on the same topic, stating, "Three superstars also belong to minority communities." Shaan's statement seemed to hint at the potential backlash against AR Rahman, who has been a part of the music industry for decades. The comments have sparked a heated debate about the working dynamics in Bollywood and the growing divide between its stars.
