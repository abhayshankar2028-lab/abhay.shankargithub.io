In a shocking statement, Bollywood actress Kangana Ranaut expressed her strong disapproval towards renowned composer AR Rahman. The actress stated that the renowned singer had called her most 'hateful and prejudiced' and even refused to meet her for the 2017 film 'Emergency'. Kangana Ranaut took issue with Rahman's actions, specifically his response to her criticism regarding the film's portrayal of her. This marks another instance of a celebrity feud between the two. Kangana Ranaut described AR Rahman as 'hateful and prejudiced' and expressed her disappointment at his response to her criticism. The incident adds to the long-standing controversy surrounding Kangana Ranaut and AR Rahman. This latest development has sparked widespread debate and discussion in the Bollywood industry, highlighting the intense rivalry and animosity between these two powerhouses in the industry.
