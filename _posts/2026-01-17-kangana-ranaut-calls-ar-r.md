**Kangana Ranaut Speaks Out Against AR Rahman's 'Hateful and Prejudiced' Comments**

Renowned Indian actress Kangana Ranaut has responded to her former collaborator, renowned composer AR Rahman, who recently made some shocking comments about her. In an interview, AR Rahman called Kangana Ranaut "hateful and prejudiced" for her fierce response to his claims. Kangana Ranaut took a strong stance, stating that her film "Queen" was actually a "stunt" and that AR Rahman had refused to meet her for the film's title track, "Radha," as a result of his biased decision. Kangana Ranaut's bold statements have sparked an intense response from fans and critics alike. Kangana Ranaut has vowed to protect her artistic integrity and assert her right to express her opinions without fear of backlash.
