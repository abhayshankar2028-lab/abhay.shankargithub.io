In a candid interview, renowned composer AR Rahman has opened up about the emotional toll of receiving two Oscar nominations, sparking a bitter debate about the pressures of stardom. He revealed that despite numerous accolades, he still feels uneasy about his work, saying: "Two Oscars can be a burden." This sentiment highlights the subjective nature of artistic expression and the emotional weight that comes with delivering high-quality work.

The 14-time Indian National Film Award winner emphasized the importance of perseverance and the need to stay true to one's creative vision. AR Rahman's candid admission serves as a poignant reminder that even the most respected artists face internal struggles, and that success should never come at the cost of one's mental well-being.
