Title: A Glimpse into the Complex World of Creativity: AR Rahman Reflects on the 'Burden of Two Oscars'

In a candid conversation, renowned composer AR Rahman opened up about the 'trauma' he experienced after being nominated for two Oscars. In a poignant summary, the singer revealed that people often gaslighted him into doubting his own abilities, fueling a cycle of self-doubt that he ultimately transcended. Rahman shared how being awarded two Oscars can be a "burden" that can erode one's confidence, especially when faced with the scrutiny of the industry and the expectations of fans. The mastermind behind songs like "Dil Se" and "Jai Ho" expressed gratitude for the opportunity to share his work but acknowledged that the pressures of being a composer in the film industry can be overwhelming.
