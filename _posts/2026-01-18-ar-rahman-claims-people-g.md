**The Unspoken Reality of Creative Overwhelming: AR Rahman's Statement**

In a recent statement, renowned music composer AR Rahman has revealed that people gaslighted him into doubting his work. The Oscar-winning maestro expressed his frustration, stating: "Two Oscars can be a burden." Rahman's words echo the harsh realities faced by many creatives, who often wear multiple hats and struggle to maintain their mental health. As AR Rahman navigates the film industry, he is no exception. With immense pressure to produce quality work, the emotional toll can be daunting. His candid statement sheds light on the issue of creative overwhelm and the emotional toll it can take on artists. It serves as a reminder that behind every successful film, there are countless hours of hard work and dedication.
