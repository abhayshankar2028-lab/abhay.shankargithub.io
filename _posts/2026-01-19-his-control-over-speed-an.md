In a recent match, Indian bowler R Ashwin expressed his admiration for New Zealand's young spinner, Jayden Lennox. During their match, Ashwin praised Lennox's exceptional speed and control over the length, remarking that he was a game-changer in their favor. Ashwin specifically highlighted Lennox's ability to pick up wickets around the middle stump, often catching batsmen off guard with his deceptive pace.

The 23-year-old Lennox has been impressive in the subcontinent, taking crucial catches and snaring wickets in big matches. Ashwin's praise is a testament to Lennox's potential and skill, and his inclusion in the New Zealand squad is expected to make a significant impact in their upcoming home series against India.

Lennox's display has left many pundits and fans in awe, showcasing his potential as a future star in the cricket world.
