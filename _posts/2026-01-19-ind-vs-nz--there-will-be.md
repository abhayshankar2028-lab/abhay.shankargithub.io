The ongoing India-New Zealand ODI series has come to a close, with India ultimately falling short of their target against New Zealand. In the post-match press conference, Indian captain Ajinkya Rahane expressed his disappointment over the series loss. Rahane urged his team to "follow the process" moving forward, stating that it's essential to learn from their mistakes and put them into practice. "There will be tough questions, but that's how we grow as a team," Rahane emphasized. He also praised his teammates for their effort, saying that the Indian squad showed a lot of heart and spirit. Rahane's words show that he is committed to helping his team improve and bounce back from their series loss. With this newfound determination, India is set to return stronger than ever.
