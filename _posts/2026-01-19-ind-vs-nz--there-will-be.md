In a post-match press conference, India's ODI prospectus, Ajinkya Rahane, expressed his concerns about the series loss to New Zealand. The 30-year-old Indian batsman highlighted the importance of discipline and sticking to the process, cautioning against impulsive decisions. Rahane emphasized that accountability is crucial in cricket, and players must adapt to the situations presented by the game. "There will be tough questions," Rahane stated. "We will have to face them, but we will also learn from our mistakes. It's essential that we follow the process, and that involves taking calculated risks." Rahane's insightful comments have left a significant mark on the Indian ODI team, highlighting the need for patience, discipline, and a clear understanding of the game.
