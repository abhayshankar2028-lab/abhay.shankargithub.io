The highly anticipated Women's Premier League (WPL) 2026 playoff has come to a close, with Gujarat Giants (GG) claiming the top spot. RCB secured their playoff spot on the back of an impressive performance from Gautami Naik, who dominated the batting department with her exceptional batting skills. Naik batted to an impressive score of 61, showcasing her incredible composure and technique. 

On the other hand, Sayali Satghare, the ace bowler for GG, proved her mettle with a stellar display, picking up 9 wickets in the tournament. Her impressive display sealed the win for GG and secured their playoff spot. GG will now look to face RCB in the final, which will be an electrifying encounter to crown the WPL 2026 champion.
