**Gautami Naik Shines, Sayali Satghare Dominates in WPL 2026: RCB Secure Playoff Spot**

In a thrilling conclusion to the Women's Premier League 2026, Gautami Naik and Sayali Satghare stole the show, showcasing their exceptional skills to lead Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) to a crucial playoff spot. Naik, the star batswoman, played a match-winning 74 runs off just 59 balls, while Satghare took 4/24 in an impressive display of bowling prowess. The 24-year-old Indian pacer proved to be the game-changer, ultimately helping RCB navigate the cut-throat competition. With 6 wins and 3 losses, RCB secured a spot in the playoffs, finishing 2nd in the standings. Gautami Naik's phenomenal display has earned her a spot in the playoff hunt, while Sayali Satghare's outstanding performance solidified her position as one of the top wicket-takers in the tournament.
