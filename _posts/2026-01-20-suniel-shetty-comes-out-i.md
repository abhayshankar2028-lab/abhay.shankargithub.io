**Shah Rukh's Former Partner and Bollywood Star Suniel Shetty Takes a Stand Against Online Trolling**

In a heartening development for fans of Varun Dhawan, Bollywood actor Suniel Shetty has come out in support of the actor amid online trolling. The actor, known for his sharp wit and sharp tongue, weighed in on the situation, saying, 'You see, it is very easy to run someone down.' His words are a testament to the importance of kindness and compassion in the digital age. Shetty's words come at a time when online trolls seem to be escalating their attacks on celebrities. This is a welcome development, and we hope that Shetty's support will help to create a safer online environment for everyone. Fans of Varun Dhawan are likely to be thrilled by Shetty's words and hope that they will find solace in knowing that someone is standing up for them.
