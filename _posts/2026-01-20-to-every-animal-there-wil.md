**A Royal Defense: Karan Johar Speaks Out**

In a recent interview with a prominent Indian publication, Karan Johar stood by his bold decision to create high-budget, mass cinema films, citing the importance of artistic expression in the entertainment industry. Johar argued that the financial investment in film productions is not merely a profit motive, but a means to create work that inspires and touches the hearts of audiences worldwide. The filmmaker emphasized the value of taking risks and pushing creative boundaries in order to produce films that not only bring people joy but also foster a sense of community and belonging. Johar's unwavering commitment to artistic vision has earned him the respect and admiration of fans and fellow filmmakers alike.
