WPL 2026: Jemimah Rodrigues-led Delhi Capitals secure a crucial win over Mumbai Indians. In a thrilling encounter, Jemimah Rodrigues made a monumental 51-ball 51, showcasing her impressive batting skills to help DC secure a 16-run victory. With the score clocked at 109/4 at the end of 20 overs, Jemimah accounted for the wickets of two MI bowlers in the final over, sealing the win for the Capitals. This impressive display earned her the Player of the Match award. A perfect start to the tournament, DC's victory sets the tone for a thrilling competition. With Jemimah's remarkable form, her side is now in a strong position to make a deep run in the league.
