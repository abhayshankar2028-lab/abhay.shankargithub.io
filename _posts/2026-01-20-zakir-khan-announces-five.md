Zakir Khan, a renowned comedian, has announced that he will be taking a five-year break from comedy to focus on his health. The news comes as a surprise to fans who have grown accustomed to his high-energy performances. According to sources, Zakir Khan's health has been a concern for some time, and he has decided to dedicate this period to his well-being. His fans are eagerly awaiting his return, speculating about the type of comedy he will bring back to the stage. When asked about his plans for the hiatus, Zakir Khan replied, "I'll probably be back by 2030." Fans are eagerly awaiting the return of the dynamic comedian to share his story and possibly even sneak peeks of his comeback.
