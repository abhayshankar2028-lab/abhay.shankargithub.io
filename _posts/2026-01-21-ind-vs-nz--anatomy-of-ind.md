**India's Stunning Win against New Zealand: A Masterclass in Depth and Flexibility**

In a thrilling match at the Eden Gardens, India secured a massive 6-0 victory against New Zealand. The victory marked a landmark moment in the series, as the Indians proved their versatility and depth in batting. Rohit Sharma's undefeated century, coupled with Rishabh Pant's scintillating display, showcased India's ability to rotate the strike. Meanwhile, a guileful bowling attack, led by Jasprit Bumrah and Ishant Sharma, restricted New Zealand's scoring, while Ravichandran Ashwin and Deepak Chahar chipped in with crucial contributions.

The match was a testament to India's ability to adapt and innovate, as they outplayed the Kiwis on multiple occasions. This win has catapulted India to the top of the series, and their performance will be remembered as one of the greatest in the history of international cricket.
