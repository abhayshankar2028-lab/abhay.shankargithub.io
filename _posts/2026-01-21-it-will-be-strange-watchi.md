**It will be strange watching it from home**: Rohit Sharma opens up on missing T20 World Cup for the first time in his career.

In an exclusive interview, Rohit Sharma expressed his disappointment and frustration at not being able to witness the 2022 T20 World Cup from home. The 2022 ICC Men's T20 World Cup was held in Australia, and Rohit's team, India, ultimately failed to win the tournament, losing to Australia in the final.

Rohit shared his memories of the tournament and how it felt to be considered one of the favorites to win. His candid reaction showcases the emotional toll the tournament took on his team. "It will be strange watching it from home," Rohit said. His words demonstrate the immense pride and disappointment he feels about not being able to witness the tournament from his own home.
