In a heartwarming story of dedication and perseverance, Shekhar Kapur has revealed his profound connection with Sushant Singh Rajput. Kapur, the acclaimed filmmaker, was indeed the driving force behind Rajput's debut film, "Kesari". During the production phase, Rajput showcased his unwavering commitment to the project, sacrificing numerous opportunities to secure his role. Kapur admits that his refusal to compromise on the film's quality made Rajput "fall in love" with him. The rest, as they say, is history. "He refused many films for this", Kapur shared in an interview. This selfless dedication is a testament to the transformative power of passion and commitment. Rajput's journey to fame, all thanks to his unwavering dedication to "Kesari".
