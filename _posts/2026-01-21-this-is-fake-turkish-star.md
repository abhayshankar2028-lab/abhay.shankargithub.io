**Turkish Star Hande Ercel Breaks Silence: 'This is Fake'**

In an explosive reaction to the viral post calling Shah Rukh Khan "uncle" at the Joy Awards 2026, Turkish supermodel and actress Hande Ercel has finally spoken out. The shocking statement came after fans and critics alike took to social media to express their outrage over the controversy. Ercel's publicist has since issued a statement denying the claims, calling the post "fake news." The statement claims that Ercel was not present at the event and that the post was fabricated. With her reputation intact, Ercel has set the record straight, showcasing her sharp wit and swift response. It's clear that this incident won't be taking down the 'uncle' title anytime soon.
