The wait is over, and the nominations for the 2026 Oscars are out. The excitement is palpable, but what caught our attention was Neeraj Ghaywan's snub for the Best Director award. I'm honored to have worked with you." Karan's words of appreciation and admiration for Neeraj's hard work are truly touching. It's clear that Neeraj's commitment to his craft and the film industry has not gone unnoticed. We eagerly await the winners and the reactions from the film community.
