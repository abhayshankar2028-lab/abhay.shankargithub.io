**Border 2 Box Office Collection Day 1: Sunny Deol-Diljit Dosanjh's War Epic Likely to Break Records**

In a highly anticipated opening weekend, Border 2, the highly-anticipated war film starring Sunny Deol and Diljit Dosanjh, has broken all the records. The film, directed by Prabhakar Kulkarni, has opened to a massive show in the Indian box office. Early reports indicate that the film has collected over Rs 25 crore on its opening day, making it the highest opening weekend for a Bollywood film in recent times.

The film's massive opening has already generated immense buzz and excitement among Bollywood fans. With its powerful storytelling, stunning visuals, and talented performances, Border 2 is expected to perform exceptionally well at the box office. It's a testament to the film's massive potential and audience appetite for a cinematic experience.
