![Irrfan Khan recommended Akshay Kumar for Airlift, told director ‘you won’t get the money with me’, he recalls](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/01/Akshay-Irrfan.jpg)

Irrfan Khan, the renowned Indian actor, has been remembered for his exceptional performance in the 2019 film Airlift. In an interview, Khan revealed that he had initially rejected the role of Inder Malhotra, only to be recommended by Akshay Kumar. Khan recalled, "Irrfan told me, 'Akshay, you won't get the money with me,' but I took that as a sign that he wanted me to play the role." Khan's words show how much he trusted Akshay Kumar's recommendation, and how grateful he was for the opportunity to portray the complex character of Inder Malhotra in the film. Khan's endorsement of Akshay Kumar's talent played a significant role in the film's success, and Airlift remains a testament to the power of professional relationships in Bollywood.
