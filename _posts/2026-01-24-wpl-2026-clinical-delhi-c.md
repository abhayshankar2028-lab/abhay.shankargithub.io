**Delhi Capitals Win, Challengers Fall Short in WPL 2026**

The Women's Premier League (WPL) 2026 got off to a thrilling start as the Delhi Capitals claimed a 7-wicket victory over the Royal Challengers Bangalore. The match, played at the FENIX Stadium in Bengaluru, saw the Capitals take an early lead, with Meg Lanning and Harmanpreet Kaur scoring crucial knocks. The Challengers, however, fought back with a valiant effort, but ultimately fell short. The win propels the Capitals to the top of the table, while the Challengers slip to third. The Capitals' victory was a testament to their solid team performance, with their bowlers and batters working in perfect harmony. This win will certainly boost their confidence ahead of the next matches.
