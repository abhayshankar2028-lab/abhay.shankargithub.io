In a stunning display of praise, renowned music composer AR Rahman recently expressed his admiration for singer Arijit Singh. During an interview, AR Rahman called Arijit Singh 'reckless' and stated that he is proud of him. The 'Haashere Mein' crooner took to Twitter to react to the praise, saying, "He works from his village". Arijit Singh's fans were overjoyed by these words, hailing them as a testament to his dedication and perseverance. AR Rahman's endorsement is a significant one, as he is known for pushing artists to explore their creative potential. Arijit Singh's recent release, 'Kar Gayi Chull', has been a massive hit, and this praise from AR Rahman is sure to further boost his career. It's a proud moment for Arijit Singh and a testament to the power of music to inspire and uplift.
