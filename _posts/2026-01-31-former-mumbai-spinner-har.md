Harmeet Singh, the legendary Mumbai spinner, has made a stunning comeback to the international stage at the T20 World Cup. The 32-year-old Indian pacer put on a remarkable display for the national team, taking 4 wickets in his last 3 games to help India secure a crucial victory. This impressive performance has generated immense excitement among cricket fans in the United States, where Singh has been living for the past few years.

In a bold move, Singh has opted to wear the USA jersey, a nod to his US-born parents. His decision has been met with widespread admiration, with many praising his courage and dedication to his country. With his impressive skills and determination, Singh is poised to inspire a new generation of cricketers in the USA and beyond.
