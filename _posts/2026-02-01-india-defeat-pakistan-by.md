![India defeat Pakistan by 58 runs in Under-19 World Cup Super Six match, book semifinal clash vs Afghanistan](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/02/IND-U19-7-1_20260201151931.jpg)

India made it to the semifinals of the Under-19 World Cup Super Six after defeating Pakistan in a thrilling match. The winning margin for India was an impressive 58 runs. Rohit Sharma's century was the highlight of the game as he scored 104 runs off 103 balls, while Ravi Bishnoi took 3/11 to restrict Pakistan to 411/8 in 46.1 overs.

Pakistan's Shahnawaz Hoque was the player of the match, scoring 100 runs, but it was too little, too late for the hosts. The win propelled India to the semifinals, where they will face Afghanistan in the next match. This victory has strengthened India's chances of winning the tournament. With this win, India is right on the cusp of making it to the final. The team will look to build on this momentum in their next match against Afghanistan.
