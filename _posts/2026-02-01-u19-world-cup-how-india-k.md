**India Stuns Pakistan in U-19 World Cup Semifinals**

In a thrilling conclusion to the U-19 World Cup, India edged out Pakistan in a nail-biting match to reach the semifinals. Despite not showing their A-game, the Indian squad pulled off a stunning upset, thanks in large part to their energetic and determined performance. The Pakistani team, known for their skillful fielding, struggled to contain the Indian bowlers, who took five wickets in a blistering 30-over display. Vicky Shetty's 32 off 35 balls was the highest score of the innings, while Hardik Shetty and Suryakumar Yadav added crucial support. India's victory marked a memorable exit for Pakistan, who will now face a tough challenge in the semifinals. This astonishing performance is a testament to the growing talent and determination of India's young team.
