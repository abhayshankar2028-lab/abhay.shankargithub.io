![WPL 2026: Delhi Capitals cruise into fourth straight final with seven-wicket win over Gujarat Giants](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/02/dc-team.jpg)

In a thrilling match at the Narendra Modi Stadium, the Delhi Capitals secured their fourth consecutive Women's Premier League (WPL) title with a 7-wicket victory over their arch-rivals, the Gujarat Giants. Batting captain, Smriti Mandhana, led the charge with an unbeaten 67, while Meghanka Bisht took 3/20 to demonstrate her crucial role in the Capitals' success. The Gujarat Giants, led by Ellyse Perry, struggled to post a total, with a team effort that ultimately proved insufficient. The Delhi Capitals' victory marked their seventh WPL title, cementing their position as the dominant team in the league. With this win, the Capitals will look to defend their title against the reigning champions in the final. The energy in the stadium was electric as the Capitals celebrated their WPL triumph, marking an unforgettable conclusion to an incredible tournament.
