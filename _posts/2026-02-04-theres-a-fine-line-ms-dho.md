MS Dhoni, the legendary captain of the Indian cricket team, has been away from commentary for quite some time. In a recent interview, he opened up about why he has stayed away from the commentary box. Dhoni revealed that he has been focusing on his family and personal life, citing the need to be present for his children. He also mentioned that he has been dealing with back issues, which have made it challenging for him to perform on the field. However, he has stated that he is excited to return to commentary after a short break. Dhoni expressed his passion for the game and his love for watching it played out on the field. He is expected to make a comeback to commentary soon, and fans are eagerly waiting to hear his voice once again.
