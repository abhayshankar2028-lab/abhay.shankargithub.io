**India's U-19 World Cup Triumph: Virat Kohli and Gautam Gambhir Pay Tribute**

In a thrilling finale, India's U-19 team clinched their sixth World Cup title, defeating Pakistan by 8 wickets in the final at the Feroz Shah Kotla Stadium in New Delhi. On the occasion, Virat Kohli and Gautam Gambhir paid heartfelt tributes to their teammates and coaches, expressing their gratitude for the incredible journey.

Kohli, the captain, praised his teammates for their "determination and resilience", while Gambhir lauded the team's "hard work and dedication". Both players were in attendance at the coronation ceremony, where they were awarded the U-19 World Cup trophy and plaques. The triumph marks a remarkable achievement for India's youth cricket team, cementing their status as one of the best teams in the world.
