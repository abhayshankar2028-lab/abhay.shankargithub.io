![BCCI announces Rs 7.5 crore reward for India’s U-19 World Cup winners](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/02/india-u19-world-cup-icc.jpg)

The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) has announced a substantial reward for the winners of the India U-19 World Cup. The prize amount has been set at a whopping Rs 7.5 crore, a significant boost for the young cricketers who will be competing in the tournament. The award is part of the BCCI's efforts to nurture and promote the development of Indian cricket at the U-19 level. This significant financial backing will enable the winning team to cover their travel and accommodation expenses, ensuring they can focus on their cricketing duties without any financial burdens. The BCCI's decision is a testament to its commitment to nurturing young talent and promoting the growth of cricket in India. The team that wins the tournament will receive this substantial reward, which will be a tremendous motivator for them to perform their best.
