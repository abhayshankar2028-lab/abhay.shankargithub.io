Mohammed Siraj has been named in India's T20 World Cup squad, a momentous call-up that has left the fast bowler in awe. In a statement, Siraj expressed his heartbreak at missing out on the tournament, citing an unforeseen family obligation. However, he couldn't help but express his excitement and thrill at being a part of the Indian squad. "This feels like a dream", Siraj exclaimed. His journey to the T20 World Cup may have been cut short, but it's clear that Siraj is thrilled to be a part of India's cricketing team. The fast bowler's eagerness is palpable, and fans are eagerly anticipating his debut. With his family's absence and the pressure mounting, Siraj will need to rise to the challenge and prove himself in the T20 World Cup.
