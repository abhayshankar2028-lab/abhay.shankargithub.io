title: "Naseeruddin Shah says ‘only failures come back to teach’ at NSD: ‘I consider it my responsibility to help these kids’"

![Naseeruddin Shah says ‘only failures come back to teach’ at NSD: ‘I consider it my responsibility to help these kids’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2025/06/Naseeruddin-Shah.jpg)

Naseeruddin Shah, a renowned Indian actor, recently spoke at the National School of Drama (NSD) regarding the importance of failures in the learning process. In his insightful lecture, he emphasized that it is only through experiencing failures that one can truly learn and grow. The actor shared his own experiences of struggling with his craft, highlighting the valuable lessons he learned from his failures. "I consider it my responsibility to help these kids," he stated, emphasizing the need for mentorship and guidance. Shah's words are a testament to his commitment to nurturing the next generation of artists. His message serves as a reminder that failures are an inevitable part of the learning journey, and it is through embracing them that we can truly flourish.
