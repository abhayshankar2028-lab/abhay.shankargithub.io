![Naseeruddin Shah says ‘only failures come back to teach’ at NSD: ‘I consider it my responsibility to help these kids’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2025/06/Naseeruddin-Shah.jpg)

Naseeruddin Shah, a renowned Indian actor, recently spoke at the National School of Drama (NSD) in New Delhi. In an inspiring address, he shared his thoughts on the art of acting. Shah emphasized that "only failures come back to teach" and encouraged the young actors to learn from their mistakes. He emphasized the importance of perseverance and resilience in the face of rejection. Shah also expressed his commitment to mentoring and guiding these talented youngsters. "I consider it my responsibility to help these kids," he said. With his vast experience in the industry, Shah is not only a great actor, but also a dedicated educator. His words of wisdom are sure to motivate and inspire the aspiring actors at NSD.
