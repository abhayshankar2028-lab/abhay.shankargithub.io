Tanmay Srivastava was a key player on the 2008 U19 World Cup-winning team for India. In an exclusive interview, he spoke about the unbreakable bond that formed among the team members. "We used to see everything as 'We'... There was no 'I or me' culture. We were a family, a unit, and that's what made us unstoppable," he shared. The 16-year-old star batsman highlighted the importance of togetherness and unity in achieving great success. On that fateful day in November 2008, Tanmay's incredible performance helped India clinch their first U19 World Cup title. He remembered the team's unwavering support and camaraderie, which played a significant role in their triumph.
