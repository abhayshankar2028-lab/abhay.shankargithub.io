In an inspiring interview, Virat Kohli revealed that he always dreamed of hitting the top bowlers in the game. The legendary Indian cricketer emphasized that there's no fun in hitting mediocre bowlers. Instead, he wants to face the best of the best. This statement serves as a valuable lesson for Vaibhav Sooryavanshi, the under-19 World Cup winner. Vaibhav, a talented young bowler, has been working hard to improve his skills. Kohli's words of wisdom will undoubtedly motivate Vaibhav to strive for excellence in his craft. As Vaibhav looks to take his bowling to the next level, he can draw inspiration from Kohli's dedication and passion for the game. With Virat's guidance, Vaibhav is sure to become a force to be reckoned with in the world of cricket.
