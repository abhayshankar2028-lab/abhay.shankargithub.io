In a thrilling T20 World Cup match, India emerged victorious against Namibia by 93 runs in New Delhi. Ishan Kishan, Hardik Pandya, and Varun Chakaravarthy played pivotal roles in their team's dominance. Ishan Kishan was the highest scorer for India, scoring 37 runs while also taking 4 crucial wickets. Hardik Pandya, a force to be reckoned with, scored 25 runs off just 28 balls, showcasing his batting prowess. Varun Chakaravarthy took 2 wickets for 18 runs, demonstrating his yorker delivery skills. The Indian bowlers' combination was key to their victory, as they restricted Namibia to a meager total. With this win, India moved to the top of the T20 World Cup standings, while Namibia registered their second consecutive loss. The teams will now face each other in their next match.
