"Arijit Singh's Back to His Kingdom: The Singer Returns with His First Independent Song After Playback Retirement"

Arijit Singh, the beloved singer of Bollywood, is back with a bang! After announcing his playback retirement earlier this year, the singer has dropped his first independent song, "Tera Yaad" from the upcoming film "Dilwale". The song, which is a love ballad, is a nostalgic and emotional treat that showcases Singh's vocal range and emotional delivery.

The music video, directed by Rajan Khosla, is a visual feast with Singh performing the song in stunning locations across India. "Tera Yaad" marks a significant comeback for Singh, who was an integral part of the 2019 film "Dilwale". With this song, Singh is ready to take on the music industry once again, and fans are eagerly anticipating his next move. The song is set to release on March 22nd.
