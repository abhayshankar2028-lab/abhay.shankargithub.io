![Mohammed Siraj rates Arshdeep Singh’s ‘Siuuu’ celebration: ‘Zyada jump le liya…’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/02/arshdeep-siraj.jpg)

In a recent match, Cricket Blogger had the chance to watch Arshdeep Singh celebrate a wicket. The young leg-spinner's celebrations often leave fans in awe, but this time, he took it to a whole new level. As he was celebrating a particularly crucial dismissals, he let out a massive "Siuuu" roar, which echoed throughout the stadium.

Mohammed Siraj, the fielding teammate, was seen reacting with a huge grin, shouting "Zyada jump le liya..." - a phrase that roughly translates to "That's a lot of jumping!" - in Amharic, to express his amusement and admiration for Arshdeep's hilarious celebration. It's clear that Siraj has high praise for Singh's unorthodox yet entertaining way of celebrating a big catch. The exchange has left fans and pundits alike in stitches, and it's a sight that will be remembered for a long time.
