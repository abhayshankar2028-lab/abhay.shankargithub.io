Michael Atherton, a renowned former English cricketer, recently expressed his disappointment with the recent series between India and Pakistan. In an interview, Atherton stated that he would much rather cover fixtures featuring teams like Nepal and Italy. "It has become a miserable spectacle recently," he lamented. Atherton felt that the intense rivalries between India and Pakistan overshadowed the quality of play. He also felt that the lack of exciting moments made the matches dull. Atherton acknowledged that India and Pakistan have a rich cricketing history, but felt that the current rivalry was dominating the narrative. He suggested that watching Nepal and Italy would be a refreshing change, allowing him to focus on lesser-known teams and watch more exciting matches. Atherton's comments highlight the growing frustration among cricket fans with the prolonged rivalry between India and Pakistan.
