**India Stuns Netherlands with Unbeaten Run**: India has secured a historic 2-0 win over Netherlands in the Group Stage of the T20 World Cup, shattering their own records. The chase for the top spot was intense, but India's dominant performance secured their spot at the top of the points table. The star-studded Indian team, led by MS Dhoni, showcased their batting skills, with Varun Chakaravarthy and Shivam Dube contributing crucial factors to the victory. Chakaravarthy's 2-for-16 spell and Dube's blistering 35 off 21 balls sealed the deal. This win has set India up for a thrilling final against the Netherlands. The Indian team's unbeaten run has raised their hopes, and they are now confident of making a deep run in the tournament.
