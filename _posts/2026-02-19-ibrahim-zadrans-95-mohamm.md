In a thrilling T20 World Cup finale, Afghanistan edged out Canada to secure a spot in the knockout stages. The match was a back-and-forth affair, with both teams trading wickets and runs. However, it was Afghanistan's Ibrahim Zadran who stole the show with an incredible 95 off 62 balls, his career-best score. His partnership with Nabi, who took 4/7 in the final over, helped Afghanistan stay in the game. The victory marked a fitting end to a campaign that saw them win 3 out of 4 matches. Zadran's heroics were a highlight of the tournament, and he will be remembered for his incredible display in the final. With a strong squad and a spirited performance, Afghanistan is one step closer to a T20 World Cup triumph.
