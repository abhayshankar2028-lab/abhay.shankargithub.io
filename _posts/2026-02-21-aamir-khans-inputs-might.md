Aamir Khan is often misunderstood due to his sometimes strict demeanor, but despite his tough exterior, Kalpana Iyer reveals that the actor is "very sincere" in his interactions. When questioned about his reputation for being overly critical, Iyer shared a heartwarming anecdote. "People often say he's very fussy," she said with a chuckle. However, Iyer quickly added, "That's just a misconception. When we talk to him, he's not as strict as everyone says. He's very sincere and genuinely cares about the things that matter." Iyer's comments offer a glimpse into Aamir's kind and empathetic personality, dispelling the notion that he's only driven by a need for perfection. It's clear that Kalpana Iyer's own experiences with Aamir have given her a unique perspective on his character.
