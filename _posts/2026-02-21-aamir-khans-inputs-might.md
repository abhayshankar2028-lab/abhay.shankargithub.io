In a candid conversation, Kalpana Iyer, a renowned Indian actress, shared her thoughts on Aamir Khan's recent criticism. "Aamir Khan's inputs might seem unfair or harsh, but he is very sincere," she said. When asked if she believed his criticism was justified, Kalpana Iyer replied, "Not at all." According to Iyer, Aamir Khan has always been very particular about his work, and it's natural for people to feel that way. However, she also expressed her admiration for his diligence and commitment to his craft. "When people say he is very fussy...," Iyer said with a chuckle. "He's just being himself," she concluded. This quote highlights Iyer's appreciation for Aamir Khan's unique approach to filmmaking and his dedication to his art.
