In the thrilling finale of the 2026 Women's Asia Cup Rising Stars tournament, India A emerged victorious by a commanding 46 runs, securing their first-ever title. The match was held at the DY Patil Stadium in Mumbai, where Bangladesh A provided a valiant effort, but ultimately fell short. India A's captain, Smriti Mandhana, led her side to a convincing win, with Mithali Raj and Harmanpreet Kaur providing crucial contributions with the bat. Bangladesh A's Shreya Ghoshal and Nishka Didi, who were outstanding with the bat, also played a crucial role in their team's efforts. The victory marked a historic milestone for India A, who clinched their first-ever Women's Asia Cup Rising Stars title. This triumph solidifies India's position as one of the top teams in the region, and sets the stage for their future endeavors in the tournament.
