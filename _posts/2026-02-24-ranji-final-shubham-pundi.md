In a thrilling Ranji final, J&K secured their first Indian Premier League (IPL) title by defeating Karnataka in a nail-biting match. Shubham Pundir, the star batter from J&K, played a magnificent innings of 115 not out, while Yawar Hassan chipped in with an impressive 88. The visitors batted first and took Karnataka to 284/2 at stumps, courtesy of Pundir's innings. Hassan, who made his IPL debut, continued his form with 88, showcasing his skill and experience. Karnataka's bowlers struggled to contain J&K's batsmen, who put up an impressive display to seal the victory. This win marks a historic moment for J&K, their first IPL title, and a testament to the team's diligence and teamwork. Pundir's innings will be remembered as one of the most outstanding performances in IPL history.
