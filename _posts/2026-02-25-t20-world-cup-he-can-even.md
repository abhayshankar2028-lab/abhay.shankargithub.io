In an exclusive interview, former Indian batsman Aakash Chopra expressed his awe at Shivam Dube's bowling skills during the T20 World Cup. Chopra acknowledged that Dube has the potential to take the game by storm with his unorthodox formula. He specifically praised Dube's ability to concede four sixes in an over against West Indies. Chopra highlighted Dube's unique blend of pace, swing, and seam movement, which makes him a nightmare for batsmen. "He can even concede four sixes in an over against West Indies...": is a testament to Dube's exceptional talent. Chopra believes that Dube's ability to read the game and adjust his pace and seam angle to keep batsmen guessing is a key factor in his success. With India's T20 team looking strong, Chopra is confident that Dube will play a crucial role in their quest for glory.
