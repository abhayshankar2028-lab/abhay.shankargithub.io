Priyanka Chopra Opens Up on Feeling 'Cornered' in Bollywood

In a candid interview, the actress shared her early days in Bollywood, where she felt like she was "drowning" in the industry. "I didn't see the longevity that I wanted," Priyanka revealed. She recalled feeling "cornered" by the expectations placed upon her. The 44-year-old actress confessed that she struggled to balance her personal and professional life amidst the pressures of fame. Priyanka also spoke about the lack of creative control and the constant scrutiny she faced. Despite her immense success, she expressed her desire for a more sustainable and fulfilling career in the industry. Her words serve as a reminder that success can be fleeting, and it's essential to prioritize one's well-being and happiness.
