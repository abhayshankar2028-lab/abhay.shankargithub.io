"I've kept on doubting myself": Sanju Samson reflects on India's T20 World Cup semifinal win

After a remarkable performance, where he captained India to a thrilling semifinal victory over West Indies, Sanju Samson has turned heads with his courageous batting. His 97 off 50 balls was a statement piece, showcasing his ability to adapt and thrive in adversity. "I've always been a bit of a doubt maker, but today, I felt like I was in control," he said in an exclusive interview. Samson's display was a testament to his hard work, dedication, and mental toughness. His teammates looked up to him, and he played the role of a leader, inspiring them to chase the impossible score. The 32-year-old's performance will be etched in the memories of fans and pundits alike, as he solidified his position as one of the world's top batsmen.
