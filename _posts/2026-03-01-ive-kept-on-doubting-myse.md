![‘I’ve kept on doubting myself’: Sanju Samson reflects after his 97 off 50 balls leads India into the T20 World Cup semifinal](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/Sanju-Samson-SKY.jpg)

In an emotional post, Sanju Samson opened up about his recent performance, which led India to a thrilling semifinal spot in the T20 World Cup. The 32-year-old batter shared his thoughts on the game, stating, "I've kept on doubting myself." He also attributed his improved game to the support of his teammates, saying, "I've learned to trust myself and my teammates." Samson's 97 not out off 50 balls was a turning point in the match, as it guided India to a decisive victory. He reflected on the journey, saying, "It was a tough opponent, but I managed my nerves and played my natural game." With this win, India is set to face Australia in the semifinal, and Samson is confident, "We're all excited to take on them."
