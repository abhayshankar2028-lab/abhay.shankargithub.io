In a shocking statement, Pakistani cricketer Mohammad Hafeez has expressed his discontent with the performance of the national team, calling it "incomprehensible". The veteran batsman, widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in Pakistan's history, was speaking to a think tank in Dubai. Hafeez's comments were laced with disdain for the team's inability to deliver on the field, stating that the way they were playing was "incomprehensible" to any cricket critic. He specifically pointed out the team's lack of ability to capitalize on the significant advantages they receive on home soil, highlighting their weaknesses in batting and bowling departments. Hafeez's remarks have sparked a heated debate in Pakistan, with many calling for a change in the team's strategy and tactics. The comments have left fans and pundits alike wondering what the future holds for Pakistan cricket.
