Sanju Samson has finally emerged as India's go-to batsman, showcasing his mettle in a crucial match against the West Indies. As India entered the semifinals, Samson's successful chase against the opposition proved to be the turning point in their series. With the West Indies struggling to post a decent score, India's innings was built upon by the young batsman, who scored 110 runs off 95 deliveries. His partnership with Rohit Sharma was instrumental in securing the match victory, as India secured a comfortable win with 8 wickets in hand. Samson's match-winning innings has cemented his position as one of the premier batsmen in the game, and his performances have been instrumental in India's success in the series. His ability to play patiently and hit crucial shots has made him a nightmare for opposing bowlers to face.
