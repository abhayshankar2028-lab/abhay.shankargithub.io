In a recent interview, Gautam Gambhir, the former Indian cricketer, spoke about his thoughts on the most crucial wicket-based performances in cricket history. When asked about Shivam Dube's impressive performances with the bat, Gambhir gave an honest assessment. "For me, Shivam Dube's two boundaries are as important as Sanju Samson's 97," Gambhir stated. He highlighted Dube's ability to score crucial boundaries to turn the game in his team's favor. Gambhir also praised Dube's work rate and consistency, which has been instrumental in India's success in the past few years. Gambhir's comments emphasize the importance of boundaries in cricket, particularly in competitive matches. His assessment is a testament to Dube's value to the team, making him a crucial player for India going forward.
