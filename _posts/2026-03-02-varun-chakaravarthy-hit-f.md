In a jaw-dropping display, Indian leg-spinner Varun Chakaravarthy became the first bowler to hit nine sixes in a single T20I match at the 2023 ICC T20 World Cup. The record-breaking effort left fans and pundits alike in awe, but what might be of greater concern to Chakaravarthy's mentor, Ravi Shastri, is the impact this performance could have on England's semifinal bid. The English team, currently holding the top spot in the standings, have been relying heavily on their batting lineup to chase down Chakaravarthy's exploits. With the pressure mounting, Ravi Shastri will be eager to assess the team's overall strategy and see how Chakaravarthy's record-breaking display will be integrated into their plans.
