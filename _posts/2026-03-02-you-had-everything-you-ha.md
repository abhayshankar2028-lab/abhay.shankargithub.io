![‘You had everything; you had timing, you had placement…’: Ravi Shastri showers praise on Sanju Samson](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/samson-express-photo-by-partha-paul-2.jpg)

In an exclusive interview, former Indian cricket captain Ravi Shastri has hailed Sanju Samson as one of the most underrated cricketers in the world. The veteran coach emphasized that Samson's batting technique and mental toughness are the key factors that set him apart from others. "You had everything; you had timing, you had placement," Shastri said. "Sanju is a true artist at the crease, with a sixth sense for running between wickets. His ability to read the game and make the right decisions is unparalleled." Shastri also praised Samson's work rate, saying he is a "complete package" who can bat, field, and even express himself on the field. Shastri's praise for Samson is a testament to his understanding of the cricketer's potential, and fans are eagerly awaiting his potential in the Indian team.
