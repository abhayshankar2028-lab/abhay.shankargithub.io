![Zaheer Khan conducts specialised red-ball camp for fast bowlers at BCCI’s Centre of Excellence in Bengaluru](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/Zaheer-Khan.jpg)

Zaheer Khan, a renowned former Indian fast bowler, recently conducted a specialised red-ball camp for talented young fast bowlers at the BCCI's Centre of Excellence in Bengaluru. This unique initiative aimed to enhance the skills of these skilled bowlers, focusing on their unique challenges in playing on red-bowled pitches. Under Zaheer's guidance, the camp saw a significant improvement in the bowlers' ability to handle the slower and more carrying ball. The expert session covered topics such as yorkers, swing, and seam movement, helping the participants to refine their techniques and become more effective on red-bowled pitches. The camp was an excellent opportunity for the young bowlers to learn from Zaheer's extensive experience and gain valuable insights into the world of red-ball cricket. The results are expected to be impressive.
