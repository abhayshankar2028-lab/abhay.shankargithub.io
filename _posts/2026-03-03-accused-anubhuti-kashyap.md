**The Dilemma of Accused: A Bollywood Enigma**

In the latest controversy to shroud the film industry, Anubhuti Kashyap, a renowned Bollywood director, has been accused of creating a film about harassment without fully understanding the subject matter. Kashyap, known for her thought-provoking films like "Shruti" and "Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani", has taken the film industry by storm with her unique storytelling style. However, in this case, it seems she is more interested in exploring the complexities of harassment rather than delving deep into the topic.

The real question here is, what is harassment, anyway? Is it the way a man pushes a woman's hair back, causing her discomfort? Or is it something more sinister, like stalking or coercion? Kashyap's film, "Accused", raises more questions than answers, leaving the audience to ponder the intricacies of this often-misunderstood concept. Will she ever find the courage to fully grasp the subject matter, or will she simply create a thought-provoking film that sparks important conversations? Only time will tell.
