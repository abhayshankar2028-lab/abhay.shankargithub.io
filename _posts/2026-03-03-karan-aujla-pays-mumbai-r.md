![Karan Aujla pays Mumbai rickshaw drivers’ fine of Rs 22,000, calls himself ‘city’s favourite’: ‘I love doing this’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/karan-1.jpg)

In a heartwarming display of kindness, Bollywood personality Karan Aujla recently paid a fine of Rs 22,000 to Mumbai's rickshaw drivers, showcasing his philanthropic side. The move is a testament to Aujla's love for the city and its people. He called himself 'the city's favourite', a title he feels honored to hold. Aujla's gesture is a throwback to the traditional Mumbai rickshaw culture, where rickshaw drivers are an integral part of the city's daily commute. By paying the fine, Aujla is giving back to the community that has supported him throughout his career. His selfless act is a reminder that even in the entertainment industry, there are kind and generous souls who make a real difference in the lives of others.
