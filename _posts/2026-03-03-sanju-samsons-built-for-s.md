In a thrilling match, Indian opener Sanju Samson proved his mettle once again, delivering a game-winning knock to seal the deal. With the Batting Order of the match looking bleak, Samson stepped up to the plate and went on a phenomenal innings. He faced 94 deliveries, hit 5 fours and 2 sixes, showcasing his incredible hand-eye coordination and consistency. His partnership with Hanumana lasted for over 90 balls, and his unbeaten 39-ball 45 was a masterclass display of batting. Michael Clarke, the former Australia captain, has lauded Samson for his "built for stand and deliver" qualities. "Sanju has a special quality, a great temperament and an incredible ability to stay at the crease," Clarke said. This display has cemented Samson's spot in the Indian team, and fans are eagerly awaiting his next knock.
