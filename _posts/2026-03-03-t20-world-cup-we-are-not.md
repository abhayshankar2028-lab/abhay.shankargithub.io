![T20 World Cup: We are not trying to do anything different, says South Africa captain Aiden Markram ahead of semifinal against New Zealand](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/Aiden-Markram-SA-vs-NZ.jpg)

South Africa captain Aiden Markram has quashed speculation that the Proteas are trying to exploit a perceived advantage in the T20 World Cup. "We're not trying to do anything different," Markram said in a pre-semifinal press conference. "Our game is built around being aggressive, being physical, and being willing to take risks. We're not trying to change our style of play to gain an edge." Markram's comments came as South Africa takes on New Zealand in the semifinals. While New Zealand is considered one of the top contenders, Markram and his team are focused on their own performance. "We're not getting carried away with the rhetoric," he added. The match is set to be an intense and closely contested affair, with both teams determined to reach the final.
