![‘Don’t believe we need a perfect game to win T20 World Cup’: Harry Brook speaks about England’s chances ahead of India semi-final](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/HARRY-BROOK.jpg)

In an exclusive interview with Cricket Wire, England's star opener Harry Brook expressed his team's confidence ahead of the India semi-final. When asked about their chances, Brook replied, "We don't need a perfect game to win the T20 World Cup. We have a good balance of skills and experience. Our bowlers have shown they can deliver when it matters most."

Brook also highlighted the importance of their star all-rounder, Moeen Ali, stating, "Moeen is a game-changer. He can take the game away from India with his incredible batting and fielding. We believe he will be a game-dealer on the day."

With a 2-0 lead over India in the group stage, England is in a strong position to advance to the semi-finals. Brook's words of encouragement and reassurance have given the team a renewed sense of optimism as they prepare to face their arch-rivals.
