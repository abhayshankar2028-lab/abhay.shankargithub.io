Finn Allen's stunning 33-ball 100 in the T20 World Cup final against Australia left the Kiwi cricket team ecstatic. The young opener, considered one of the best young players in the world, took the game by storm to help New Zealand secure the top spot in the standings. As he celebrated the milestone, Allen's teammates were over the moon with joy, hugging each other in celebration. In an interview, Allen expressed his relief and gratitude, saying, "Hopefully they can get up and have Monday off." He couldn't stop smiling as he reflected on the incredible feeling of scoring a 100 in such a high-pressure situation. Allen's emotional response was a testament to the team's collective effort and the bond that forms during such intense competitions.
