![‘Hopefully they can get up and have Monday off’: Finn Allen reacts after taking New Zealand into T20 World Cup final with 33-ball 100](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/FINN-ALLEN.jpg)

In a thrilling conclusion to the T20 World Cup Group Stage, New Zealand managed to edge out Pakistan by a single run, securing a spot in the final. Finn Allen was the star of the show, scoring an incredible 33-ball 100 off 57 deliveries to power the Blackcaps to victory. "Hopefully they can get up and have Monday off," Allen quipped in the post-match press conference. "We've worked so hard for this moment, and it's going to take a lot of TLC to celebrate. But we're happy, we're proud, and we're going to enjoy this one tonight." With this historic triumph, New Zealand has solidified its position as the number one team in the world rankings. The stage is now set for a high-stakes final against Australia, and Allen is already looking forward to the celebrations.
