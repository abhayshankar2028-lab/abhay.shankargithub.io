Priyanka Chopra recently opened up about her pregnancy journey, sharing that she had a "really tough time" with the whole process before giving birth to her daughter. The actress revealed that she had to put everything on hold, not just her career, but also her personal life. However, when her daughter was born, everything "stopped". Priyanka stated that the moment her daughter was born, all the challenges and struggles she faced during pregnancy just melted away. The actress expressed her gratitude for the relief and joy that came with having a beautiful child. She emphasized that her daughter's birth marked a new chapter in her life, one that she will cherish forever. Priyanka's openness about her pregnancy struggles has sparked a positive conversation about the challenges faced by new mothers everywhere.
