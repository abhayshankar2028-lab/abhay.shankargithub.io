![‘Sanju Samson is the most loved cricketer’: India opener’s fan following similar to Dhoni, Kohli and Rohit, says Dinesh Karthik](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/sanju-samson-fans.jpg)

In a recent interview, Indian wicket-keeper batsman Sanju Samson has been hailed as the most loved cricketer in the country. Dinesh Karthik, a former Indian captain, expressed his admiration for Samson, stating that his fan following is similar to that of MS Dhoni, Virat Kohli, and Rohit Sharma. Karthik praised Samson's exceptional fielding skills, batting form, and leadership qualities, which have earned him the respect of fans across the country. "Samson's all-round game has made him a fan favorite in India," Karthik said. "His ability to bat and bowl with equal ease has made him a crucial member of the Indian team. He has the potential to be a top-class player for years to come," Karthik added. Samson's impressive performances in international and domestic cricket have solidified his position as the most loved cricketer in India.
