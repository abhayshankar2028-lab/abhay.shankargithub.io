![‘Sanju Samson is the most loved cricketer’: India opener’s fan following similar to Dhoni, Kohli and Rohit, says Dinesh Karthik](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/sanju-samson-fans.jpg)

In an exclusive interview, Indian television commentator Dinesh Karthik has expressed his admiration for Sanju Samson, India's stalwart opening batsman. Karthik, a former wicketkeeper, noted that Samson's fan following is similar to that of the legendary MS Dhoni, Virat Kohli, and Rohit Sharma. "Sanju Samson is the most loved cricketer in India," Karthik said. "His determination, work ethic, and consistency make him a fan favourite. Just like Dhoni, Kohli, and Rohit, Samson has a special place in the hearts of Indians. His dedication to the team is unmatched, and his success on the field is a testament to his hard work and passion. Samson's fans love him for who he is, both on and off the field," Karthik added. This endorsement has solidified Samson's status as one of the most beloved cricketers in the country.
