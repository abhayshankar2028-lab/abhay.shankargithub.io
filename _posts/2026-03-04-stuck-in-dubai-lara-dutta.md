"I've been stuck in Dubai for hours, and it's the perfect storm of frustration and exhaustion. As I'm stuck here, I couldn't help but think about the experiences of others who are trapped in this beautiful city. I'm currently talking to Lara Dutta, who is visibly emotional as she talks about the struggles of being stuck in UAE. She says, 'It's like being in India, but instead of freedom, we have the option to leave.' Her words got me thinking - isn't that what we all want? A safe haven where we can breathe, live, and thrive. It's not just about the physical walls; it's about the people, the culture, and the way they protect their citizens. Dubai may have its advantages, but the UAE's commitment to its people is something that I think we can all learn from."
