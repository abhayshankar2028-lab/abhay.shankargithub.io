![‘Every over he bowls is an event’: Ravi Shastri on Jasprit Bumrah ahead of India vs England semi-final](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/Jasprit-Bumrah-Roston-Chase_20260305102957.jpg)

Ravi Shastri, India's former head coach, has raved about Jasprit Bumrah, stating that every over he bowls is an event. "Bumrah is a phenomenal bowler," Shastri said. "He has a slight variation in his action, which makes it difficult for batsmen to score quickly. His deliveries are well-paced, and he has a lot of shades of variation in his spin. Every over he bowls, it's an event. The way he handles the ball, the way he picks up the wicket, it's just fantastic to watch." Shastri's praise for Bumrah is no surprise, considering the latter's incredible performances for India in the recent years. With the semi-final against England on the horizon, Shastri's sentiments are likely to carry weight as he guides his team towards a comeback.
