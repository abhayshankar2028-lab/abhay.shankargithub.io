The 2022 ICC T20 World Cup has come to a close, and the memories of the thrilling tournament will be etched in the minds of cricket fans for years to come. Among the plethora of talented players who stole the show, one name stood out - Sanju Samson. This young Rajasthan Royals batsman truly emerged as the leader of the pack, showcasing his exceptional skill and mental toughness. With a record-breaking 18 fours and 6 sixes in the tournament, Samson's impressive display in the final against Australia left fans in awe. His ability to adapt and perform under pressure also highlighted his exceptional qualities. As one of the biggest stars of the tournament, Samson's legacy will continue to inspire future generations of cricketers. His performance will undoubtedly be remembered as one of the greatest in T20 World Cup history.
