![‘It’s Arijit Singh’s universe’: Iulia Vantur recalls visiting the singer’s hometown, says was touched by the community atmosphere there](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/Arijit.jpg)

It's true, Iulia Vantur has shared her experience of visiting Arijit Singh's hometown in Meerut where he was born. The singer often talks about his roots and how they have influenced his music. Iulia shared her thoughts on the community atmosphere where Arijit grew up. "I was in Meerut and I visited his hometown. It's indeed a special place where music runs deep. The community here is very warm and welcoming. The air is filled with the spirit of music and love," she said. It was a heartwarming experience for Iulia to see the place where her friend Arijit grew up. The singer often talks about his love for Meerut and the people who live there. It's a place where music and love are deeply rooted, and Iulia has captured that essence beautifully in her memories.
