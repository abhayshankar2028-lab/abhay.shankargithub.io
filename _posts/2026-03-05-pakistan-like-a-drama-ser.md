![Pakistan ‘like a drama series’: Usman Khawaja in disbelief over news of PCB fining players after T20 World Cup exit](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/usman-khawaja-pak.jpg)

Pakistan's cricket team has left fans stunned after the Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB) announced that players who fail a fitness test will face fines of up to 80,000 rupees. The news has been likened to a drama series, with players expressing disbelief over the harsh punishment. Usman Khan, one of the players involved, took to social media to express his shock, stating that the fine was outrageous. "How can they fine us over something that happened in the middle of the match?" Khan asked. "We're already struggling with our fitness, let's not take it out on ourselves." The PCB has defended its decision, claiming that the fines are aimed at ensuring the players' health and well-being. However, fans are divided on the matter, with some calling for leniency and others backing the PCB's decision.
