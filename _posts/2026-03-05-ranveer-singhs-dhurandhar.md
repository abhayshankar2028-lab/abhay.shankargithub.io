Ranveer Singh's much-anticipated film Dhurandhar 2 has opened to a spectacular advance in the US, leaving fans and critics alike in awe. The movie, which marks Ranveer's return to Bollywood after a brief hiatus, has broken all records. According to reports, Dhurandhar 2 has secured a bumper advance of over $5 million, courtesy of its strong word-of-mouth and positive reviews. This impressive opening is a testament to the film's enduring popularity and its ability to generate buzz among American audiences. Meanwhile, Ranbir Kapoor's Animal has also seen huge pre-sales, with reports suggesting that it could gross over $20 million in the US. While both films are expected to perform well, it's clear that Ranveer Singh's Dhurandhar 2 has set a new standard for Bollywood releases in the US.
