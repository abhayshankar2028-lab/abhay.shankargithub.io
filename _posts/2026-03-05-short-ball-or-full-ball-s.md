In a breathtaking display of dominance, Sanju Samson continued his remarkable form in the T20 arena, once again showcasing his ability to turn the tables with the bat. Playing for Rajasthan Royals, Samson faced a short ball from the bowler, but instead of being rattled, he pounced onto the ball, using his incredible footwork to tame the pace. The result was nothing short of spectacular, as Samson took a single off the first ball and then played a watchful innings, showing an uncanny understanding of the game. His ability to adapt to different bowling styles is a hallmark of his game, and today was no exception. With 27 runs added to his score, Samson proved once again that when on song, he is unstoppable in T20 cricket.
