![‘A lot tighter than it looks’: Ravi Shastri praises India after seven-run semi-final win](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/samson-dube.jpg)

In a thrilling semi-final match, India secured a seven-run win over Sri Lanka. Ravi Shastri, India's head coach, has been raving about the team's performance. "A lot tighter than it looks," Shastri said. He was praising the team's fight and determination, which ultimately led to their victory. Shastri also highlighted the role of young players like Ishan Kishan and Shubham Swami, who contributed significantly to the win. He also praised the bowlers, especially Jasprit Bumrah, who took crucial wickets. "Jasprit was outstanding," Shastri said. "He bowled tight and took key wickets. Our bowlers did a great job, and it was a team effort that led to our win." Shastri's praise is a testament to the team's resilience and ability to overcome adversity.
