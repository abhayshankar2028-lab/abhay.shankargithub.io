In an electrifying display, Sanju Samson has left the cricket world speechless as he smashed Rohit Sharma's World Cup sixes count, setting a new benchmark for Indian cricket. The 28-year-old wicketkeeper batsman surpassed Sharma's record of 14 sixes in the Indian Premier League and equally surpassed Virat Kohli's record of 24 sixes in the T20 World Cup. This monumental achievement not only shattered Sharma's records but also equaled Kohli's record, cementing India's place in the spotlight. Samson's heroic knock in the T20 World Cup semifinal, where he scored 62 off 51 balls, was a testament to his exceptional skill and mental toughness. The match witnessed a thrilling contest, with both teams giving it their all, and Samson's remarkable performance will be remembered for ages.
