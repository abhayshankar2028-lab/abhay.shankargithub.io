However, the Real India star's efforts were also led by a stunning relay catch by Axar Patel, where he successfully caught a lob from Ravichandran Ashwin to deep square leg. 

The moment was deeply emotional for Axar Patel as he exclaimed, "Shivam udhar khada hain, lob kar deta hoon" ("Shivam is like a magician, he catches the ball with his mind"). The crowd erupted in cheers as Axar's incredible display of reflexes and acrobatic skills sealed the win for India.
