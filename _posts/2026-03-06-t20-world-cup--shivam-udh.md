In a thrilling match between India and England at the T20 World Cup, Axar Patels team-mates pulled off an incredible relay catch. With the score tied, Dube took the field with Rishabh Pant at the non-striker's end. As Pant attempted a pull shot, Dube's long leg fielder, Patels, leapt high into the air to snag the ball. Patels' acrobatic effort was met with a nod of approval from his teammates, who were ecstatic about their display. Patel's exceptional reflexes and dash down the pitch allowed him to leap high into the air, snatching the ball with his long leg fielder's glove. The team's coach, Ravi Shastri, was impressed by Patels' display, saying, "Shivam udhar khada hain, lob kar deta hoon" - meaning 'I'm so fast, I catch the ball with my feet'.
