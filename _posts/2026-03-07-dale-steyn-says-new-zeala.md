In a recent statement, South African veteran bowler Dale Steyn expressed his candid opinion on the upcoming T20 World Cup final between India and New Zealand. Steyn, known for his exceptional ability to pressure bowlers, suggested that New Zealand should be considered "chokers" if they fail to overcome India in the tournament's final. The South African all-rounder claimed that New Zealand should accept the label, as it would be a fitting acknowledgment of their inability to deliver. Steyn's comments came as a response to a question from an interviewer about his expectations from the tournament. Steyn's blunt assessment has sparked a lively debate among cricket enthusiasts, with many hailing him as a brilliant tactician. Steyn's statement serves as a stark reminder of the high stakes involved in international T20 cricket.
