Dhurandhar: The Revenge, the highly anticipated Bollywood thriller, is set to pay back its fans in stunning fashion. The revenge drama, directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali, is based on the 1966 film Dhund, and stars Ranveer Singh, Paresh Rawal, and Sanjay Dutt. The film, which is an adaptation of a popular Punjabi song of the same name, promises to deliver an electrifying experience with its gripping storyline, outstanding music, and impressive performances.

The payback preview shows are set to begin at 7 pm sharp on March 10th at various theaters across the city. Tickets can be booked online or at the box office. The preview shows will be followed by a press meet and event. Don't miss this opportunity to witness the thrilling tale of revenge and redemption unfold on the big screen. Get ready to experience the power of revenge like never before!
