**India's T20 World Cup Final Record: A Journey to the Top**

India's T20 World Cup final record is a testament to their dominance in the shortest format of the game. With an impressive 17 appearances, the team has consistently shown their mettle in international T20 tournaments. Two of those appearances have been in the World Cup, where they've successfully navigated the competition without losing.

India's greatest moment in T20 cricket came in the 2009 World Cup, where they reached the final against South Africa. Though they ultimately fell short, it marked a turning point in their T20 journey. Now, they're set to face off against New Zealand in the World Cup final, seeking to join the elite club of teams who have won the tournament twice. Will they be able to overcome the might of the Kiwis and claim their second title? Only time will tell.
