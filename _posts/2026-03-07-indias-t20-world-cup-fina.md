India's Cricket World Cup T20 journey has come to an exciting conclusion. The Men in Blue have reached their 12th World Cup final, with a record 2 titles to their name. The most successful T20 bowlers in the tournament represent India, with the likes of Jasprit Bumrah, Kuldeep Yadav, and Avesh Ahmed boasting impressive strike rates. India's path to the final was not without its challenges, but they ultimately emerged victorious in the 2021 edition. This year, they face their arch-rivals, New Zealand, in the final. The Kiwis boast an impressive record in the tournament, with a strong middle order and impressive bowlers like Lockie Hatley and Tim Southee. Can India replicate their 2021 success and add another title to their trophy case? The suspense is palpable as they take on the Kiwis in the final. Who will emerge victorious?
