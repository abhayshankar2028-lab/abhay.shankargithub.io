**India's Dominant T20 World Cup Record: A Legacy of Greatness**

India, the reigning champions of the ICC T20 World Cup, has etched its name in the history books with a remarkable record. With a total of 13 appearances, they have consistently demonstrated their ability to rise to the occasion and deliver on the biggest stage. India has claimed two titles, in 2007 and 2021, and has shown an uncanny knack for making deep runs and turning tournaments around.

The Indian team has been led by legendary captains like Sanath Jayasuriya and MS Dhoni, and has had the talent of players like Virat Kohli, Rohit Sharma, and Jasprit Bumrah to propel them to victory. As they prepare to take on New Zealand in the T20 World Cup, India stands poised to make history and cement their place among the world's best teams. The encounter promises to be a thrilling and unpredictable contest.
