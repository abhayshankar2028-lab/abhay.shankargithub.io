**Rani Mukerji's Exit from Amit Rai's Oh My Goddess Sparks Controversy**

The Bollywood film industry is abuzz with news that Rani Mukerji has exited her upcoming project with Akshay Kumar, director Amit Rai. According to a report, the creative differences between Mukerji and the director led to her sudden departure from the film. The movie, titled Oh My Goddess, was scheduled to release in the near future, but the exit of the lead actress has left fans and industry insiders surprised. Mukerji had reportedly been working closely with Rai to develop the script, but the director's vision seemed to clash with her own creative aspirations. The exit of the talented actress has raised questions about the dynamics of the project, and the fate of the film remains uncertain until now.
