In an exclusive interview, New Zealand's Mitchell Santner has expressed his desire to finally lift the T20 World Cup trophy. When asked about breaking records and winning championships, Santner stated, "Wouldn't mind breaking a few hearts to lift the trophy for once." He emphasized that it's time for his team to bring home the coveted title. Santner believes that the team's hard work and dedication have been the key to their recent success, and he's eager to see them claim their first T20 World Cup title. He also praised his teammates and coaches for their efforts, stating, "I'm proud of the way we've played and the way we've worked together." With the T20 World Cup final approaching, Santner's comments have generated excitement among fans, and it remains to be seen if his team will rise to the challenge.
