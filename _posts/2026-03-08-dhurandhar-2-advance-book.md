With the film's total collections expected to cross the Rs 50 crores mark, it is clear that 'Dhurandhar 2' is a must-watch for fans of the franchise. As the film's release date approaches, 'Dhurandhar 2' is poised to solidify its position as one of the highest-grossing Bollywood films of the year."
