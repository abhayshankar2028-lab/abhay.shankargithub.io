Gary Kirsten, a renowned South African cricket coach, has been appointed as the new head coach of the Sri Lankan men's cricket team. This appointment comes after Sri Lanka's decision to part ways with their previous coach, Ravi Shastri, in February 2023. Gary Kirsten, who has previously worked with South Africa and Kings XI Punjab in the Indian Premier League, was named as the interim head coach of the team in May 2023. His appointment is seen as a significant step forward for Sri Lanka cricket, which has been struggling with inconsistent performances in recent years. Kirsten's experience and expertise will be crucial in helping Sri Lanka regain their competitiveness in Test cricket. The appointment has been welcomed by Sri Lanka cricket, and fans are eagerly awaiting the team's prospects under Kirsten's guidance.
