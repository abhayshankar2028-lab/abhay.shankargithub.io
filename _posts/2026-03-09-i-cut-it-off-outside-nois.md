I cut it off... outside noise could be controlled': Sanju Samson reveals social media blackout during T20 World Cup triumph

In a candid interview, Rajasthan Royals' batsman Sanju Samson has opened up about his social media blackout during the recent T20 World Cup triumph. The 32-year-old cricketer revealed that he took a break from social media to avoid distractions from his teammates and the team's celebrations after their World Cup win. Samson stated that he wanted to focus on the game and the team's hard work, which paid off in the most dramatic way. The social media blackout allowed the team to celebrate together without any external distractions, creating a memorable moment for the players. The T20 World Cup victory marked Samson's first major triumph with the Royals, and his social media blackout served as a testament to his dedication to the team.
