![“I cut it off… outside noise could be controlled’: Sanju Samson reveals social media blackout during T20 World Cup triumph](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/samson-sanju-icc.jpg)

In an exclusive interview, Indian wicket-keeper batsman Sanju Samson opened up about his post-T20 World Cup social media blackout. During the triumph, the team was celebrating their historic win, but Samson chose to take a step back from social media to regain a sense of calm. "I cut it off... outside noise could be controlled": Samson shared, reflecting on the moment. He revealed that the collective energy of the team, coupled with the excitement of a World Cup win, made it difficult for him to manage his online presence. Samson expressed gratitude for the support of his teammates and the team's management, while avoiding specific details about the incident. This move has been seen as a sign of respect for the team's victory and a desire to maintain a sense of normalcy in the midst of jubilation.
