![‘India deserved the title’: Shahid Afridi, other ex-PAK players laud IND for defending T20 World Cup title](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/Afridi-India.jpg)

In a strongly worded statement, Pakistan's all-time leading runs scorer, Shahid Afridi, has expressed his admiration for the Indian team's courageous display in the 2023 T20 World Cup final. Afridi was a key figure in Pakistan's 2022 World Cup title win, scoring a crucial 50 in the final. However, the 2023 tournament proved to be a different story as India defeated Pakistan in the final. Several former PAK players, including Shahid Afridi, have lauded the Indian team's determination and resilience in defending their title.

"We deserved the title," Afridi told ESPN Cricinfo. "We fought hard all the way. We were not going to let the Red Lions win the tournament. We gave it our all and left everything on the pitch." Afridi's sentiments echoed those of other ex-PAK players, who praised the Indian team's grit and determination.
