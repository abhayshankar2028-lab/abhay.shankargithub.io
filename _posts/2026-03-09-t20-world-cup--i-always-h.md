In a recent interview, Indian batter Shivam Dube shared his thoughts on the upcoming T20 World Cup. When asked about his preparation, Dube expressed his confidence, saying, "I always had the confidence and just backed myself." His words of encouragement resonated with fans, who are eagerly awaiting the Indian squad's performance in the tournament. Dube also talked about his preparation, stating that he worked tirelessly to improve his skills. The batter highlighted the importance of mental preparation, saying, "You can't just go out there and expect to win. You have to prepare mentally as well." With a mix of experience and youth in the Indian squad, Dube is optimistic about the team's chances. Fans are eagerly anticipating Dube's performance in the tournament, and his words of encouragement are sure to boost their confidence.
