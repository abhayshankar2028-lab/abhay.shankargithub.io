"Ishan Kishan, India's talented young wicket-keeper batsman, has been thrust into the spotlight after scoring his first fifty in international cricket. But what's not often seen is the emotional depth of his reaction, as he shared his feelings about the tragic news that had shaken his family just days prior.

In a candid interview, Kishan revealed that he had recently received news about his grandmother's illness, which had left him heartbroken. The news had come just days before India's T20 World Cup final against Australia, and Kishan's family had to be away for the crucial match. His emotional response, as he looked up after scoring his fifty, was met with a mixture of sadness and determination, as he vowed to do everything in his power to conquer his grandmother's illness and bring home the championship."
