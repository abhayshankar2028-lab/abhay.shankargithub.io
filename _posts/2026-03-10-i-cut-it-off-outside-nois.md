I cut it off... outside noise could be controlled': Sanju Samson reveals social media blackout during T20 World Cup triumph.

In a post that has left fans abuzz, former England all-rounder Sanju Samson revealed that he took a social media blackout to his heart's content during the T20 World Cup triumph. The sudden absence of social media updates from the team captain sent shockwaves through the cricketing world.

"It was a difficult decision, but I made it for the sake of the team," Samson captioned a post where he had gone silent. "We had a great run with the ball, but also a great run without it. I took the decision to cut off social media, outside noise could be controlled': The post sparked a flurry of comments, with fans expressing their support and admiration for Samson's bold move.
