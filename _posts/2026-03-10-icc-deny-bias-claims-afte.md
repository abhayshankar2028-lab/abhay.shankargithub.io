**ICC Denies Bias Claims Amidst West Indies, South Africa Delays**

The International Cricket Council (ICC) has come under scrutiny after reports emerged that West Indies and South Africa's delayed flights from India following their exit from the T20 World Cup. The teams, who were eliminated from the tournament just days ago, were supposed to return to their respective countries but faced significant delays in their flights. The ICC has denied any bias or irregularities in the matter, stating that the delays were due to various factors such as bad weather and air traffic control issues. The ICC also highlighted that the teams had been given ample time to prepare for their return to their countries and that the delays were not in response to any "unfair" treatment by the organization. The ICC has assured that all necessary measures will be taken to ensure the safety and security of its members.
