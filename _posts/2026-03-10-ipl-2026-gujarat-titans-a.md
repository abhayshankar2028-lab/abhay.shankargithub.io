IPL 2026: Gujarat Titans appoint Matthew Hayden as batting coach

In a move to bolster their batting lineup, the Gujarat Titans have appointed legendary cricketer Matthew Hayden as their new batting coach. Hayden, a former Australian international player and captain, brings a wealth of experience and expertise to the role. As a seasoned batter himself, Hayden is well-versed in the intricacies of the game and is known for his exceptional batting skills. With a reputation for being one of the greatest batters in the history of the game, Hayden is expected to bring a new level of depth and strategy to the Titans' batting lineup. This appointment is a significant boost to the team, as they look to build on their success in the previous season. Fans are eagerly anticipating Hayden's arrival, and the Titans' fans are confident that he will help them become the next IPL champions.
