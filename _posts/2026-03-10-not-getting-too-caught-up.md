![‘Not getting too caught up in qualification mathematics’: Bangladesh coach Simmons shifts focus to standards over ODI World Cup qualification worry](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/01/bangladesh-phil-simmons-ap_20260104160341.jpg)

In a recent interview, Bangladesh coach Rahul Shrivath Simmons has emphasized the importance of focusing on the standards rather than just qualification mathematics in the upcoming ODI World Cup. While qualification is undoubtedly a concern, Simmons believes that the team's ultimate goal should be to set high standards and excel in every match.

By doing so, the team will not only ensure qualification but also gain valuable experience and exposure to different playing conditions. Simmons also emphasized the need for the players to step up and deliver in big games, rather than just focusing on individual performances.

"Qualification is a target, but the real question is, can we perform under pressure?" Simmons asked. "We need to focus on setting high standards and playing with intensity, rather than just worrying about getting into the top four. With a strong team and a clear strategy, I have no doubt that we can achieve our goal."
