![‘Outlier in world cricket’: Former England batsman heaps praise on Jasprit Bumrah for his bowling performances at the T20 World Cup](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/JASPRIT-BUMRAH-ap-2.jpg)

In an exclusive interview, former England batsman Michael Atherton has praised Indian pacer Jasprit Bumrah for his outstanding bowling performances at the 2022 T20 World Cup. Atherton, who played for England in the tournament, was in awe of Bumrah's exceptional skills, saying: "Jasprit Bumrah is an outlying performer in world cricket. His ability to swing the ball and york the batsmen is unmatched." Atherton also highlighted Bumrah's exceptional ability to read the game, stating: "He's got an uncanny ability to read the game and adjust his bowling accordingly." The 24-year-old pacer took 8 wickets in the tournament at an impressive average of 9.95, and Atherton's endorsement has further cemented Bumrah's status as one of the best bowlers in the world.
