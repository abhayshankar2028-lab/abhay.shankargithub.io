Randeep Hooda and his wife, Arwa Hooda, have welcomed a beautiful baby girl into their family. The couple shared the news on social media, and the excitement is palpable. Their newborn daughter has been named Aaradhya, which is a popular Sanskrit name symbolizing the pursuit of goodness.

In a heartwarming gesture, the family has decided to welcome Aaradhya on the same day as Randeep's father, Kuldip Kumar, who recently welcomed his first child. This thoughtful move is a testament to the family's love and commitment to their values. As the family celebrates the arrival of their precious daughter, they have become one more layer of love and warmth. Aaradhya's birth day is a special moment for the entire family, and we wish them all the best in their new beginning.
