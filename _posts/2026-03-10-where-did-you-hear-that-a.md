**"Where Did You Hear That?" - A Scathing Rebuke to Aamir Khan's Bollywood Bias**

In a recent controversy, Bollywood actor Aamir Khan took to social media to deny rumors that he had tampered with the title of Sunny Deol's 2017 drama film "Lahore 1947". The film, which tells the story of a young boy's quest to reunite with his family in post-partition Lahore, was released in several countries under different titles. However, the Hindi version of the film, which was released by Balaji Telefilms, was titled "Lahore 1947". Sunny Deol, who stars in the film, had expressed disappointment over the title change, citing that it did not accurately reflect the story's themes and plot.

Khan's denial of the controversy has sparked a heated debate, with many fans and critics questioning his impartiality in Bollywood films. It remains to be seen how this incident will affect Khan's reputation among Bollywood fans.
