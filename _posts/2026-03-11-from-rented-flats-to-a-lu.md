This luxurious abode sprawls across multiple floors, seamlessly blending modernity with classic charm. The family-friendly layout accommodates generations, from the grandparents who reside in a spacious, ground-floor wing to the parents and children in an elegant, third-floor residence. The pièce de résistance is the rooftop garden and the sprawling pool, perfect for relaxing after a long day. Ghazal and Varun's home is a masterclass in balancing form and function, exuding a sense of warmth and hospitality. A true gem in Indian real estate.
