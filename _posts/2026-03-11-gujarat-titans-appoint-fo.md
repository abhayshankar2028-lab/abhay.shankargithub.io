![Gujarat Titans appoint former India wicketkeeper Vijay Dahiya as assistant coach ahead of IPL 2026](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/vijay-dahiya.jpg)

IPL 2026 is just around the corner, marking a new chapter for the Gujarat Titans as they look to bolster their coaching staff. After a successful maiden season, the team has announced the appointment of former India wicketkeeper Vijay Dahiya as their assistant coach. Dahiya, a highly respected figure in Indian cricket, will work closely with the team's captain, Hardik Pandya, to help guide the side towards success.

As an experienced wicketkeeper, Dahiya brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the table. His expertise will undoubtedly be valuable in shaping the Titans' strategy and tactics ahead of the upcoming season. With the team's reputation growing rapidly, it's clear that Dahiya is the perfect candidate to help drive them to even greater heights. With a strong foundation in place, the Titans are poised for another exciting season ahead.
