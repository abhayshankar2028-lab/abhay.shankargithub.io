IPL 2026 Is on the Horizon: Gujarat Titans Appoint Dahiya

The Gujarat Titans have made a significant move ahead of the upcoming IPL season by appointing former India wicketkeeper Vijay Dahiya as their assistant coach. Dahiya, who had a stellar stint with the Indian cricket team, will bring a wealth of experience and expertise to the team. As an assistant coach, Dahiya will play a crucial role in shaping the team's strategy and tactics. His guidance will undoubtedly be instrumental in helping the Titans navigate the challenges of the IPL. Dahiya's appointment is a testament to the Titans' commitment to investing in the best talent in the country. With his impressive records and extensive experience, Dahiya is poised to make a significant impact on the team's performance. This appointment is set to be a focal point of interest for fans and pundits alike.
