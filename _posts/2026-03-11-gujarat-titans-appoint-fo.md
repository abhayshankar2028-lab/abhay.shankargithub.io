This move is a testament to the Titans' commitment to developing their cricketing talent. Dahiya, who has played for India in both Tests and ODIs, brings valuable experience and expertise to the table. As an assistant coach, he will support the head coach and contribute to the team's strategy and player development.

Dahiya's appointment comes at a time when the Gujarat Titans are gearing up for their maiden IPL season. With a strong squad and a talented squad of IPL players, the Titans are confident of making a strong comeback. Vijay Dahiya's expertise in the game will undoubtedly be a valuable asset to the team.
