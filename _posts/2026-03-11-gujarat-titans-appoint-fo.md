IPL 2026 is rapidly approaching, and Gujarat Titans have announced their coaching staff. Former India wicketkeeper Vijay Dahiya has been appointed as the assistant coach for the team. This move is a significant one for Dahiya, who brings extensive experience in the game. As an 8-time Ranji Trophy winner, Dahiya has a remarkable record in domestic cricket and has also played in international matches for India. His appointment is a testament to the Titans' commitment to developing the next generation of cricketers. With his expertise in batting and wicket-keeping, Dahiya will play a crucial role in shaping the team's strategy ahead of the IPL 2026. This move is expected to bring a new dimension to the Titans' batting lineup, with Dahiya's innovative approach likely to benefit the team's overall performance. The appointment is seen as a major coup for the Titans.
