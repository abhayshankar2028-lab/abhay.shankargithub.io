IPL News Update: Gujarat Titans Appoint Vijay Dahiya as Assistant Coach

The Gujarat Titans have made a significant move ahead of the upcoming Indian Premier League (IPL) 2026, appointing former India wicketkeeper Vijay Dahiya to the role of assistant coach. Dahiya, who played a crucial role in India's T20 World Cup campaign in 2022, will join the Titans' coaching staff to aid in their quest for the championship title. As an experienced wicketkeeper, Dahiya's expertise in identifying and managing opposition batsmen will be invaluable to the team. The appointment of Dahiya comes at a crucial time, as the Titans look to build a strong foundation for their IPL 2026 campaign. This move is expected to send a strong message to the competition and set the tone for a successful season.
