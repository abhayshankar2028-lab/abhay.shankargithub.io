"Bollywood Buzz: Shakti Mohan Spills the Tea on Padmaavat's 'Nainowale Ne' and Sanjay Leela Bhansali's Influence"

Shakti Mohan, the talented choreographer who brought magic to the 2018 hit film Padmaavat, has finally opened up about a pivotal moment in her career: refusing to be paid for choreographing the song "Nainowale Ne". In an exclusive interview, Mohan revealed that she was hesitant to accept compensation for her work, citing the high expectations placed upon her by the film's director, Sanjay Leela Bhansali. "People hold their breath around Sanjay," Mohan quipped, referencing the intense pressure to deliver a flawless performance. This candid admission has sent shockwaves through the Bollywood industry, with fans and colleagues alike taking notice of the renowned choreographer's bold move.
