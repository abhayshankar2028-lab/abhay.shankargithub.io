**Sorab Bedi's Rebuttal to Malaika Arora Rumours**

In a shocking turn of events, Bollywood's very own Sorab Bedi has come out in defence of his close friend Malaika Arora. The video of Sorab having a drink with Malaika at a party has fuelled dating rumours between the two. However, Sorab has shook off the notion, stating that he "didn't party with Malaika for the first time". When asked about the viral video, Sorab defended himself, saying "I didn’t party with her for the first time". In a bold move, Sorab also expressed his gratitude towards Malaika for being one of his closest friends. It seems like the drama has finally subsided, leaving fans eagerly waiting for Malaika's response.
