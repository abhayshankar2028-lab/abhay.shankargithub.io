"Has Jasprit Bumrah's 8/15 in the second innings of the T20 World Cup final truly been a 15-wicket haul, or was it just a masterclass performance by the Indian fast bowler? In the aftermath of the victory, Alastair Cook has been left questioning whether Bumrah's spell, where he conceded just 15 runs, can be considered a 15-wicket haul. While it's true that he took the game away from England with his swing and seam bowling, some may argue that his 0.80 economy rate and 33.33 economy rate per wicket make it difficult to label it a 15-wicket haul. Nevertheless, Bumrah's performance in this match will be remembered for a long time, and his ability to swing the game in his favor will undoubtedly be crucial in future Test cricket matches."
