"I Started My Cricket Here" is a heartwarming post from Indian pacer Jasprit Bumrah, who has won the T20 World Cup on his home turf. In the post, Bumrah reminisces about his journey to achieving this monumental feat, highlighting the significance of the moment he started playing cricket at his local ground. He expresses his immense gratitude to the people who supported him throughout his journey, from his parents to the cricket clubs and coaches who helped shape him into the player he is today. Bumrah also emphasizes the importance of his family and friends, who have been instrumental in his success. As he looks back on this incredible achievement, Bumrah cherishes the memories and moments shared at the venue where his journey began.
