Breaking News in the Cricket World: Lord's Goes Sour

In a shocking move, Lord's Cricket Ground, the iconic home of English cricket, has severed its long-standing alliance with the popular beer and brewer, BrewDog. The news comes as a surprise to fans, as BrewDog has been a long-time supporter of the London-based cricket club. However, it appears that their partnership has come to an end due to a change in ownership.

Tilray Brands, a Canadian cannabis company, has acquired the rights to the Medicinal Marijuana brand, Tilray. The company announced its intention to expand its presence in the British market, with a focus on the craft beer and brewing industry. This significant shift in focus raises questions about the future of Cricket and its ability to attract major brands in the industry.
