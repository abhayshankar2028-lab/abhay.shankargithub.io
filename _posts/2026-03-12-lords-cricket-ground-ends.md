**Lord's Cricket Ground Ends Ties with BrewDog**

Lord's Cricket Ground, one of the world's most iconic cricket stadiums, has parted ways with its long-standing beer partner, BrewDog. The UK-based brewery announced its intention to take over the sponsorship deal, a move seen as a strategic move to expand its brand reach. Meanwhile, Tilray Brands, the parent company of cannabis giant Tilray, has acquired the rights to supply beer from the renowned brewery. The partnership will see Tilray become the official beer supplier to Lord's, marking a significant shift in the stadium's sponsorship landscape. This deal is expected to attract a new audience and inject fresh energy into the cricket grounds. As the partnership comes to life, fans can expect a change in the cricketing experience, but one thing remains constant: the iconic Lord's Cricket Ground.
