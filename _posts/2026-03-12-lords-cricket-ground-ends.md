**BrewDog at the Brink as Tilray Brands Acquires Anheuser-Busch's US Brewery**

In a move that's sending shockwaves through the sports and beverages world, Lord's Cricket Ground, the iconic home of English cricket, announced that it has severed its alliance with BrewDog, a Scottish brewery and beer company. This development comes after BrewDog acquired US craft brewery Tilray Brands, which produces medicinal marijuana. The news has left fans and analysts stunned, as the brewery's involvement with Lord's Cricket Ground raises questions about the compatibility of their brands.

BrewDog's decision to end its partnership with Lord's Cricket Ground is likely due to concerns over the potential impact of medicinal marijuana on the brand's reputation. As Tilray Brands continues to expand its presence in the US market, its acquisition of Anheuser-Busch's US brewery is a significant coup for the company. The outcome of this move will be closely watched in the sports and beverages industries.
