In a remarkable display of humility, former Indian cricketer Rahul Dravid expressed his gratitude for the opportunity to celebrate his 100th Test century within the iconic Eden Gardens in 2001. Despite a reputation for being a perfectionist, Dravid acknowledged that his reputation preceded him and that he was determined to leave a positive impression. "Whatever may be my reputation, I have always been human," he said. Dravid's 200th Test century was a milestone moment for him, and he wanted to make the most of it. He expressed his gratitude to the Eden Gardens' staff, the Indian team, and his family, who were all present to witness this momentous occasion. Dravid's humility and sportingmanship were on full display as he basked in the adoration of the crowd.
