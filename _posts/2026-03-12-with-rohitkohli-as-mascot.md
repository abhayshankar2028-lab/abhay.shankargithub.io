![With Rohit-Kohli as mascots and 2027 ODI World Cup as next mission, India set to play more 50-over games than before](https://images.indianexpress.com/2025/12/kohli-rohit-2.jpg)

India is gearing up for a major overhaul of their cricketing format with the introduction of Rohit Sharma and Virat Kohli as their new mascots. This strategic move will undoubtedly catapult the Indian cricket team to new heights. As the country prepares for the 2027 ODI World Cup, fans are eagerly awaiting the prospect of more 50-over games. This format change will give India a significant edge in terms of match duration, allowing them to exploit the Indian summer better. The 50-over format, being a more condensed version of the full Test match, will also enable India to play more matches in a short span of time. With the likes of Rohit and Kohli leading the charge, India is poised to become the dominant force in one-day cricket once again.
