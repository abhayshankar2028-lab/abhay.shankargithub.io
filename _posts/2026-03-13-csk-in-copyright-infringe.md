Cricket fans, it's time to pay attention. The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are facing a copyright infringement lawsuit in the Indian Premier League (IPL). According to reports, the team has been using Rajinikanth dialogues and Anirudh tunes in their advertisements without the necessary permissions from Sun TV Networks, who own the rights to these assets.

The issue gained attention after the CSK's 2021 IPL campaign sparked a heated debate on social media. Many fans felt that the team's use of the dialogues and tunes was an infringement on Sun TV's intellectual property rights. The Chennai Super Kings have denied any wrongdoing, stating that they obtained the necessary permissions to use the content. However, the team's use of the dialogues and tunes has raised concerns about copyright infringement.
