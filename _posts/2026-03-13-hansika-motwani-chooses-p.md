Hansika Motwani has shared a heartwarming post on social media, choosing positivity after her divorce from Sohail Khan. In a dreamy, ethereal tone, she says, "Chardi Kala" - a Hindi phrase meaning 'joy to the heart'. This uplifting message reflects Hansika's newfound enthusiasm for life after a tumultuous divorce.

The actress, known for her strong personality, has been vocal about her struggles in the past, but now, she's choosing to focus on the positives. Her social media post is a testament to her resilience and determination to move forward. By embracing positivity, Hansika is sending a powerful message to her fans, encouraging them to do the same. As the saying goes, "Chardi Kala" - a beautiful reminder to spread joy and positivity in our lives.
