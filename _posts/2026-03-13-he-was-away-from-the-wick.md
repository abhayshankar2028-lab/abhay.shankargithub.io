In a chaotic scene at the Lahore Cricket Stadium, Bangladesh skipper Mehidy Hasan Miraz expressed his frustration after the team's star player, Salman Ali Agha, was run out by a brilliant throw from the wicketkeeper, Mohammad Rizwan.

Miraz took to the microphones to share his thoughts, saying, "He was away from the wicket and I was just looking for the ball. It's a shame that the game is over before it even starts." The incident sparked heated discussions among fans and pundits alike, with many questioning the decision-making of the umpires. Miraz's candid assessment offered a glimpse into the emotions of the players and the fans, highlighting the intense pressure and excitement that comes with professional cricket.
