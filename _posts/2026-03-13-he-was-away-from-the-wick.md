![‘He was away from the wicket and I was just looking for the ball’: Bangladesh skipper Mehidy Hasan Miraz on Salman Ali Agha run out row](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/PAK-BAN-AGHA-DISMISSAL.jpg)

In a lively match between Bangladesh and England, skipper Mehidy Hasan Miraz gained fame for his spectacular run-out effort that captured the attention of fans worldwide. During the crucial sixteenth over, Mehidy spotted an English batter nearing the crease, but was too late to react as an English bowler, Salman Ali Agha, attempted to run out the batsman.

The scene unfolded with Mehidy being trapped inside the crease as Agha swung his bat and sent a direct hit straight to the stump, leaving the Bangladeshi skipper stunned. "He was away from the wicket and I was just looking for the ball", Mehidy later explained, "In that split second, I lost track of the batsman, and I was left with the ball. It was a top-notch effort from Salman Ali Agha, and I'm glad he was able to complete the run-out."
