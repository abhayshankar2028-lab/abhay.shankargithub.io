In an exclusive interview, former Pakistani cricketer Rashid Latif shared his insights on the upcoming ICC Cricket World Cup, where India and Pakistan are set to face off in the finals. When asked about India's mindset, Latif stated, "India's DNA is to play finals and win, Pakistan's is to lose before knockouts." He emphasized that India's team is built around producing high-quality players, while Pakistan's team seems to lack the same depth. Latif believes that India's team has the talent and expertise to take home the trophy, stating, "They have a strong batting lineup, and with the right combination of bowlers, they can win the game." Pakistan, on the other hand, seems to be relying on spinners to get them past knockouts, but Latif warned that if they can't adapt to Indian spin, they might struggle to progress.
