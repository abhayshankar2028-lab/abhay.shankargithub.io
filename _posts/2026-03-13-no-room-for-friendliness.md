**Ramiz Raja's Scathing Criticism of Salman Ali Agha's Run Out**

In a scathing commentary, renowned Bangladesh cricketer Ramiz Raja has slammed Salman Ali Agha's run out in the ICC World Cup. Raja, who has been a vocal critic of the cricketing world, took to the airwaves to express his disappointment with Agha's decision. "No room for friendliness in this contest," Raja stated. "Agha's decision is a classic example of every player using the run out as a way out, rather than a clean dismissal. It's a cheap shot and undermines the integrity of the game." Raja's comments have sparked a heated debate among fans and players alike, with many praising Agha's instinctive decision to run out his opponent. The incident has sparked a renewed focus on the importance of discipline and sportsmanship in cricket.
