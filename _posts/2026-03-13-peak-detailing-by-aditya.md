In a shocking turn of events, the director of 'Dhurandhar 2: Captain Express', Aditya Dhar, has officially approved the 'Peak Detailing by Aditya Dhar' meme that has been making waves online. The hilarious memes, featuring the character of Captain Express, have been a staple of the internet, with fans using them to poke fun at the film. However, it seems that Aditya Dhar has taken the memes to the next level, giving his seal of approval to the 'Peak Detailing' meme. In a surprise move, Captain Express' captain has expressed his gratitude to the fans, stating that without their support, the film would not have been possible. He also acknowledged the challenge of creating the memes, stating that it was a difficult task, but one that was worth it in the end.
