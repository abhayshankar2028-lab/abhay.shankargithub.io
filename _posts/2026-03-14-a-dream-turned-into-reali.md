**Suryakumar Yadav on Completing Five Years in International Cricket**

In an exclusive interview, Suryakumar Yadav reflected on his incredible journey as a cricket player, particularly his remarkable five-year stint in international cricket. As India's all-rounder, Yadav led the team to their historic T20 World Cup title in 2021. He shared his thoughts on how this achievement was a dream turned into reality.

"I'm overwhelmed with emotions as I look back on this incredible journey," Yadav said. "It's been an incredible five years, and I'm grateful for the opportunities I've had. I've worked tirelessly to hone my skills and contribute to the team's success. This title is not just a personal achievement but a testament to the hard work and dedication of every player, coach, and support staff."
