In an exclusive chat with us, Abhishek Sharma, the Indian cricketer who played a crucial role in the 2011 World Cup-winning team, revealed that he has found an unlikely 'huge fan' in none other than the legendary Australian cricketer, Shane Watson.

Shane, a former Australian all-rounder, was a key player in India's World Cup-winning team back in 2011. Abhishek, who has been in the national team for over a decade, mentions that he was initially surprised to see Shane's name on the list of his favorite cricketers.

However, after meeting Shane in person, Abhishek is now a huge fan of the Australian legend. "You'd think he is under pressure, but these are the guys you want," Abhishek gushed. The two cricketers had a heart-to-heart conversation, and it's clear that their bond is as strong as their cricketing partnership.
