In a heartwarming display, Indian cricket team star Abhishek Sharma has revealed that he has found a huge fan in none other than former Australia World Cup winner, Steve Waugh. The 33-year-old bowler shared a touching moment with Waugh, who is no longer actively involved with the cricket world.

According to Sharma, Waugh is more focused on his personal life and doesn't seem to be under immense pressure. "You'd think he is under pressure but these are the guys you want to support," Sharma quipped in an interview. The Indian bowler expressed his gratitude to Waugh for his kind words and wishes. Sharma's gesture is a testament to the power of sportsmanship and the strong bond of friendship between two cricketers who have played for the same team.
