BREAKING NEWS IN HOLLYWOOD: The wait is finally over! Rohit Shetty, the mastermind behind the Golmaal franchise, has announced that Akshay Kumar will be joining forces with his old buddy Ajay Devgn in the upcoming fifth installment of the series, Golmaal 5!

Rohit Shetty, the director behind the previous four films, hinted at a massive comeback from the duo, and now it seems like it's finally happening. Akshay Kumar's addition is expected to bring in a significant boost to the film's production, making it a highly anticipated release. The excitement is palpable, with fans eagerly waiting for the big reveal. When asked about the new film, Rohit Shetty confirmed, "We're cooking up something big, something that will blow the minds of the fans. Get ready for a blockbuster of a film!" And we can't wait to see what's in store!
