![‘I can’t be the mentor for Delhi Capitals in this IPL season’: Kevin Pietersen ends one-year coaching stint with DC](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/KP-2.jpg)

"I'm proud of the work we've done as a team this season, but I think it's time for me to step aside and let someone else take the reins," Kevin Pietersen said, announcing his one-year coaching stint with the Delhi Capitals. The legendary cricketer, who joined DC in the 2022 season, expressed his gratitude for the experience and opportunities provided. However, he stated that he's no longer the right fit to lead the team. "I've enjoyed this journey with the Capitals, but I think it's time for me to take on a different challenge. I'm confident that the team will continue to thrive and have a great season," Pietersen said. The 42-year-old legend's decision marks the end of his one-year coaching stint with DC.
