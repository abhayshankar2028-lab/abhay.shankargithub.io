![‘I told Govinda don’t do the film if you can’t come on time’: Priyadarshan recalls telling the actor to ‘not add his own lines’ in Bhagam Bhaag](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/Govinda-2.jpg)

In an exclusive chat with Bollywood Buzz, actor Priyadarshan reminisced about the time he got quite the stern lecture from the one and only Govinda. It was during the filming of Bhagam Bhag, a massive action film that showcased Govinda's impressive dance skills. Priyadarshan jokingly reminded Govinda that if he couldn't make it on time, he shouldn't be involved in the project. "I told him, 'Govinda, don't do the film if you can't come on time,' and his eyes lit up with excitement. To our surprise, he started rehearsing his lines and making the most of his time on set. It was a hilarious and heartwarming moment that showcased Govinda's dedication to his craft. We wish he could have been a part of more films, but even without it, that particular moment will always be etched in our memories."
