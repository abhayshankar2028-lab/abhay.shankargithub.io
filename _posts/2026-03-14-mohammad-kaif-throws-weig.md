In a shocking display of restraint, former Pakistan captain Salman Ali Agha has been left fuming over a controversial run-out decision against Bangladesh in the T20 World Cup. "Never do it even in the World Cup final," Kaif advised Agha in a heartfelt post. "It's not worth risking your career over a simple mistake. The pressure is immense, and the stakes are too high. I know you're a great player, but this is a one-off. Focus on your game, not your ego," Kaif added. Agha's teammates, too, have rallied around their captain, with many expressing relief that Agha has taken the high road. Will Agha heed Kaif's words and avoid controversy? Only time will tell.
