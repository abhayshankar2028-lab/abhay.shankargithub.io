Robin Uthappa has been a reliable batsman for Vaibhav Sooryavanshi in the Ranji Trophy, but in recent matches, he has struggled to find form. He says, "He is riding his luck a little bit...". Uthappa has been given opportunities to bat at No. 8 in Vaibhav's middle order, and although he has scored some important centuries, he is still under pressure to deliver. The team manager, Vaibhav Sooryavanshi, is keen to get Uthappa back into the top six, but for now, the batsman needs to stay calm and focused. Uthappa has shown glimpses of his former self in the past, but his recent performances have been inconsistent. As the tournament progresses, his position in the batting order may be under threat.
