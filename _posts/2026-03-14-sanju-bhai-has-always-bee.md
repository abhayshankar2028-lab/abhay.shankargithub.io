In an exclusive interview, renowned Indian cricketer Abhishek Sharma shared his thoughts on the highly-acclaimed batsman Sanju Samson. Sharma praised Samson for his exceptional effort in the field, stating that the latter is "a very sorted guy" who always puts in the hard work required to excel in the game.

Hailing from the same Punjab-based family as Sanju Samson, Sharma expressed his admiration for the batsman's humility and love for cricket. "Sanju bhai has always been a very sorted guy. Very loving guy, very caring guy," Sharma said with a warm smile. Sharma also highlighted Samson's impressive record in the Indian Premier League, solidifying his place as one of the best wicket-keepers in the league.
