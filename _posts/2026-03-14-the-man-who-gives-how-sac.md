In a world where cricket legends often leave lasting legacies, one Indian cricketer stands out for his quiet yet profound impact on the game. Sachin Tendulkar, widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in cricket history, has had an unassuming yet profound influence on several of India's greatest careers. A phone call from Tendulkar, the iconic captain, can catapult a player's career to new heights. This is the story of Rohit Sharma, India's current Test captain, who credits Tendulkar with securing his spot in the Indian team, much to the surprise of others. Sharma, who has been a prolific individual batsman, credits Tendulkar for giving him the confidence to take on the best players in the world. Tendulkar's influence extends far beyond Sharma, shaping the careers of many other Indian cricketers.
