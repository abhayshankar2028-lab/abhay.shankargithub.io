![Akshay Kumar lauds Ranveer Singh’s Dhurandhar, reveals whether he regrets not being part of the film: ‘There is no jealousy’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/Akshay-Kumar-Ranveer-Singh-Dhurandhar.jpg)

In a recent interview, Bollywood superstar Akshay Kumar expressed his admiration for Ranveer Singh's performance in the film 'Dhurandhar'. The actor, who was not part of the film, praised Singh's portrayal of the iconic singer and actor. "There is no jealousy," Akshay said. When asked if he regrets not being part of the film, the actor replied, "I don't regret it at all. I think the audience loved the film and Ranveer brought his best to the role. I'm happy for him and his team." Akshay also praised Ranveer's dance moves, stating that he is a "dance genius". The actor's kind words for Ranveer have further solidified their long-standing bromance.
