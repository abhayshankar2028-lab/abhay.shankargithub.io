I'm excited to share my latest update, folks! Bollywood superstar Akshay Kumar recently expressed his immense admiration for Ranveer Singh's critically acclaimed film, Dhurandhar. The actor came to the set of the movie to promote the film and was impressed by Ranveer's energetic and engaging performance. When asked if he regrets not being part of the film, Akshay simply smirked and replied, 'There is no jealousy. I think Ranveer has done an amazing job with this film.' When asked if he felt any remorse, Akshay simply shook his head and said, 'No, no, no. This is a compliment to my friend Ranveer. We're just colleagues.' It's clear that Akshay respects and appreciates Ranveer's hard work in Dhurandhar. The actor's kind words only add to the film's success!
