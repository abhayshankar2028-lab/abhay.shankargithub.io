![Farah Khan says the Tees Maar Khan fallout was the darkest phase of her career: ‘I felt nobody should question me since I’d just made Om Shanti Om’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/farah-tmk.jpg)

In a recent interview, renowned choreographer Farah Khan reminisced about the tumultuous days following the release of her magnum opus, 'Tees Maar Khan'. This critically acclaimed film, co-starring Shah Rukh Khan, was a major comeback for Farah Kahn, who had recently completed her stint as the dance director for 'Om Shanti Om'. The 'Tees Maar Khan' debacle left the industry questioning Farah's professionalism and judgment. However, the veteran choreographer has expressed that the harsh criticism was the darkest phase of her career. She stated, "I felt nobody should question me since I'd just made Om Shanti Om. Those were the best days of my career, and I'm grateful to have come out of it stronger." This candid assessment serves as a testament to Farah Khan's resilience and ability to bounce back from adversity, ultimately solidifying her position as one of Bollywood's most respected choreographers.
