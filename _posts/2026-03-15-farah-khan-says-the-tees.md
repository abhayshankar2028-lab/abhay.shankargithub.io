I'm excited to dive into the complexities of the Bollywood industry with you. Yesterday, I came across an insightful quote from renowned choreographer Farah Khan, who openly discussed her experience working on the hit film 'Tees Maar Khan'. Khan shared that the project's fallout was indeed a particularly dark period in her career. Despite having a massive hit like 'Om Shanti Om' under her belt, the backlash from the controversy surrounding 'Tees Maar Khan' left her feeling vulnerable.

Khan expressed that she felt nobody questioned her during her heyday, but looking back, 'Tees Maar Khan' was a defining moment that tested her resolve. The experience taught her the importance of navigating criticism constructively. Khan's candid assessment of her career serves as a reminder that even the most successful artists are not immune to setbacks and that resilience is key to overcoming them.
