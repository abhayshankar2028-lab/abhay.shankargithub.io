**The Tees Maar Khan Fallout: A Dark Phase in Farah Khan's Career**

Farah Khan, the renowned Bollywood choreographer, recently spoke about the aftermath of her highly publicized dispute with Shah Rukh Khan. The Tees Maar Khan fallout, which shook the film industry in 2016, was indeed a dark phase in her career. Khan's bitter response to Shah Rukh's public criticism of her work left many in the industry questioning her decisions. However, there was one thing that Khan did not question: her artistic vision. In fact, her response only validated her dedication and passion for choreography. Khan's statement, "I felt nobody should question me since I’d just made Om Shanti Om," highlights her resilience and refusal to back down from her artistic endeavors. Her experience has undoubtedly made her a stronger and wiser individual, and her continued success is a testament to her unwavering commitment to her craft.
