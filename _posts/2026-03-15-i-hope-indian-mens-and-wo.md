**Momentum on the horizon for India's cricket teams**

In an exclusive interview, Indian cricket captain Rohit Sharma expressed his hopes for the country's two national teams, the men's and women's teams, to carry momentum in the next few months. Sharma, the skipper of the Men's Team, emphasized the team's need to regain their form after a series of inconsistent performances.

"We're not just playing for ourselves, but for the entire nation," Sharma said. "We need to start winning consistently and steadily to regain our momentum. The next few months will be crucial, and we need to come together as a team to achieve our goals."

Sharma also highlighted the importance of building on the team's strengths, particularly the spin bowling attack, which has been a key factor in their success in recent years. With the Indian Premier League (IPL) and the World Cup just around the corner, Sharma's words are more than just empty promises – they're a call to action for the Indian cricket team to bounce back from a disappointing 2023 season.
