In an exclusive interview, India's batting sensation, Sanju Samson, expressed his optimism that the team will continue to dominate the cricketing world. "India is going to do this more and more often," he confidently stated. When asked about the team's future prospects, Samson highlighted their ability to adapt to different cricketing conditions. "We have the talent, we have the experience, and we have the will to succeed," he emphasized. The 27-year-old all-rounder also mentioned that the team will continue to focus on building strong relationships between the players, coaches, and the support staff. "We have a great support system, and I'm confident that we will achieve great things together," Samson said. With his infectious enthusiasm and calm demeanor, it's clear that India is ready to take on the world.
