Rahul Dravid recently expressed his delight at J&K's Ranji Trophy triumph, highlighting the success of the tournament in showcasing cricket's presence across the nation. The J&K team's impressive performance demonstrated that cricket is a staple in every region of India. Dravid, the former Indian captain, emphasized that the Ranji Trophy is a premier format that is played in various states, including J&K.

"Cricket is not just a game in J&K, it's an integral part of the culture," Dravid stated. "The Ranji Trophy is a testament to the passion and dedication of the players, coaches, and support staff who work tirelessly to make it a success." Dravid's comments reflect a deep understanding of the game's diversity and its ability to thrive in different environments. The J&K triumph serves as a shining example of cricket's universal appeal.
