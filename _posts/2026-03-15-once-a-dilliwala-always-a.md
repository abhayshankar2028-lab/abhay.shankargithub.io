As the Delhi Capitals prepare to bid farewell to one of cricket's biggest stars, their captain Kevin Pietersen has expressed his gratitude and appreciation for the support he received during his stint with the team. In a heartfelt message, Pietersen thanked the fans for their unwavering loyalty and the team for the opportunity to be part of their journey. The 39-year-old all-rounder has been the heartbeat of the Delhi Capitals, providing the batting and bowling support that the team needed to compete at the highest level. As he departs for England, Pietersen's legacy at the Delhi Capitals will always be remembered, and his contributions will be cherished by the fans. The team sends their best wishes to Pietersen on his well-deserved retirement, and we wish him all the best as he embarks on a new chapter in his cricketing career.
