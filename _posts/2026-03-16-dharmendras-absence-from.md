![Dharmendra’s absence from Oscars’ in Memoriam segment draws backlash from Hema Malini, Shatrughan Sinha: ‘It is a shame’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/02/hema-malini-dharmender.jpg)

Bollywood's golden boy Dharmendra has been noticeably absent from the Oscars' in Memoriam segment, sparking a heated reaction from the stars who were left feeling left out. Actress Hema Malini took to social media to express her disappointment, calling Dharmendra's absence "a shame". "What a great honor it would have been to see him receive the tribute from the Academy," she tweeted. Actor Shatrughan Sinha also weighed in, stating that Dharmendra's omission was "unfortunate". "It's a great loss to our fraternity as Dharmendra would have been a wonderful addition to this segment," he said. The tweetstorm has sparked a mix of emotions, with some fans defending Dharmendra's decision while others feel he should have been included. It remains to be seen how the incident will be addressed by the Academy.
