**A Lesson in Humility: Akshay Kumar Opens Up About Inspiration**

In a candid interview, Akshay Kumar shared a poignant story about why he chose to work with none other than the legendary Amitabh Bachchan. While working on the film "Bhoothnath", Akshay has often cited Amitabh as his guiding force. However, Akshay revealed that his father-in-law was not the main inspiration behind the role. "Rajesh Khanna, my father-in-law, I learnt what not to do," Akshay quipped. He then went on to talk about how working with Amitabh was a valuable learning experience for him. "I learned that even the greatest stars have their own path to success. I'm grateful for that experience and it's been a defining moment in my career." Akshay's humility and gratitude towards Amitabh are truly inspiring, and we can learn from his approach to success.
