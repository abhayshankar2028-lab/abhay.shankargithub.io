**Indian Team Prioritises Collective Success Over Personal Milestones**

In an exclusive interview, Sanju Samson, the wicket-keeper batsman, emphasized the importance of collective success for the Indian team. While individual performances are crucial, Samson stressed that the team's collective efforts and camaraderie are equally vital. "We need to work together as a unit, not just as individuals," he emphasized. "Our team's strength lies in our ability to support each other, and that's what has guided us to our success so far." Samson highlighted the importance of toiling behind the scenes, working tirelessly, and being aware of each other's strengths and weaknesses. He also emphasized the need for patience and understanding, saying, "We need to learn to appreciate each other's differences and work as a team, not just as individual players."
