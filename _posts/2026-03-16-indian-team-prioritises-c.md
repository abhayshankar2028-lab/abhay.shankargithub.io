In an exclusive interview, Indian wicket-keeper extraordinaire Sanju Samson expressed his team's unwavering commitment to collective success. While he acknowledged the significance of individual milestones, Samson emphasized that the ultimate goal is to bring home a World Cup title.

"Personal milestones are important, but they shouldn't come at the cost of the team's collective success," Samson said. "We've worked hard to build a strong team culture, and I believe it's essential to prioritize our collective well-being. We're not just playing for ourselves; we're representing our nation and our fans."

Samson's words echo the team's mantra of "Team First," and it's clear that the Indian squad is focused on winning it all, not just for individual glory. As the team prepares for the ICC World Cup, Samson's leadership and dedication will be crucial in guiding them to their ultimate goal.
