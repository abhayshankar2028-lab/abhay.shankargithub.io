**Amaal Mallik Speaks Out: Arijit Singh's Decision to Step Back**

In a bold move, Arijit Singh has decided to step away from playback singing, sparking concerns about his future in the industry. Amaal Mallik, the renowned composer, hasn't minced words in defending his friend's decision. "It cannot be treated like an obituary," Amaal said in an interview. He emphasized that Arijit's decision is a personal one and not a reflection on his talent or dedication to the craft. Amaal also highlighted that Arijit's decision is not taken lightly, and he respects the singer's choice. With Amaal's support, the music industry will have to adapt to Arijit's new path, while also acknowledging his contributions to the scene. As Amaal put it, "We will have to evolve and find new ways for Arijit to express himself."
