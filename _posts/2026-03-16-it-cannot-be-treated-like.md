Title: Amaal Mallik's Sharp Wits On Arijit Singh's Departure

In a stunning display of diplomatic finesse, Amaal Mallik, the renowned composer, recently weighed in on Arijit Singh's decision to step away from playback singing. When questioned about Singh's departure, Mallik cleverly reframed the situation as a "healthy shift" in Arijit's career trajectory. "It cannot be treated like an obituary," he astutely observed, suggesting that Singh's time in playback singing has been a valuable learning experience. This astute remark highlights Mallik's ability to navigate the nuances of celebrity culture with ease. By reframing Singh's departure as a natural progression, Mallik has effectively deflected any criticism, implying that Singh's decision is a testament to his artistic growth rather than any personal dissatisfaction.
