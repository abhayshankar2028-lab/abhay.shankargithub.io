In a recent interview, Amaal Mallik expressed his unwavering support and understanding for Arijit Singh's decision to step away from playback singing. The iconic composer and singer took to social media to defend his friend, who has been facing immense pressure and scrutiny due to his career choices. Mallik emphasized that just because Singh decided to focus on playback singing, it doesn't mean he's abandoning his music entirely. He pointed out that every artist has their own unique path and priorities, and Singh's decision is not a reflection on his talent. "It cannot be treated like an obituary," Mallik said, urging his peers to respect Arijit's wishes and support him in his decision. This heartwarming defense has sparked a positive response from fans and fellow artists alike, celebrating Singh's courage and decision to prioritize his happiness.
