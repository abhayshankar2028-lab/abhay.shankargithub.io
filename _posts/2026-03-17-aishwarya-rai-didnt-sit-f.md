A fascinating anecdote about Aishwarya Rai's style transformation has left us all in awe. The renowned jewelry designer, Ponniyin Selvan, shared an extraordinary story about how Aishwarya Rai, who was preparing to walk the red carpet, opted to sit for only 7 hours to minimize creasing on her designer gown. What's remarkable is that she didn't just cut the time, but also took extraordinary measures to prevent creasing. The designer revealed that Aishwarya asked for a specific type of fabric and requested her team to carefully fold and iron the gown to prevent wrinkles. The result was a stunningly flawless look, leaving fans and critics alike in awe of Aishwarya's extreme professionalism. This testament to her dedication to her craft is a reminder that with great power comes great responsibility – and Aishwarya Rai undoubtedly lives up to it.
