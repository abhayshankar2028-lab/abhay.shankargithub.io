IPL 2026 is just a few months away, and with it comes the excitement of team dynamics and player pairings. One such interesting development is the recent statement made by Indian cricketer Mohammad Kaif. In an interview with ESPN Cricinfo, Kaif expressed his opinion that K.L. Rahul should only open the innings for the Delhi Capitals in the upcoming season. Kaif reportedly stated, "I think K.L. should only open the innings for Delhi Capitals. I think he has the ability to do that." While Kaif's comments may raise some eyebrows, it's essential to consider his perspective as an opponent and a teammate. Kaif has been a key player for the Delhi Capitals in the past, and his opinion on Rahul's batting position could have significant implications for the team's strategy.
