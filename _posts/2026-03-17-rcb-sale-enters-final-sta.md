**RCB Sale Enters Final Stage: $2 Billion Price Tag Revealed**

The Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) sale has entered the final stage, and it's getting increasingly closer to its target price. According to reports, the team is looking to sell a significant portion of its ownership, and the price is expected to be in the $2 billion range. This valuation is significantly higher than the previous estimates, which pegged the sale price around $1.5 billion. The move is seen as a strategic maneuver by the RCB management to secure a significant investment in the team. With the Indian Premier League (IPL) season just around the corner, the team is eager to finalize the sale and secure the necessary funds to compete with top teams. The $2 billion price tag is expected to make the RCB sale one of the most expensive in the history of the IPL.
