MS Dhoni, the legendary Indian cricketer, has always been known for his impressive skills on the field. However, football is his true passion. When asked about his favorite football team, former Chennai Super Kings (CSK) teammate Sam Billings revealed that MS Dhoni's heart belongs to the English Premier League team, Manchester City.

"I've known MS for a long time, and I've had the pleasure of playing with him during our time in Chennai," Sam Billings said. "He's a true leader and has always been a fan of Manchester City. His love for the team is evident, and I'm not surprised that he's an avid supporter."

With a keen eye for detail, Sam Billings couldn't help but reveal the Indian cricketer's secret love for Manchester City. It's no surprise that MS Dhoni is the proud owner of the team.
