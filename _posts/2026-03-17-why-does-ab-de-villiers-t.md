In a recent interview, AB de Villiers expressed his thoughts on MS Dhoni's performance with the Chennai Super Kings (CSK). De Villiers believes that MS Dhoni's decision-making is not always in the best interest of the team. He mentioned that Dhoni often delays decision-making, which can lead to missed opportunities. The South African cricketer also pointed out that Dhoni's tendency to adopt unorthodox strategies can sometimes backfire. Furthermore, Dhoni's reluctance to bat with a specific plan can cause frustration among the team. De Villiers concluded that Dhoni's approach is "almost there for the wrong reasons," as it can lead to disappointment and frustration for the players. This criticism is likely to be a topic of discussion among cricket fans, with many questioning Dhoni's leadership style.
