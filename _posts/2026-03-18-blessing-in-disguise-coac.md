IPL 2026 is on the horizon, and the excitement is palpable. One name that has left fans buzzing is Blessing Muzarabani, who has joined Mumbai Indians as a replacement for Ishan Kishan. Coach MS Dhoni has made the move to bring in the talented South African right-arm fast bowler. While it's early days for Muzarabani, the coach has already expressed his delight in his signing. "Muzarabani is an incredible talent, and I believe he can be the fourth star for Mumbai Indians," Dhoni stated. The 22-year-old has already made a strong impression in the Indian Premier League, showcasing his skills with the new ball. With Muzarabani at the helm, the Mumbai Indians are well-positioned to make a strong push for the title. The IPL 2026 season is shaping up to be an exciting one, and fans are eagerly anticipating the performances of their new signing.
