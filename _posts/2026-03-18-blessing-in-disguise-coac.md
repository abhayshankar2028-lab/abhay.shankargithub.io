IPL 2026 has just opened, and it's proving to be an exciting season for many teams. One player who has caught the eye of Coach Nayar is Blessing Muzarabani. The talented all-rounder has been a valuable asset to KKR, providing both batting and bowling opportunities. Coach Nayar credits Muzarabani's versatility for his impressive performances. "Blessing Muzarabani's signing has been a blessing in disguise for us," Coach Nayar said. "He has been a game-changer for us, especially in the middle order. His ability to bat and bowl has been a game-decider for us. We are excited to see how he performs in IPL 2026." With Muzarabani on board, KKR looks to make a significant impact in the tournament.
