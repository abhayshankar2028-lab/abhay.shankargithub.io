**Dhurandhar 2 to Hit Telugu and Tamil Screens at Unconventional Hours: A Delight or Disappointment?**

The highly anticipated Bollywood remake, Dhurandhar 2, is set to hit the screens in Telugu and Tamil with a unique twist – it's scheduled to premiere at 9 pm, followed by a special showing of various other Bollywood shows that are expected to start at the same hour.

However, due to various delays, exhibitors are facing a significant setback, and the situation is causing a ripple effect on the cinemas' operations. Producers are being blamed for the delays, which are mainly attributed to the scramble to acquire the necessary permissions and clearances for the new shows.

As a result, the cinemas are struggling to manage the fallout, and the audience is left wondering if the delay will ultimately lead to a better viewing experience. The situation remains uncertain, and only time will tell if this unconventional scheduling will pay off for the film and its accompanying shows.
