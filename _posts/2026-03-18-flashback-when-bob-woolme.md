**Flashback: Death in the Air - Bob Woolmer's Tragic End**

It's been over a decade since a devastating turn of events shook the cricketing world. On July 18, 2007, Bob Woolmer, the Head Coach of the Pakistan cricket team, was found dead in a hotel room in Kingston, Jamaica. It was just 24 hours after Pakistan's crushing defeat to Ireland in the ODI World Cup. The investigation revealed that Woolmer was stabbed to death in his hotel room by a man he had discovered to be an associate. This senseless act of violence sent shockwaves through the cricketing community and raised questions about the safety of cricketers. The incident remains one of the darkest moments in cricket history, serving as a reminder of the risks and sacrifices made by players and staff to pursue their passion.
