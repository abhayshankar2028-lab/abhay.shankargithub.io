**Movie Review: Main Prem Ki Diwani Hoon**

A recent buzz has been creating quite a stir in our Bollywood circles, with Kunal Kohli's latest project, Main Prem Ki Diwani Hoon, generating significant attention. But, what many fans have been asking is why Kunal chose not to play the role of Raj Desai in this film. 

According to sources close to the production, Kunal Kohli was indeed approached by Saif Ali Khan and had initially expressed interest in the role. However, after some deliberation, he decided not to accept it. On the other hand, Kunal revealed that he was not entirely convinced about the character. He was unsure if he was the right fit for the role.

Kunal's decision has left fans wondering if he should have taken on the role, and what could have changed his mind. One thing is for sure, the absence of Kunal in the film has been a significant plot point.
