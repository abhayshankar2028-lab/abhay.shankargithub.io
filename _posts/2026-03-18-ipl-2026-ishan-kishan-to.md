IPL 2026: SRH Announce Leadership Changes

The Indian Premier League (IPL) has seen significant changes as star player Pat Cummins will be sidelined due to injury. In his absence, Ishan Kishan has been named the new captain of the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) team. RCB's management has made this announcement, citing the need for a fresh perspective under the captaincy. To complement Ishan Kishan, Abhishek Sharma has been named the vice-captain of the team. Sharma, a veteran batsman, will take on a crucial role in guiding his team through the season. With Ishan Kishan at the helm, RCB is expected to be a contender for the title. The team will be led by Ishan Kishan, who has consistently impressed with his batting skills, and will be supported by Abhishek Sharma, who brings extensive experience and leadership qualities.
