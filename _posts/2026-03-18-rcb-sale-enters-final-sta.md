RCB sale: The final stage of the Indian Premier League (IPL) has entered, with the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) sale reaching a fever pitch. The Indian cricket board, the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI), has announced that the RCB sale will take place on March 18, 2023. The price of RCB has skyrocketed to an astonishing $2 billion, making it one of the most expensive IPL teams ever sold. This sale has raised curiosity among cricket fans, with many speculating that the team may be sold to an international player or a team from another country. The BCCI has confirmed that the sale will be completed in a matter of days, with the highest bidder being announced soon. The sale marks a significant turning point in the IPL, as it sets the stage for the league's next season.
