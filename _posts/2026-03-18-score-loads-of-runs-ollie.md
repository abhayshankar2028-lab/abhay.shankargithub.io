In a recent interview, England batsman Ollie Pope expressed his optimism for England's Test squad comeback. When asked about the roadmap for improvement, Pope stated, "I think we've got a good foundation now, and we're just looking to build on that. We need to score loads of runs, and that's what we're going to do." He emphasized the importance of patience and consistency in the batting department. Pope also highlighted the need for a more cohesive unit, citing the recent Test series against India as a prime example. "We've got a great team spirit, and we just need to work together," he added. Pope concluded by stating that he believes England can regain their Test status, with the batting unit being the key to their resurgence. With Pope's experience and the team's renewed focus, it seems like England is on the path to a successful comeback.
