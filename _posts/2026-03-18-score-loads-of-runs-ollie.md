In an exciting development for England cricket, Ollie Pope has been named on a potential Test squad roadmap. The 25-year-old right-hander, who has made a significant impact in the T20 phase, has been given a selection opportunity in the upcoming Test series against India. Pope's impressive form in the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2022 has caught the attention of the England selectors, who have been keen to see him re-evaluate his Test cricket. With his impressive batting skills and aggressive style, Pope has the potential to make a significant impact in the Test arena. This development could be a game-changer for England's Test squad, and fans are eagerly anticipating Pope's potential Test debut. The England team will likely announce the updated squad shortly, giving Pope a chance to prove himself in the red jersey.
