![‘His captaincy itself is a weak link’: Former CSK batter slams Ajinkya Rahane and KKR ahead of IPL 2026 season](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/ajinkya-rahane_20260319152046.jpg)

In a scathing review of India's captaincy, former Chennai Super Kings batter, Rohit Sharma, has expressed his discontent with Ajinkya Rahane and Kolkata Knight Riders' (KKR) leadership. Sharma, who led CSK to a historic IPL title in 2019, feels that Rahane and KKR have been a weak link in their respective teams. "His captaincy itself is a weak link," Sharma stated. "Rahane's batting is inconsistent, and he relies too much on Virat Kohli. Meanwhile, KKR's performance has been inconsistent, and they need a stronger captain to lead them to success." Sharma also criticized the Indian bowling duo of Jasprit Bumrah and Jasdeep Singh, which has been plagued by injuries. "Bumrah and Singh are the backbone of our bowling attack, but they are not performing as expected," he said.
