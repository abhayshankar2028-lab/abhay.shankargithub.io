In a scathing attack on his team's leadership, former Chennai Super Kings batter Ajinkya Rahane slammed his captaincy in the IPL 2026 season, leaving fans and teammates alike in shock. Rahane expressed his disappointment with the way the team has been run, pointing out areas where the management has failed. Specifically, he criticized the captaincy decisions, stating that "his captaincy itself is a weak link." Rahane also took aim at the team's star player, who he stated was under pressure to perform and not given enough opportunities to do so. The criticism has sparked a heated debate within the team, with Rahane's teammates calling for a change in leadership. The IPL 2026 season is just around the corner, and Rahane's outspoken comments have raised more questions than answers about the team's future under captaincy.
