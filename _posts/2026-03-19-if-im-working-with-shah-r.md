I'm Anupam Kher, and I'm thrilled to share my thoughts on Hollywood's portrayal of the "hero" movie star. When I'm collaborating with Bollywood legends like Shah Rukh Khan and Akshay Kumar, I often receive questions about the stereotype of the "hero" in Tinseltown. "Are we truly equals?" I ask. While it's great to be mentioned alongside these icons, I'd like to challenge the notion that we're interchangeable.

As actors, we bring our own experiences, emotions, and perspectives to the table. We're not just one-dimensional heroes; we're multidimensional individuals with our own strengths, weaknesses, and quirks. By embracing our differences, we can break free from the mold of the "hero" trope and create more authentic, nuanced performances. So, to all the "heroes" out there, let's celebrate our uniqueness and work together to redefine what it means to be a hero.
