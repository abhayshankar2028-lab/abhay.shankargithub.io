Breaking News in the Music Industry

Recently, the National Commission for Women (NCW) has issued summons to several high-profile song makers in connection with the song 'Tateeree' and 'Sarke Chunar'. The music duo, composed of Badshah and Nora Fatehi, are among those who have received the summons.

The NCW has alleged that the song has been promoting the objectification of women, which is a serious social issue in India. The summons have also invited renowned actors, Sanjay Dutt, who is known for his progressive views on social issues.

The NCW's inquiry is likely to dig deeper into the content of 'Tateeree' and 'Sarke Chunar', which has been widely criticized for its lyrics and music video. It remains to be seen how the music industry will respond to these allegations and whether the song makers will cooperate with the NCW's investigation.
