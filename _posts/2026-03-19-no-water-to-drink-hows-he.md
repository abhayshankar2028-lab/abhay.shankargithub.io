In a candid interview, Aamir Khan shared a lighthearted anecdote about his on-set camaraderie with Ashutosh Gowariker. During the filming of Lagaan, they often joked about the state of their co-stars, particularly Bhuvan Salil. One memory that stands out is when Bhuvan refused to shave off his beard, and Aamir jokingly asked Ashutosh to cut it off for him. Ashutosh, embarrassed by the request, jokingly disputed that Bhuvan had no water to drink, implying that the actor was always clean-shaven. The exchange became a laugh-filled moment on the set, and it's a testament to their playful dynamic. This funny anecdote highlights the close bond that developed between Aamir and Ashutosh, and how they learned to appreciate each other's quirks during the making of Lagaan.
