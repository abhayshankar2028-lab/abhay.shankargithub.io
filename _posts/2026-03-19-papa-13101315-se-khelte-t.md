IPL camp just concluded, and Arjun Tendulkar and Rishabh Pant couldn't stop discussing their idol, Sachin Tendulkar. During a post-match interview, the duo took the opportunity to share their thoughts on Sachin's iconic bat weights. Tendulkar revealed that Sachin's bat weights were around 1310-1315 grams, which is roughly equivalent to 3.6-4.2 pounds. Pant then chimed in, stating that Sachin's bat was lighter, weighing around 1310-1320 grams. Both players expressed their admiration for Sachin's incredible batting skills and mentioned that he was a true epitome of a batsman. The conversation highlighted the admiration and respect that the two cricketers have for Sachin Tendulkar, who is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the game.
