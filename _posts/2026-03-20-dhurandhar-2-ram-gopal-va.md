"Ram Gopal Varma's latest film, Dhurandhar 2, has sent shockwaves through the Bollywood industry. The director has come down on Aditya Dhar, co-director of the film, stating that he has "ended the era of 'godly heroes'". Dhar's decision is a bold statement against the traditional hero archetype, which Varma claims has been tried and tested for decades. Other directors, including Karan Johar and Aamir Khan, are said to be "rattled" by Dhar's comments. Varma has declared a war on the established order, warning that directors like Dhar will be "waking up shivering in cold sweat" to adapt to the changing landscape of Bollywood. This bold statement has sparked a heated debate, with many questioning the relevance of the traditional hero archetype in an era of diverse storytelling.
