Ek star hai, usko bhi side karega: Aamir Khan's 'Lagaan' Shoot Fears Revealed

In the early days of the Lagaan shoot, Aamir Khan was so nervous that he feared the film would never come off. During the first week, the entire team was on edge, and the pressure was palpable. Aamir, who played the lead role of Chappal, was anxious about meeting the director Ashutosh Gaud and the rest of the team. He was worried that his character's story would be overshadowed by the backdrop of the Indian cricket team's victory in the 1999 World Cup. Aamir's fears were validated when the team's victory was announced, and the film's script was rewritten to include the cricket match. By the end of the first week, the team had come together, and the film was on track to become a blockbuster.
