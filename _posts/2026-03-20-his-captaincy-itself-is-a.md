"IPL 2026: Ajinkya Rahane, the former Indian captain, has opened his mouth to slam his former team, Mumbai Indians, and Kolkata Knight Riders. In an exclusive interview, Rahane called out his former captain, Rohit Sharma, and KKR for their lack of leadership during the 2022 season. Rahane expressed his disappointment in the team's inability to perform under pressure and lacked the creative decision-making skills, citing his captaincy as the weak link. "His captaincy itself is a weak link," Rahane said. "We needed a better leader to guide us through the tough times. Rohit's leadership was lacking, and it affected the team's performance. I think we all know what happened. The team's performance was a result of his leadership." Rahane also hinted at the absence of his former team-mates and coach, suggesting that their absence was another factor in KKR's downfall."
