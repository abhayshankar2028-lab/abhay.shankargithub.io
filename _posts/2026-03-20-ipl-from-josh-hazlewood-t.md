**IPL Injury List: Overseas Players**

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is known for its high-intensity matches and unpredictable injuries. The latest season has been no exception, with several overseas players succumbing to various injuries. In this post, we'll take a look at the injury list of some of the biggest names in the sport.

Josh Hazlewood's absence from the squad has been a significant blow to the Delhi Capitals. Similarly, Moeen Ali's injury setback has forced him to sit out the second IPL season. Meanwhile, Sam Curran's finger injury has left him without a bat for the second time this year. The injuries have exposed the vulnerabilities of the overseas players and have raised concerns about their ability to perform under pressure. These incidents are a reminder of the physical toll that professional cricket can take and the need for teams to prioritize player safety.
