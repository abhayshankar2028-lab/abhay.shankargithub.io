In a stunning display of maturity, Prakash Raj has distanced himself from the Dhurandhar 2 frenzy. The actor, known for his sharp wit, took a few jibes at his co-stars, including Allu Arjun and Jr NTR, in a recent interview. When asked about his criticism, Raj stated, "Signs of obligations spreading to South too. The thrill of a film's release is what truly excites the audience, not the controversy surrounding it." This bold move has left many questioning whether Prakash Raj's latest film is the one to look forward to, or if he's just playing it safe. The actor's tongue-in-cheek remark has also sparked a debate about the role of controversy in a film's success. As fans eagerly await the release of Prakash Raj's next project, one thing is certain: the actor's sharp wit and caustic humor have once again left a mark.
