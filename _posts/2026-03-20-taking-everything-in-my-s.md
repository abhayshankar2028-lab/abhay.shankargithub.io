In an exclusive interview, KKR captain Ajinkya Rahane opened up about the immense pressure that comes with leading the team in the IPL 2026 season. While the tournament is still a year away, Rahane acknowledged that he is feeling more pressure than ever before. "I think every player feels the pressure of leading their team in a major tournament," Rahane said. "But for me, it's about what I can control – my preparation, my team's performance, and my own attitude." Rahane emphasized that he will take everything in stride, focusing on his game and the team's overall performance. He also highlighted the importance of setting a good example for his players and the team's fans. With a focus on hard work and determination, Rahane is confident that KKR will rise to the challenge in IPL 2026.
