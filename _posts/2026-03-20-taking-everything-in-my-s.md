**KKR Captain Ajinkya Rahane on IPL 2026 Pressure: A New Chapter Awaits**

In a recent interview, KKR captain Ajinkya Rahane revealed his thoughts on the increased pressure that comes with playing in the IPL 2026. "It's a new chapter in my career, and we have to take everything in our stride," Rahane stated. The captain acknowledged that the competition will be fierce, but emphasized that they are ready to face it head-on.

Rahane also expressed his gratitude towards the team, saying that they have a strong squad and a great team dynamic. He emphasized that the focus should be on winning the tournament, rather than worrying about individual pressure. With a new season around the corner, Rahane is looking forward to the challenge and is confident in his team's ability to perform under pressure.
