In an exclusive conversation with me, Bollywood superstar Akshay Kumar shared his profound thoughts on watching classic films like "Way of the Dragon" and "Missing in Action". The 45-year-old actor stated, "Watching Chuck Norris in these films wasn't just entertainment; it was education. It taught me discipline, perseverance, and the importance of self-defense." Born in Tamil Nadu, Kumar's journey to stardom started with films like "Surya" and "Aap se Kya Hota Hai". He further emphasized, "I remember watching 'Missing in Action' for the first time, and it made me realize the sacrifices made by our war heroes. It humanized them, and it made me appreciate the value of their service." Kumar's perspective on these films serves as a valuable lesson for those who aspire to become action heroes or veterans.
