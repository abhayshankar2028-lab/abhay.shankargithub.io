The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) has announced that India will be playing a two-match T20I series against Ireland on June 26 and June 28 at the MCG in Melbourne, Australia. The series will be a crucial one for the Indian cricket team, following their recent tour of the United Kingdom. The Indian squad has been confirmed, with players such as Hardik Pandya, Ravichandran Ashwin, and R. Ashwin playing key roles in the series. The series is expected to be a high-intensity, competitive clash of bowlers and batsmen. The Indian team will look to build on their recent success in the UK, where they defeated Australia in a T20I series. The series is expected to be a thrilling one, and fans of Indian cricket are eagerly anticipating the matches.
