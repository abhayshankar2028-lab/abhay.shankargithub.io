![Irfan Pathan on Rinku Singh: ‘Couldn’t fully capitalize on the chances he got’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2025/05/Rinku-6.jpg)

Irfan Pathan has been a stalwart for the Indian cricket team throughout his illustrious career. Recently, he lauded Rinku Singh's impressive performances in the domestic circuit. Pathan expressed that Rinku Singh couldn't fully capitalize on the chances he got, adding that he was often in the right place at the right time. Irfan Pathan's praise for Rinku Singh's batting skills, as well as his impressive record in the Ranji Trophy, only serves as further testament to the young player's potential. The former Indian bowler acknowledged that Rinku Singh worked hard to improve his skills, and it's heartening to see him pushing himself to become a better player. With the benefit of experience, Rinku Singh has a genuine chance of making a mark in international cricket, and Irfan Pathan's endorsement is a testament to his dedication and hard work.
