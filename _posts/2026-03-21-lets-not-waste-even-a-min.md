IPL 2026 is just around the corner, and Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is gearing up to face off against their arch-rivals, the Delhi Capitals. To get their squad fired up and ready for the battle, Virat Kohli, the team captain, has delivered a rousing pep talk.

In a passionate and motivational speech, Kohli urged his players to "let's not waste even a minute of any session". He emphasized the importance of focus, discipline, and teamwork, reminding them that every moment counts in the pursuit of success. Kohli's words have sent a strong message to his players to be fully committed and put in their all. With a fierce determination in their eyes, RCB is ready to take on the best teams in the league and make their fans proud.
