In a recent interview, Indian spin bowler R Ashwin expressed his thoughts on the Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) batting lineup. When asked about the current state of the team's batting, Ashwin stated that he would be preferring Rinku Singh to Karun Nair at No. 4. Ashwin believes that Singh's aggressive batting style would provide a much-needed boost to KKR's batting line-up, which has been struggling with consistency. With Singh's ability to score big runs, Ashwin thinks that the team would benefit from his presence at No. 4. KKR's batting lineup, which has been led by Nair, has been inconsistent in recent games. Ashwin's comments suggest that Rinku Singh could be a game-changer for the team.
