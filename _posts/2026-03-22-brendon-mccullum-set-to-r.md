**Brendon McCullum to remain England head coach: ECB Ashes review outcome**

In a surprise move, England's cricket team has announced that Brendon McCullum will remain their head coach, despite the proposed Andrew Flower appointment for the Ashes series. The English Cricket Board (ECB) has released an Ashes review, which suggests that McCullum's experience and leadership style would be beneficial in the upcoming series against Australia. McCullum, a highly successful captain and batsman, has been instrumental in England's recent success, leading them to the final of the 2019 World Cup. The ECB has opted to retain McCullum as head coach, citing his ability to deliver a high-powered attack and effective team management. The decision is seen as a significant boost to England's Ashes hopes, as the team prepares to face their arch-rivals in a highly anticipated series.
