Gary Kirsten, the former South African captain, has expressed his surprise and disappointment at the allegations of under-performance by Pakistan cricketers at the 2022 T20 World Cup. In an interview, Kirsten stated that he was not shocked to hear about the fine imposed on several Pakistani players for their lackluster display during the tournament. Kirsten had initially expressed his support for the players, but it appears that they have faced severe punishment for their underwhelming performance. The incident has sparked a heated debate among cricket fans, with many questioning the fairness and consistency of the punishment. Kirsten's comments have been seen as a reflection of his own expectations and standards, judging by his past experiences with Pakistani cricketers. The incident serves as a reminder that in international cricket, results can be decisive in determining the fate of players.
