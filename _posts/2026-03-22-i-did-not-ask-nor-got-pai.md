![‘I did not ask nor got paid’: Sunil Gavaskar defends himself after appearing on Pakistani show in Dubai during CT 25](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/gavaskar-abrar.jpg)

I did not ask nor got paid to appear on the popular Pakistani show 'I Did Not Ask nor Got Paid' in Dubai. While the show's hosts were praising the Indian cricket icon, I felt it was an opportunity to set the record straight.

As a sports legend, I have always maintained my integrity and refused to accept any compensation for my time. When I was told about the show, I was surprised and concerned. I felt it was essential to address the issue directly.

In a statement, I expressed my appreciation for the show's hosts but reiterating that I did not receive any payment or compensation for my appearance. I also emphasized that my decision not to engage in any promotional activities was based on my personal preferences rather than any financial gain.
