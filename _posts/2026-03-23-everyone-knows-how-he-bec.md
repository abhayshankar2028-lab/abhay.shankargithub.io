In a recent interview, former India opener Virender Sehwag took a jibe at the appointment of Riyan Parag as the new vice-captain of the Indian cricket team. Sehwag, who is a renowned cricketer, expressed his surprise and skepticism towards Parag's selection. "Everyone knows how he became RR captain," Sehwag said with a smirk, implying that Parag's rise to the top was unexpected and not entirely justified. Sehwag also questioned Parag's batting skills, stating that he would need a lot of hard work and dedication to surpass Virat Kohli and Rohit Sharma. The comments were met with a lukewarm response from Parag and the Indian cricket team management, who have since opted to keep Parag as the vice-captain. Sehwag's candid remarks have sparked a lively debate on social media, with many fans taking sides and expressing their opinions on the matter.
