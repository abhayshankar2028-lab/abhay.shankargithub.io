"From Hathod to the IPL: How a small Rajasthan academy is producing big-money cricketers"

The small town of Hathod in Rajasthan has been a breeding ground for India's top cricketers, producing stalwarts like Sachin Tendulkar, Kapil Dev, and Harbhajan Singh. However, the academy's journey didn't begin here. Founded in 2008, Hathod Cricket Academy has been instrumental in nurturing young talent from the state's rural areas. With a strong emphasis on batting and bowling, the academy's players have gone on to represent India in international cricket. Their success has caught the attention of the Indian Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI), who have taken notice of Hathod's unique approach. Today, Hathod Cricket Academy is one of the most respected cricketing academies in the country, producing cricketers with big-money contracts and opening up new opportunities for rural players.
