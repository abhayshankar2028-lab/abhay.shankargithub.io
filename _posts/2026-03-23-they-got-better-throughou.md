![‘They got better throughout the Ashes’: Rob Key on why Brendon McCullum will continue on as England coach](https://images.indianexpress.com/2025/12/McCullum-2.jpg)

In an exclusive interview, former England batsman Rob Key expressed his confidence in Brendon McCullum's ability to continue as the team's coach. 

Key stated that McCullum has "gotten better" over the past two Ashes series, citing improved team dynamics and tactics. "He's got a better understanding of the game now," Key said. "The players trust him more, and he's able to get the most out of them."

Key also praised McCullum's "dry wit" and ability to "strip players of their confidence." "He's a winner, and winners don't give up," Key continued. "In the end, that's what's going to keep him at the helm." With England set to face new challenges in the upcoming Ashes series, Key believes McCullum is the right man to lead the team forward.
