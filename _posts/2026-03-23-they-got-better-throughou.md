![‘They got better throughout the Ashes’: Rob Key on why Brendon McCullum will continue on as England coach](https://images.indianexpress.com/2025/12/McCullum-2.jpg)

Rob Key has spoken highly of Brendon McCullum, crediting him for helping England improve during the Ashes. McCullum's tenure as England's director of cricket saw significant growth in the team, with a renewed focus on batting and bowling. Key emphasized that McCullum's expertise was instrumental in their success. "They got better throughout the Ashes," Key said. "Brendon's a brilliant coach, not just a batsman. His input was invaluable." Key also praised McCullum's ability to connect with the players, which was crucial in helping them improve. "He's a great bloke, a terrific leader," Key continued. This assessment has left fans and pundits eagerly anticipating the future of the England cricket team, with many believing that McCullum's expertise will continue to be a key factor in their success.
