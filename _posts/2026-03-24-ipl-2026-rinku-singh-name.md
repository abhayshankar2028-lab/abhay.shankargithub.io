IPL 2026: Rinku Singh named KKR vice-captain. 

 Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) announced that Rinku Singh will be their new vice-captain for the upcoming season. Singh, a talented all-rounder, will take on the responsibility of leading the team as KCR signals a future leadership shift. The franchise, known for their impressive teams, has been trying to build a strong leadership foundation. Rinku Singh's selection is a testament to his growth as a player and his ability to lead from the front. The new vice-captain will be instrumental in shaping the future of the team. This move is expected to boost KKR's chances in the upcoming season. With Rinku Singh at the helm, the franchise is poised for a successful campaign.
