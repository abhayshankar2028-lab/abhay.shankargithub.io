IPL 2026 just concluded, and the Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) have made a significant move to prepare for the future. Rinku Singh, the vice-captain of KKR, has been appointed as the team's new vice-captain. This development comes amidst a change in leadership, as KKR has signaled a future shift in leadership. The team's management has been keen to explore fresh perspectives and leadership styles, and Rinku Singh's appointment is a step in that direction. As the franchise continues to evolve, Singh's leadership skills and experience make him an ideal candidate to take charge. With his successful stint as vice-captain, Singh is poised to play a crucial role in shaping the team's future. This move is expected to give KKR a strong foundation for their upcoming challenges.
