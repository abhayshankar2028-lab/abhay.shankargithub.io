Rishabh Pant's price tag of Rs 27.5 crore for the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) seems too steep considering he hasn't delivered his best cricket yet. As a former IPL captain, I've seen my fair share of players who are hyped up only to fail in their contracts. Pant's T20 numbers aren't exactly impressive either, with a strike rate of just 122.57 and a batting average of 4.33.

I'm not saying he's bad, but to pay a whopping amount for someone who's not quite living up to expectations seems excessive. Can't the franchise find another player with better credentials and performance? RCB must have done their research and considered other options before signing Pant. It's time for them to re-evaluate and potentially let him go.
