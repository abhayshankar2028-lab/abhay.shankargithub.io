In a recent interview, MS Dhoni's impact on Indian cricket was thoroughly discussed, with R Ashwin sharing his thoughts on the coach's influence. Ashwin stated that Dhoni's leadership and guidance played a significant role in shaping the team's approach and strategy. He highlighted Dhoni's ability to balance individual and collective brilliance, which contributed to India's success in major tournaments. Ashwin also praised Dhoni's ability to make tactical decisions, even when they were unpopular. The veteran bowler suggested that if Dhoni doesn't want to play the entire season, that's his prerogative. However, Ashwin emphasized that Dhoni's legacy would be diminished if he withdrew from the team at a critical stage. Ashwin's comments reflect his deep respect and admiration for Dhoni's leadership and impact on the team.
