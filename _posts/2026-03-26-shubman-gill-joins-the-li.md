In a significant development, Shubman Gill has joined the growing list of critics for the Impact Player rule in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The new rule, implemented to tackle the problem of players' unauthorized use of technology, has already seen several players face disciplinary action. Gill's inclusion in this list sends a strong message that the IPL will not tolerate such practices. According to sources, the Impact Player rule aims to prevent players from using technology to gain an unfair advantage in the game. Shubman Gill's inclusion in this list takes the skill out of the game, as he is a talented cricketer who has made a significant contribution to the team's success. His potential suspension under the Impact Player rule would serve as a deterrent to other players who might think of exploiting the system.
