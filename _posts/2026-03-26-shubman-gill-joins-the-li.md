In a move that promises to shake the very foundations of the Indian Premier League, Shubman Gill has joined the growing list of critics for the Impact Player rule. Effective immediately, the IPL has banned players who have been involved in betting and matching on matches. This significant step was taken to curb the scourge of match-fixing that has plagued the league for years.

Shubman Gill, a talented all-rounder, is now the latest player to face the consequences of his actions. His addition to this list sends a clear message to the players that their actions will no longer be tolerated. The impact player rule is a step in the right direction, taking the skill out of the game and restoring the integrity of the competition. This move is expected to have a profound impact on the future of the IPL, and we can't wait to see the outcome.
