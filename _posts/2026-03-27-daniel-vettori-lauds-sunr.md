Ishan Kishan has been touted as the future of Indian cricket, and Daniel Vettori has taken notice. The former New Zealand leg-spinner has lauded the Sunrisers Hyderabad stand-in skipper, praising his confidence and leadership qualities. Vettori, a seasoned cricketer in his own right, believes that Kishan's attitude and poise have earned him the captaincy gig. "Ishan has been an outstanding player for Sunrisers Hyderabad, and his confidence as a captain is palpable," Vettori said. "As a player, you want to be part of a team that believes in you, and I think Ishan has achieved that with Sunrisers. His leadership qualities have been instrumental in the team's success, and I have no doubt he will continue to play an important role in India's cricket team."
