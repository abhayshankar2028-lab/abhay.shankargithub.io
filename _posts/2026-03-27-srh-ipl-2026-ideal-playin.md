**Sunrisers Hyderabad vs Royal Challengers Bengaluru in IPL 2026: Ideal Playing XI**

The highly anticipated season of Indian Premier League (IPL) 2026 is just around the corner, and Sunrisers Hyderabad is gearing up to take on Royal Challengers Bengaluru in the season opener. Here's a suggested playing XI for the Pink Ball side:

* Captain: David Warner
* Wicket-Keeper: Manish Pandey
* Batsman 1: Rohit Sharma
* Batsman 2: Quinton de Kock
* Batsman 3: AB de Villiers
* All-Rounder: Rashid Khan
* Bowler: Rashid Khan
* Spin Bowler: Kuldeep Yadav
* Fast Bowler: Bhuvneshwar Kumar

This XI combination brings together some of the best players in the league, with a perfect balance of batting and bowling options. David Warner's leadership and experience will be crucial in ensuring a strong start for Sunrisers Hyderabad. Rohit Sharma and AB de Villiers will provide a potent batting threat, while Rashid Khan and Kuldeep Yadav will provide the necessary spin and pace to trouble Royal Challengers Bengaluru's batting lineup.
