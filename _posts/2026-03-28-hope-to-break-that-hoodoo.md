"Hope to break that hoodoo: Jayawardene on MI's first-match defeats since 2013

In a thrilling start to the new season, Mumbai Indians (MI) faced their first challenge since 2013 in their opening match against the Chennai Super Kings. Despite a valiant effort from their new team, MI ultimately succumbed to a massive 8-wicket defeat. The loss came as a wake-up call for the team, with captain Rohit Sharma acknowledging that they had been riding a high after a 2013 season where they went down to the final.

"We knew it was going to be a tough match, but we didn't expect to lose by 8 wickets," Sharma said after the match. "We need to learn from our mistakes and come back stronger. We have a long season ahead of us, and we can't let one game define us. We'll bounce back and come back stronger." The team will now focus on regrouping and preparing for their next challenge."
