![‘One of the biggest challenges is dealing with the outside noise’: Jos Buttler on pitfalls of being an international player](https://images.indianexpress.com/2025/03/Buttler-8.jpg)

As an international cricketer like Jos Buttler, one of the biggest challenges he faces is dealing with the outside noise. The constant scrutiny and pressure to perform can be overwhelming, especially when you're a high-profile player. "It's tough to go out on the field and not have anyone watching," Buttler explained in an interview. "You're constantly being questioned about your game, your temperament, your decisions. It can be really frustrating at times." The media attention can also be intense, with fans and pundits constantly bombarding him with questions and comments. But Buttler credits his experience and preparation for helping him cope with the challenges. He's learned to manage his emotions and stay focused on the task at hand, even in the face of adversity.
