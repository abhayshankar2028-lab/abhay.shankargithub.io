Title: 'They called me panauti': Dia Mirza recalls being tagged 'bad luck'

Dia Mirza, the talented actress, has shared an hilarious story about being referred to as 'panauti' - a term that invoked 'bad luck'. She recounts how she was called this by her colleagues after a particularly tough day at work. Mirza reveals that she never took it seriously, instead choosing to laugh it off and joke about it. She even quipped, "I told Rajkumar Hirani, 'I have no work'." Mirza's lighthearted response has become an iconic moment in Bollywood history. The actress's candidness has shed light on the lighter side of the film industry, reminding us that even the most dire situations can't bring us down. Mirza's story is a testament to her signature wit and humor, proving that laughter is the best medicine, especially when life gets tough.
