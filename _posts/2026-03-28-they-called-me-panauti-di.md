Dia Mirza, the talented Bollywood actress, has shared a hilarious and heartwarming story about being referred to as 'panauti' - a term that translates to 'bad luck' in Hindi. In an interview, she revealed that during her early days in the film industry, she was often labeled as 'panauti' by her colleagues and friends. However, she never let it get her down and instead, used it as a badge of honor. When asked about her experience, she laughed and said, "I told Rajkumar Hirani, I have no work. That's how I knew it was me, not you." Dia's story highlights the power of attitude and resilience, proving that sometimes, being called 'panauti' can become a blessing in disguise. This quote has become an iconic representation of Dia's no-nonsense approach to life.
