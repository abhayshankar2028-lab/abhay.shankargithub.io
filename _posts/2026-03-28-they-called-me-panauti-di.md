**A Blast from the Past: Dia Mirza Opens Up about Being Tagged as "Panauti"**

In a candid interview, Bollywood actress Dia Mirza shared an amusing story about being referred to as "Panauti" - a term that still brings a smile to her face. The term was allegedly used by producers and directors to curse her, implying that she would never be successful in her career. Dia Mirza recounts the incident, saying, "They called me Panauti... I told Rajkumar Hirani, 'I have no work.' It was a clever way of telling me I was destined for failure." This amusing anecdote highlights the lighthearted side of the Bollywood industry, where even the most talented stars often find themselves subjected to negative stereotypes. Despite the taunts, Dia Mirza proved them wrong, going on to achieve great success in the industry.
