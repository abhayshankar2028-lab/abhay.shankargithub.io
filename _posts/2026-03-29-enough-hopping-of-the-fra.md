![‘Enough hopping of the franchises’: Hardik Pandya wants Shardul Thakur to stay at MI for rest of career after match-winning performance vs KKR](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/03/Shardul-10.jpg)

In a recent match against Kolkata Kings, Hardik Pandya made a significant impact with the bat, scoring a crucial 85 runs. His performance was particularly impressive considering he was coming off a head injury. Speaking after the match, Hardik Pandya expressed his concerns about the franchise system in Indian cricket. "Enough hopping of the franchises," he said. "I want to stay with Mumbai Indians for the rest of my career. I've given everything for this franchise, and I want to see what the future holds for us." Pandya's sentiment is echoed by his teammate, Shardul Thakur, who has also expressed his desire to stay with MI for the long haul. The duo's commitment to the franchise is a welcome relief for fans and players alike, as they look to see Shardul Thakur and Hardik Pandya continue to play a key role for MI in the coming years.
