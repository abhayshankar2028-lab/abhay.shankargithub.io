Mumbai Indians made a triumphant return to the IPL campaign, securing a resounding 6-wicket win over Rajasthan Royals on their inaugural match since 2012. Captain Rohit Sharma proved to be a masterstroke, leading his side to a stunning victory over the in-form Royals. Sharma's impressive batting display was key to his team's success, as he stroked the ball with ease to take the crease in the final over. His 45 from 37 deliveries was instrumental in securing the win, with the final blow coming off the economy rate. The win marked a fitting return to the IPL for Sharma, who was last seen in action in 2012. As the Indians embarked on their IPL journey, Sharma's leadership and batting prowess were on full display, setting the tone for a successful campaign.
