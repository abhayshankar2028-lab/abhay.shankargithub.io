IPL Summary - Rohit Sharma's Quickest IPL Half Century

In a thrilling match, Mumbai Indians continued their winning streak with a 6-wicket victory over Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR). The excitement was palpable as Rohit Sharma became the first batsman in IPL history to reach a half-century in the shortest format in under 47 balls. His blistering 66 not out helped Mumbai Indians to post a formidable total of 189/4 in 18.2 overs.

However, it was a crucial partnership from Ryan Rickelton that sealed the deal for the Mumbai Indians. The 34-year-old opener played a crucial role in helping his team break the 13-year-old opening match hoodoo, which had seen many top teams struggle to break the 100-run mark at the start of the match.
