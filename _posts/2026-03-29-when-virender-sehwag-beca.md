**A Historic Moment: Virender Sehwag Becomes First Indian to Hit a Test Triple Century**

On March 24, 2005, a momentous day in cricket history occurred when Virender Sehwag became the first Indian to achieve a Test triple century. Sehwag, who was then playing for Delhi, batted for 316 against England at the Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai. This remarkable feat not only marked a milestone for the country but also showcased his incredible batting skills. Sehwag's triple century was a testament to his unwavering dedication to the game and his unrelenting ability to perform under pressure.
