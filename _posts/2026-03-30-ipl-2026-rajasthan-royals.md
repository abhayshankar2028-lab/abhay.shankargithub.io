IPL 2026: Rajasthan Royals' pace battery controlled the game to a dominant victory over Chennai Super Kings. The hosts were on a mission to capitalize on a CSK batting collapse, but Vaibhav Sooryavanshi's explosive innings had other plans. Sooryavanshi, who was batting at number 9, came to life in the final over and scored 17 off 14 balls, taking the game away from the Super Kings. His blistering innings was marred by a single, but his power-hitting in the middle overs was instrumental in the Royals' victory. Sooryavanshi's innings had the Super Kings reeling, and by the time he was done, they were in for a losing draw. Rajasthan Royals' bowling was equally impressive, and they sealed the win with a convincing total.
