IPL 2026: Rajasthan Royals Sweep CSK convincingly Defeat Chennai Super Kings

In a dominant performance, Rajasthan Royals sealed a convincing 8-wicket win over Chennai Super Kings in a thrilling encounter at the Narendra Modi Stadium. The visiting Royals pace battery, spearheaded by veteran fast bowler Jofra Archer, proved too much for the Super Kings' batting lineup. Archer's blistering 28-ball 6/2, coupled with a special knock from star all-rounder Rihsad Mannan, helped the Royals to bowl out the Super Kings for 145 runs. Vaibhav Sooryavanshi, who made a stunning 48 off just 41 balls, and Gurpreet Brar's 5/20, wrapped up the innings for the Royals. Sooryavanshi's whirlwind knock helped turn the tide of the match, as the Royals secured their second consecutive win in the league. The victory propels the Royals to the top of the points table, while the Super Kings now sit in second place.
