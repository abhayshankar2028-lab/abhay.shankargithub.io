IPL fans were left disappointed on Sunday when the Indian Premier League (IPL) playoffs were canceled due to a COVID-19 outbreak. However, not everyone was affected, as Krunal Pandya, an Indian cricketer, shared a crucial piece of advice on social media.

When asked about the cancellation of IPL, Pandya said, "If Virat Kohli had been born in any generation, he would still be one of the greats." He added, 'Virat would have been the best batter in the world.' Pandya's words of wisdom are indeed sage advice. Pandya is known for his incredible batting skills and exemplary sportsmanship.

His words remind us that even with successes, humility and sportsmanship are just as important as winning. This message of Virat Kohli's reinforces the importance of staying grounded, even when achieving great things.
