His unique blend of pace, accuracy, and variation has made him a nightmare for opposing batsmen. Burger's stats speak for themselves, with a strike rate of just 3.4 and a economy rate of 7.41. His IPL numbers are a testament to his hard work and dedication. As the IPL season heats up, Burger is sure to be a hot tip for the Big Bash League.
