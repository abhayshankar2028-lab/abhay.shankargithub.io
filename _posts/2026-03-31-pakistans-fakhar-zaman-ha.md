Pakistan's Fakhar Zaman has been handed a 2-match suspension by the Pakistan Super League (PSL) due to ball tampering allegations. The 22-year-old cricketer was accused of using a banned substance to gain an advantage in the tournament. After an investigation, the PSL has decided to take disciplinary action against him. The suspension will keep him out of action for the next two matches. This is not the first time that Fakhar has been involved in such controversy, as he was banned for six matches in 2022 for using a banned substance. The PSL has a strict code of conduct that includes strict penalties for players found guilty of such offenses. The suspension serves as a warning to Fakhar, and it will be interesting to see how he recovers from this setback.
