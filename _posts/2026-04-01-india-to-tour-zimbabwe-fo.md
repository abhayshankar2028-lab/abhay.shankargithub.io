India has announced their tour schedule, and they will be heading to Zimbabwe for a three-match T20I series in July. The series will kick off on July 4th at the St. George's cricket ground in Harare. India's coach, Ravi Shastri, has highlighted the importance of this series, stating that it will be a great opportunity for the team to test themselves against a new environment. The tour dates are as follows:

* July 4th: India vs Zimbabwe, St. George's cricket ground, Harare
* July 6th: India vs Zimbabwe, Bulawayo Gymnasium, Bulawayo
* July 8th: India vs Zimbabwe, Harare

This series will be India's first T20I tour of Zimbabwe, marking a significant milestone in their Cricket World Cup preparations. The team will face stiff challenges in the second innings on July 7th, but with their experience and skill, they are confident of emerging victorious.
