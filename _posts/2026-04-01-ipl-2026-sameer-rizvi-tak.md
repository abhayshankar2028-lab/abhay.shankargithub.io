IPL 2026: Sameer Rizvi Leads Delhi Capitals to Thrilling Victory

In a nail-biting encounter, the Delhi Capitals secured a crucial 5-wicket win over the Lucknow Super Giants, thanks in large part to a sensational display from all-round bowler Sameer Rizvi. The Capitals initially struggled, but Rizvi's incredible bowling effort turned the tide of the match. His blistering spell, which included a stunning 4-wicket haul, saw the Capitals dominate the Super Giants. Despite a valiant effort from the Super Giants, they were unable to mount a comeback, and Rizvi's heroics sealed the win for Delhi Capitals. This win propelled the Capitals to the top of the table, making it a milestone victory for the team. Rizvi's performance was truly exceptional, and he will be remembered for his outstanding display in this thrilling encounter.
