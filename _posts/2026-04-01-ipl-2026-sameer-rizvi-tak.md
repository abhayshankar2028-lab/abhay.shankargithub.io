IPL 2026: Sameer Rizvi leads the charge for Delhi Capitals, but a dramatic turnaround in fortunes after bowlers set up an early win against the Lucknow Super Giants. The Supergiant opener was left stunned and shell-shocked as the spin duo of Ravi Bishnoi and Harish Rayan delivered a masterclass display to take the Capitals to a dominant lead on the trot. The damage was done before even the 10th over, with the Super Giants struggling to rebuild. As the Capitals closed in on a resounding 6-wicket win, the Super Giants' players were left to ponder what could have been. Rizvi's heroics, including a six to seal the deal, will be remembered for a long time to come.
