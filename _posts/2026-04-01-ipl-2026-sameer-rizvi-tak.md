IPL 2026: Delighting Delhi Capitals fans, Sameer Rizvi led their charge to an impressive 8-wicket win against the Lucknow Super Giants. The Capitals, struggling with a bowler's setback, rallied behind Rizvi, who dominated the proceedings with his blistering innings. The Namakmal Ranjitsinhji-led side, needing just 15 runs off his first 10 deliveries, sealed the victory with a crucial catch off a Super Giants fielder. Rizvi's 52-ball 60 off 64 deliveries, including a 5-ball 6, was the cornerstone of Delhi's innings. His partnership with Ravi Bishnoi formed a potent mix, as they kept the Super Giants' bowlers at bay. The win puts Delhi firmly in contention for the playoffs, and Rizvi's heroics will be remembered for a long time. Delhi Capitals are now back on track after a rollercoaster ride.
