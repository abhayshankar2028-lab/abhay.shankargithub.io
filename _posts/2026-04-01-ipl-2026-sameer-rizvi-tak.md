IPL 2026 Summary: Sameer Rizvi and Delhi Capitals seal historic victory against Lucknow Super Giants, avenging their earlier defeat in the same series. 

The Delhi Capitals took an early lead after a thrilling 8-wicket win over the Lucknow Super Giants. However, a crucial batting collapse and a double-digit dismissal for Delhi stunned the team. In a dramatic turn of events, Sameer Rizvi filled the void with a 68-ball score, and the Capitals rode on his efforts to seal a historic win.

The win cements Delhi's position at the top of the table, with a 2-1 lead over the Super Giants. This thrilling victory is a testament to the team's resilience and determination. Rizvi's impressive display has earned him a spot in the team's all-rounder of the series, cementing his place in the IPL history books.
