In a heartwarming display of respect and gratitude, Bollywood legend Amitabh Bachchan paid tribute to his 'senior' co-star Vinod Khanna at the recent Screen Awards. During the ceremony, Bachchan reminisced about their time working together, stating that Vinod was the 'pillar of our film'. He expressed his admiration for Vinod's dedication to his craft and his unwavering commitment to excellence. The 'Sholay' and 'Majnaarlykaan' veteran actor's words of praise were met with thunderous applause from the audience, showcasing his deep appreciation for his 'senior' colleague. This heartfelt gesture is a testament to the strong bond that exists between Bachchan and Khanna, two legends of the Hindi film industry. Their mutual respect and admiration for each other's work is a shining example of the power of dedication and collaboration.
