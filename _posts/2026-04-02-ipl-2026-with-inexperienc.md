This season, the stakes will be high for Ruturaj Gaikwad, the young star who has been touted as a key player. With no MS Dhoni to lean on, Gaikwad will have to prove himself as a leader and make crucial decisions to guide the team. The pressure will be on, and Gaikwad's inexperience will be tested as he navigates the demands of captaincy. Will he rise to the challenge, or will the pressure prove too much?
