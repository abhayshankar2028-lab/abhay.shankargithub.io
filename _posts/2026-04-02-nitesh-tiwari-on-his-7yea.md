Nitesh Tiwari, the renowned Indian filmmaker, has been delving into the timeless tale of the Ramayana for an impressive 7 years now. His journey into this epic story has been nothing short of extraordinary. With unwavering dedication, Nitesh Tiwari has brought the ancient Hindu epic to life on the big screen, captivating audiences worldwide. What sets his work apart is the meticulous attention to detail and the ability to weave the intricate narrative of the Ramayana seamlessly into a contemporary setting. His passion for the story is evident in every frame, making his film an unforgettable experience for viewers. Filmmaking, he confesses, can be a "speculative and lonely process", but his unwavering commitment to his craft has paid off.
