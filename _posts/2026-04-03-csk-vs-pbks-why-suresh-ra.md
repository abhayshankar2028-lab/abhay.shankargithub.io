**Suresh Raina's 87: A Masterclass in Powerplay Innings**

It's been over 7 years since Suresh Raina last took the field for Chennai Super Kings in the IPL. However, his 87* off 55 balls against Punjab Kings in 2014 still stands out as one of the greatest T20 powerplay innings. With the game hanging in the balance, Raina's exceptional batting was instrumental in turning the tide. He took 87 runs off just 55 deliveries, showcasing his exceptional hand-eye coordination and mental toughness.

Raina's innings was a perfect blend of caution and aggression, as he navigated the bowling attack with ease. His partnership with MS Dhoni was crucial in taking down the Kings, and his 87-run stand remains the highest in T20 cricket. This innings is still remembered as one of the greatest powerplay innings in IPL history, and it's a testament to Raina's enduring legacy as one of the greatest cricketers of all time.
