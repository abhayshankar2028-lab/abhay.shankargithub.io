Vaibhav Sooryavanshi is undoubtedly one of the most promising young bowlers in the IPL. His exceptional leg-spinner skills have been on full display, particularly against the opposition's top-order batsmen. One aspect that sets him apart from other leg-spinners is his ability to target the off-side, particularly the leg-side. Sooryavanshi's pinpoint accuracy ensures that the batsmen are always in for a challenge.

His seam movement is superb, allowing him to consistently bowl deliveries that catch the batsmen off guard. This is particularly evident against teams like Rajasthan Royals and Punjab Kings. Sooryavanshi's ability to effectively rotate the ball and pose a threat to the top-order batsmen makes him a formidable opponent. His skillset is undoubtedly one of the key reasons why he is dismantling the IPL bowling attacks with his leg-side range.
