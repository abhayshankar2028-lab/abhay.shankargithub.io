IPL 2026 is just around the corner, and the anticipation is building up. In a recent interview, Kuldeep Yadav, one of the most feared fast bowlers in the cricket world, has expressed his support for the attacking mindset ahead of the upcoming match between Delhi Capitals (DC) and Mumbai Indians (MI).

When asked about the key to success in T20s, Kuldeep emphasized that teams need to be aggressive and take risks to gain an edge. "You will be hit for runs in T20s," he stated bluntly. "So, we need to be bold and take the game away from the opposition. We need to be like a storm, unpredictable and explosive. If we can do that, we will have a chance to win." This mindset shift is expected to give the DC MI clash the edge it needs to emerge victorious.
