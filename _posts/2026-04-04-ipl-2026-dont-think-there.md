IPL 2026: The Indian Premier League (IPL) is set to kickstart its eleventh edition, and the excitement is palpable. However, the newly appointed head coach of Chennai Super Kings (CSK), Stephen Fleming, has highlighted a crucial aspect of the game. According to him, "Don't think there's a thing called finishers anymore," a statement that echoes the changing dynamics of the game.

In an era where teams have access to an arsenal of skilled bowlers, the role of finishers is becoming increasingly diluted. With the advent of new bowlers like Jasprit Bumrah and Bhuvneshwar Kumar, the pressure on batsmen to score at a rapid rate has increased.

Fleming's warning serves as a timely reminder that the game has evolved, and teams need to adapt to stay ahead. As the IPL 2026 season unfolds, fans can expect a thrilling ride with teams pushing the boundaries of innovation and skill."
