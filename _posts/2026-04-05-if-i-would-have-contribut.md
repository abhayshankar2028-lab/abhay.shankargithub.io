![‘If I would have contributed more, maybe it could have been different’: CSK skipper Ruturaj Gaikwad after loss to RCB](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/Ruturaj-4.jpg)

In a post-match press conference, CSK skipper Ruturaj Gaikwad reflected on the loss to Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB). While he acknowledged the team's efforts, he expressed disappointment with the lack of contributions from his players. Gaikwad said, "If I had contributed more, maybe it could have been different." He emphasized the importance of teamwork and camaraderie, stating that "we are all together as a team, and we have to work together to achieve our goals." However, the team's inability to execute their plans and capitalize on opportunities was a significant factor in their loss. Gaikwad also praised RCB's batting lineup, saying, "RCB's bowling attack is one of the best in the league, and they played well." Despite the disappointment, the skipper remains optimistic about the team's prospects.
