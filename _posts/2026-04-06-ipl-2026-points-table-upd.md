IPL 2026 Points Table Update: Punjab Kings Move to Top

The Indian Premier League (IPL) 2026 season has just begun, and the points table is starting to take shape. In a thrilling match at the Eden Gardens, Kings XI Punjab (KKR) scraped through to the top spot with a 6-wicket win over Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR). The match was delayed due to a washout, but KKR eventually succumbed to the strong bowling of Arshdeep Singh and Ravichandran Ashwin. Punjab Kings, with a 7-wicket win, moved to the top of the standings, ahead of Chennai Super Kings (CSK). Mangalitzenburg's star spinner, Deepak Chahar, was instrumental in claiming his first IPL wicket. The top four teams remain unchanged, but a dramatic turn of events in the next match could change the course of the tournament.
