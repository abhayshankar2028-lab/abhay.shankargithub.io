![Ravi Kishan slams those calling Dhurandhar ‘propaganda’ films; Paresh Rawal says, ‘Mujhe hona chahiye tha ismein’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/ravi-kishan-Paresh-Rawal-review-dhurandhar.jpg)

In a recent statement, actor Ravi Kishan expressed his strong disappointment over the numerous reactions against the Bollywood film 'Dhurandhar'. He vehemently condemned those who call the film 'propaganda', stating that it is a bold and eye-opening portrayal of India's independence movement. Ravi Kishan's words are a stark contrast to those of actor Paresh Rawal, who recently expressed his support for the film, saying "Mujhe hona chahiye tha ismein" (I wish it had been there). Ravi Kishan's statement highlights the importance of artistic freedom and the right to express one's opinions, even if they differ from the mainstream consensus. The debate surrounding 'Dhurandhar' is a complex one, with some viewing it as a historical drama and others as propaganda. Ravi Kishan's stance serves as a reminder of the need to engage in respectful discussions and respect differing opinions.
