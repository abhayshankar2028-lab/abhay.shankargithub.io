In a recent interview, New Zealand cricket legend Stephen Fleming has called for patience towards the underwhelming Indian rookies. Fleming, who managed the Indian team during their brief stint in international cricket, believes that the young Indians are not yet ready to shine on the global stage. He stated, "I don't think the Indian team is ready to win a Test match or a Twenty20 World Cup just yet. They are still finding their feet, and I'm not saying that they can't be successful, but I do think they need to develop a bit more patience and understanding of the game before they can start to make an impact."

Fleming's comments come as the Indian team continues to struggle in international cricket, with limited success in Test matches and limited T20 World Cup victories.
