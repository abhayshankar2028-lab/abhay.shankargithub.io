Title: 'You knew a star was there': Kevin Pietersen recalls young Virat Kohli's curiosity during RCB days

In an interview with ESPN CricScore, former Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) captain Kevin Pietersen reminisced about the young Virat Kohli, who was just 17 at that time. Pietersen recalled Kohli's curiosity during the early days of RCB, saying, "You knew a star was there. He was always asking questions, always trying to improve himself."

Pietersen's words are a testament to Kohli's relentless work ethic, which has been the hallmark of his career. From practicing his swing to analyzing the game, Kohli's curiosity and passion for the sport were evident even at a young age. Pietersen's praise for Kohli's dedication is a fitting tribute to the young batsman's rise to stardom. Kohli's journey from RCB to India's Test team is a testament to his hard work and natural talent.
