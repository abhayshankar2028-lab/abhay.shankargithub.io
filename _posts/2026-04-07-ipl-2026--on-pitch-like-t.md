IPL 2026 is just around the corner, and the excitement is building up. In a recent interview, former India leg-spinner Anil Kumble shared his thoughts on KKR captain Ajinkya Rahane's decision to bat first in the rain. Kumble, known for his tactical prowess on the pitch, expressed his surprise at Rahane's choice. "On pitch like this, you'd want to field first," Kumble said. He believes that batting first in adverse weather conditions can be a smart move, as it allows the team to settle in and build a solid foundation. Kumble also praised Rahane's decision, saying that the batter's experience and composure under pressure were evident from the start. It's indeed a bold move, but it might pay off in the long run.
