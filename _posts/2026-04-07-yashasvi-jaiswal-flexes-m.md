In a thrilling display of athleticism, Yashasvi Jaiswal showcased his impressive muscular build as he hit a powerful six off Jasprit Bumrah, propelling Delhi to a top spot in the league table. The young opener's performance was, however, marred by a crucial wicket as he was trapped on the periphery of the boundary, narrowly avoiding a certain six. This was not Bumrah's day, as he took three wickets in the match, including the dismissals of openers Prithvi Shaw and Shreyas Iyer. Despite the loss, Jaiswal's muscular build and explosive batting style have earned him a spot in the Delhi lineup. His fireworks display will be a topic of discussion among cricket enthusiasts for a long time, showcasing his immense potential and electric presence at the crease.
