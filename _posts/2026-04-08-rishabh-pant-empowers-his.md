Rishabh Pant has been at the helm of the Delhi Capitals, and it's clear that he's taken his teammates under his wing. In an interview, LSG spin bowling coach, Anshul Thakur, praised Pant for his ability to "empower" his bowlers, pushing them to perform at their best. "Rishabh is a players-first captain," Thakur said. "He trusts his bowlers to deliver, and as a result, they've performed exceptionally well. His leadership is contagious, and it's inspiring to see his bowlers rise to the occasion." Thakur also praised Pant's ability to make split-second decisions on the field, often opting for the safer option rather than taking risks. Pant's leadership style is undoubtedly key to the Capitals' success this season, and it's clear that he's been instrumental in shaping his team's performance.
