In a recent interview, former Indian cricketer Sourav Ganguly expressed his opinion on bringing back pacer Mohammed Shami to the national team. Ganguly feels that Shami is in top form and should be given a chance to showcase his skills. "He looks fit and sharp," Ganguly said, highlighting the benefits of having Shami back in the lineup. The 32-year-old leg-spinner has been out of the team for several years due to an injury, but Ganguly believes he would be a great asset to the team. "I think he's ready to go back in," Ganguly stated. Many fans and pundits are also in favor of bringing Shami back, citing his impressive bowling statistics and ability to perform under pressure. With Ganguly's backing, it's likely that Mohammed Shami will be given a chance to regain his place in the Indian team.
