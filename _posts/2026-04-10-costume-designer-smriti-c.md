**Breaking Down Akshaye Khanna's Viral Moment: Smriti Chauhan's Costume Design Secrets**

In a recent interview, costume designer Smriti Chauhan took a closer look at Akshaye Khanna's infamous viral moment from the movie Dhurandhar. The scene, where Akshaye Khanna's character dons a bright orange jumpsuit, has become an instant meme. Smriti Chauhan revealed that she intentionally designed the jumpsuit to be bold and eye-catching, allowing the actor to make a statement through his attire.

Chauhan discussed the challenges of working on such a high-profile film, where every scene is meticulously planned and choreographed. "It's a delicate balance between creating a memorable scene and not overwhelming the audience," she explained. Smriti Chauhan's expert costume design played a significant role in making Akshaye Khanna's character stand out, and we can't wait to see what other projects she has in store for us.
