**Breaking Down the Timeless Appeal of Dhurandhar: An Exclusive Interview with Costume Designer Smriti Chauhan**

In a recent interview, costume designer Smriti Chauhan shed light on the making of Akshaye Khanna's viral moment in Dhurandhar. The film's energetic and quirky vibe was perfectly captured through Chauhan's meticulous costume design. "I wanted to create a character that was both nostalgic and modern," Chauhan explained. "Akshaye's character is a time capsule of the 90s, with a dash of 80s flair. I had to balance the vintage looks with the need to make it relatable to modern audiences."

Chauhan also discussed the challenges of working on Dhurandhar, which required her to balance multiple storylines and character arcs. "It was a huge project, but the cast and crew were amazing," she said. The result is a film that is both a love letter to the 90s and a commentary on the modern world.
