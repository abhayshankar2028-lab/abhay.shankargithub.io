![T Natarajan opens up on pain behind DC comeback ahead of IPL 2026 clash against CSK: ‘Injury always in my mind’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/T-NATRAJAN.jpg)

IPL 2026 is just around the corner, and the excitement is building up. In a recent interview, Indian spin bowler T Natarajan opened up about the pain he's been experiencing lately. The 33-year-old leg-spinner has been dealing with a knee injury, which has forced him to take a break from the game.

In his candid conversation, Natarajan revealed that the pain has been a constant companion, always lurking in the back of his mind. "Injury always in my mind," he confessed. "It's something that I have to deal with every time I step onto the field. But I'm working hard to get back to my best form." With the DC comeback on the horizon, Natarajan is eager to put his injury woes behind him and contribute to his team's success.
