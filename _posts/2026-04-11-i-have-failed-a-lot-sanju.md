![‘I have failed a lot’: Sanju Samson on his first CSK hundred, the Rajinikanth salute — and why it was all for Fleming](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/Sanju-11.jpg)

In an emotional interview, Sanju Samson opened up about his maiden fifty for Chennai Super Kings (CSK) in the 2022 IPL. The 32-year-old Indian batter expressed disappointment in not being able to contribute more to his team's success. However, what struck attention was the Rajinikanth salute he gave to his idol, M.S. Dhoni, in the post-match ceremony. Samson revealed that he had been inspired by Dhoni's work ethic and dedication, and wanted to pay homage to him. He mentioned that Fleming (Dhoni's former skipper) was the reason behind his salute. Samson hopes to have a better understanding of the game and his role in the team, following this moment of introspection. His humility and willingness to learn have left fans eager to see what the future holds for him.
