Kieron Pollard recently spoke about his thoughts on 16-year-old Indian cricketer Vaibhav Sooryavanshi. In an interview, the Jamaican all-rounder expressed his surprise at finding similarities between himself and the young Indian cricketer. Pollard, who is 32, reflected on his own formative years, saying, "At 15, I was still in school. Vaibhav is 16, so he's still in his school life." Pollard acknowledged that the young Indian cricketer's skills and determination reminded him of his own journey. The all-rounder praised Sooryavanshi's work ethic and adaptability, which he believes are essential qualities for success in the game. Pollard's comments suggest that he is rooting for the young Indian cricketer to make a lasting impact in the sport.
