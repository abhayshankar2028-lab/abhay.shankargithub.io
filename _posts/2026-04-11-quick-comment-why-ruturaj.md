**Ruturaj Gaikwad's T20 Batting Needs to Evolve**

As we approach the new T20 season, Ruturaj Gaikwad's impressive form in domestic cricket has left fans and pundits alike eager to see him regain his top spot as a T20 opener. However, his performances have been somewhat inconsistent in the format, and it's high time he evolves his game to truly excel. Gaikwad's strengths lie in his aggressive batting style, but he often struggles to deal with the pace and movement of T20 pitchers. To take his batting to the next level, he needs to work on his footwork, timing, and hand-eye coordination. He must also be more selective with his shots, opting for smarter, more refined ones that exploit the opposition's weaknesses. With patience, practice, and a willingness to adapt, Gaikwad can become an even more formidable T20 batsman.
