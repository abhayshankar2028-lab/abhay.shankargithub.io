In a dramatic turn of events, Sanju Samson's blistering century for Chennai Super Kings (CSK) at the Feroz Shah Kotla Ground in New Delhi left the cricketing world in awe. What made this moment even more special was Samson's gesture towards Rajinikanth, the film superstar, who was in attendance. As Samson was mobbed by fans, Rajinikanth, resplendent in his signature attire, stood and applauded, giving Samson a thunderous Padayappa salute. The crowd erupted in cheers, and even the on-field players couldn't help but join in on the excitement. This impromptu display of affection was more than just a gesture - it was a testament to the special bond between Samson and the "King" of Tamil cinema.
