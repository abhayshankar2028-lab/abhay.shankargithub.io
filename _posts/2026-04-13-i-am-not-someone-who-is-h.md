I am not someone who is happy sitting in the stands and waving: Abhishek Bachchan reveals why he never bought an IPL team. In a recent interview, Bollywood superstar Abhishek Bachchan opened up about his decision not to invest in the Indian Premier League (IPL). When asked why he never bought an IPL team, Bachchan stated, "I am not someone who is happy sitting in the stands and waving." He explained that he never had the passion or energy to be involved in the IPL in a significant way. "I don't have the time or the inclination to be involved in such a huge project," he said. Bachchan's words are a refreshing change from the usual fanboy antics, and he seems to be focusing on his acting career rather than getting entangled in the IPL drama.
