![‘I am not someone who is happy sitting in the stands and waving’: Abhishek Bachchan reveals why he never bought an IPL team](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/Abhishek-Bachchan.jpg)

I am not someone who is happy sitting in the stands and waving. As a Bollywood celebrity, I have always been fascinated by the world of cricket and the Indian Premier League. However, my experience with the IPL has been a bit different from what I expected. While I have attended several matches, I have never felt the urge to buy an IPL team. In fact, I have laughed off the idea of investing in a team, saying that I am not someone who is happy sitting in the stands and waving. I have always preferred to enjoy the game from the comfort of my own home, watching it with my family and friends. For me, it's not about being involved in the league, but about being a part of the excitement and the community that comes with it.
