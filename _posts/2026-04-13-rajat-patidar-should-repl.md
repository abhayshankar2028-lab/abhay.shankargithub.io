In the ongoing Indian Premier League (IPL), the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) secured a crucial 18-run victory over the Mumbai Indians (MI). While the win was a welcome one, many fans are left wondering if RCB's bowler, Rajat Patidar, should replace his teammate, Tilak Varma. Patidar has been in decent form, taking 4 wickets in 3 matches, but some fans feel that he has been all over the place. Varma, on the other hand, has been more consistent, taking 6 wickets in his last 4 matches. Kamran Akmal, a seasoned commentator, weighed in on the debate, saying, "Patidar has shown flashes of brilliance, but he needs to stabilize himself. Varma has been the backbone of MI's bowling attack, and I think he should be retained. RCB needs a consistent performer in the middle order, and Patidar is that player."
