In a candid conversation with a leading publication, none other than Bollywood superstar Abhishek Bachchan opened up about his unorthodox approach to cricket. The 43-year-old actor, who is a die-hard fan of the sport, revealed that he never bought an IPL team despite being a huge fan of the league. "I am not someone who is happy sitting in the stands and waving," he said with a chuckle. "I'm an active player, and to be honest, I never had the time or the inclination to invest in a team. I'd rather spend that money on my family or philanthropic causes. My love for cricket will always be with me, but I don't think it's fair to expect me to rain money down on the likes of Mumbai Indians or Delhi Capitals."
