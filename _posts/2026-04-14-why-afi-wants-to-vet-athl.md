**Protecting the Integrity of Cricket: AFI's Commitment to Athlete Wellbeing**

As the governing body of international cricket, the International Cricket Council (ICC) has been pushing for greater transparency and accountability in the way athletes' contracts are negotiated with sponsors. Former Australian First Class cricketer, AFI, has taken a leading role in championing this cause, stating that they want to 'vet' athlete contracts to ensure they align with the best interests of the player.

This means having a thorough review process to consider factors such as the individual's career trajectory, marketability, and potential for increased earnings. By doing so, AFI aims to prevent sponsors from exploiting athletes for financial gain, while also promoting a more sustainable and equitable model for the sport. As the ICC continues to advocate for athlete wellbeing, it's essential to recognize the importance of this initiative and support AFI's efforts to safeguard the interests of cricket's brightest stars.
