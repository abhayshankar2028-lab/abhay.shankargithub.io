![Ranbir Kapoor joins TIME 100 list; Ayushmann Khurrana says he’s a ‘storyteller talking to a global audience’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/Ranbir-Ramayana-1-1.jpg)

Ranbir Kapoor has made history by joining the TIME 100 list, solidifying his position as one of the most influential Indians on the global stage. The Dussehra-winning actor's impressive accomplishments have earned him the recognition he deserves. What's even more remarkable is that Kapoor has written a foreword for a special issue of TIME, highlighting the importance of storytelling in bridging cultural divides and connecting with people worldwide.

In an interview, Ayushmann Khurrana shared his thoughts on being a 'storyteller talking to a global audience'. He emphasized the power of storytelling in shaping our perspectives, challenging norms, and fostering empathy. Khurrana's words serve as a testament to Kapoor's remarkable impact on the entertainment industry and beyond. Kapoor's inclusion in TIME's 100 highlights his remarkable journey and the immense potential he holds for inspiring and educating a global audience.
