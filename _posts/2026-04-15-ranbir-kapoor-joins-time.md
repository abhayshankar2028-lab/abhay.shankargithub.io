Ranbir Kapoor is the latest star to join the esteemed TIME 100 list. As one of the most bankable stars in Bollywood, Kapoor's talent and versatility have earned him a spot among the world's most influential people. The list, which recognizes individuals who have made a significant impact on the world, is a testament to his dedication and hard work.

In an interview, Ayushmann Khurrana expressed his pride and gratitude towards joining the list. "I'm a storyteller talking to a global audience," he said. Kapoor, known for his critically acclaimed performances and versatility in various genres, is a perfect fit for this list. With his passion, creativity, and global appeal, Kapoor's inclusion on the TIME 100 list is a celebration of his achievements and contributions to the world of entertainment.
