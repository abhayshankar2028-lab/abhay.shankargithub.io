![Ranbir Kapoor joins TIME 100 list; Ayushmann Khurrana says he’s a ‘storyteller talking to a global audience’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/Ranbir-Ramayana-1-1.jpg)

Bollywood superstar Ranbir Kapoor has been in the spotlight lately with his rising stardom in films like 'Brahmastra'. The actor has now been featured on TIME Magazine's 100 most influential people list. In an exclusive interview, Kapoor expressed his gratitude and said he is "thrilled" to be included in the list. When asked about his journey, Kapoor mentioned that he is a storyteller who talks to a global audience. He believes that his work transcends boundaries and connects people from different walks of life. Kapoor also highlighted the importance of his father, Raj Kapoor, in shaping his career. With his captivating performances and storytelling skills, Kapoor has undoubtedly cemented his place in the entertainment industry. His inclusion in the TIME 100 list is a testament to his hard work and dedication to his craft.
