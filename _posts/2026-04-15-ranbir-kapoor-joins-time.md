![Ranbir Kapoor joins TIME 100 list; Ayushmann Khurrana says he’s a ‘storyteller talking to a global audience’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/Ranbir-Ramayana-1-1.jpg)

Ranbir Kapoor, the talented actor, has made a significant milestone in his career. He has just been included in TIME Magazine's 100 most influential people list. This is a testament to his immense contribution to the Indian film industry and beyond. When asked about his achievement, Ranbir expressed his gratitude and humility. He stated, "It's a great honor to be included in this list. I feel blessed to be part of a global community that understands my passion for storytelling." Ayushmann Khurrana, a fellow Bollywood actor, recently shared his thoughts on being a 'storyteller talking to a global audience'. He emphasized the importance of his craft in conveying messages to a diverse audience worldwide. Both Ranbir and Ayushmann are known for their captivating performances and are widely respected in the industry.
