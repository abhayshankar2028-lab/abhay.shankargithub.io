**Exclusive Update: Bodyguard Salaries in Bollywood**

In a recent development, the buzz surrounding Salman Khan's bodyguard Shera and Shah Rukh Khan's ex-security chief Ravi has taken an interesting turn. Sources close to the matter revealed that both the actors' bodyguards are reportedly earning an exorbitant salary, with some reports suggesting that Shera's salary is around Rs 10 crores per year. However, claims of Ravi's ex security chief debunking these claims by stating that "no one pays more than Rs 10 lakhs" for his services have sparked a lot of interest. When questioned about the discrepancy, Ravi's ex-security chief simply smiled and said, "Let's just say that everyone's salary is different." It's unclear what prompted these claims, but one thing is for sure - the salaries of these bodyguards are indeed a topic of discussion in the Bollywood fraternity.
