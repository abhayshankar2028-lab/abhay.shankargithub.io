![‘Rishabh Pant getting himself out more often than opposition bowlers’: Sidhu says LSG need ‘a lot of soul searching’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/pant-lsg-ap.jpg)

In his latest press conference, Indian captain MS Dhoni mentioned that the Royals Guwahati (LSG) need to focus on their fielding and bowling skills. Rishabh Pant, the young and talented wicket-keeper, has been getting a lot of attention for his fielding skills, but not as many dismissals as the opposition bowlers. Dhoni stated that Rishabh Pant getting himself out more often than the opposition bowlers is a concern for the team. He also mentioned that the team needs to 'lose their emotional game' and focus on the game plan. The captain further emphasized that LSG needs to 'search within' for solutions to their problems. These comments from Dhoni suggest that the team is looking to Rishabh Pant to be more decisive in his dismissals and that the team needs to work on their overall strategy.
