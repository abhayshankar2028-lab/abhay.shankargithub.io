**A Backlash to the Angry Young Man Era: Akshay Kumar's Lament**

In a recent interview, Bollywood superstar Akshay Kumar paid tribute to two iconic actors - Animal and Dhurandhar - who were part of the iconic film "Dhoom". The veteran actor expressed his admiration for the duo, calling them "artists" and stating that their performances in "Dhoom" brought back the "angry young man" era. "The film brought back the whole angry young man era," Akshay exclaimed. "Animal was a true artist, and Dhurandhar was a master of his craft. Their chemistry on screen was electric, and it's a wonder that their films have stood the test of time. Their legacy continues to inspire new generations of actors and filmmakers." The nod to the "angry young man" era is a fitting tribute to two filmmakers who left an indelible mark on Bollywood.
