![New Zealand beats Bangladesh by 26 runs in first ODI](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/bangladesh-vs-new-zealand.jpg)

In a highly anticipated encounter, New Zealand secured a thrilling 26-run victory over Bangladesh in the first One Day International (ODI) at Eden Park in Auckland, New Zealand. The home team batted first, with Ross Taylor scoring a crucial 78 runs, while Kane Williamson added 60 runs of his own. Bangladesh's star player, Shakib Al Hasan, played a significant role in their innings, scoring 57 runs. However, the Kiwis' bowlers performed admirably, dismissing Bangladesh's batters at crucial intervals. New Zealand's captain, Kane Williamson, led his team to a convincing win, securing their second win in as many ODI matches. With this victory, New Zealand is looking to continue their winning streak in the series. The match was played in front of a packed crowd, with New Zealand's fans creating an electric atmosphere.
