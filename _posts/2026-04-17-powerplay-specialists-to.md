**Powerplay Specialists to Jasprit Bumrah: Where it's Going Wrong for Mumbai Indians**

As the Mumbai Indians continue to struggle in the Indian Premier League (IPL), their powerplay specialists, particularly Jasprit Bumrah, have been under immense pressure. While Bumrah has consistently delivered impressive performances in the powerplay, the team's overall season has been marred by inconsistency.

The key issue lies in the powerplay's inability to capitalize on India's aggressive batting line-up. The Indians have been able to score crucial runs in the powerplay, but their fielding has been inconsistent, leading to several missed opportunities. Additionally, Bumrah's yorkers have been falling short of their intended length, allowing the Indians to build strong partnerships. As the powerplay approaches, it's high time for the Mumbai Indians to reassess their strategy and get their powerplay specialists back on track.
