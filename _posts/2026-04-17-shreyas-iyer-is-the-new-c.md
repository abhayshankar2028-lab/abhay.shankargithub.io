In a comparison of the current and past Indian openers, former India opener Shreyas Iyer has likened the batting of Rajasthan Royals' skipper Rajeev Shrivastava (not PBKS) to that of Virat Kohli. Iyer, who made his T20 and first-class debut for Royals, has been in scintillating form lately, scoring crucial runs against top teams. His ability to play the game with both hands and his adaptability to different conditions have earned him a spot in the Royals' hierarchy. Shrivastava, on the other hand, has been under the spotlight for his contrasting form, struggling to find his groove. Iyer's comments on Shrivastava's batting remind us of Kohli's incredible abilities, who has been a benchmark for Indian openers for years. It's worth noting that Royals are not a top-tier team, and Iyer's assessment might not reflect the team's actual performance.
