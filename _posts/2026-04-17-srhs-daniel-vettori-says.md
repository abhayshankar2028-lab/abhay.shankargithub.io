In a recent development, former New Zealand cricketer Daniel Vettori has spoken out about his experience playing in the Indian Premier League (IPL). During the auction, Vettori expressed his desire to have 10 spinners in the league. However, due to a lack of suitable players, he was limited to just five. Vettori stated that the lack of quality spinners was a major factor in his decision to leave the IPL in 2021. He also praised the quality of the spinners he did have, saying that they were among the best he had ever faced. Despite this, Vettori's experience in the IPL was a significant factor in his decision to leave the league. He has since joined the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) for the 2023 IPL season.
