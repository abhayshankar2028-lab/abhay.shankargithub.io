In his recent stint with the Durham Lions, Glenn Phillips has been a revelation in the outfield, showcasing his impressive fielding skills and ability to read the game. While discussing the current state of the New Zealand pace bowling department, Phillips made a bold statement: "We may want to back our pace bowlers more." This sentiment is not new, but Phillips' assertion has sparked a necessary conversation. The key to unlocking the full potential of the pace bowlers lies in their ability to score runs. When they are able to do so, the opposition's bowling attack becomes much more formidable. Phillips and his teammates need to work on scoring runs to make their bowlers more effective. With the right mindset and a focus on creating a conducive batting environment, the pace bowlers might just find themselves scoring more runs and leading to a more balanced team performance.
