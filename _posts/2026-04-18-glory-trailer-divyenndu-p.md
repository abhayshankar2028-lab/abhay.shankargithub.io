The highly anticipated trailer for Netflix's upcoming sports drama, "Glory", has finally arrived, and it's packed with action, drama, and revenge. The movie, directed by Vishnu Bapat, stars none other than the dashing Divyenndhu Mishra and the charming Pulkit Samrat.

In "Glory", Divyenndhu plays a former footballer who is wrongly accused of murder and seeks revenge against the people who betrayed him. With the help of his childhood friend (Pulkit Samrat), Divyenndhu sets out to clear his name and take down the corrupt.
