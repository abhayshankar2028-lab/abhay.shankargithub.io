In a nail-biting IPL 2026 contest, Mumbai Indians (MI) defeated Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) by 10 runs. The match was a highly anticipated encounter, with both teams pushing each other to the limit. However, it was Mumbai Indians who managed to wait it out, capitalizing on the momentum to seal the deal. Malinga, the senior all-rounder, played a crucial role in the victory, bagging 3 wickets and scoring 25 runs. Sakib, on the other hand, batted valiantly, scoring 38 runs off 30 deliveries. Despite his efforts, Sakib was unable to break the back of the runs, ultimately allowing MI to secure a comfortable victory. The win propelled MI to the top of the IPL points table, while SRH dropped to third place on the table. A thrilling encounter that will be remembered for a long time.
