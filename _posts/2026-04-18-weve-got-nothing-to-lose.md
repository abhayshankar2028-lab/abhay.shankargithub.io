![‘We’ve got nothing to lose’: KKR’s Ajinkya Rahane on playing next matches after GT loss](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/Rahane-19.jpg)

In an intense post-match press conference, Ajinkya Rahane expressed his emotions after the Indian Premier League (IPL) Qualifier 2 loss to Gujarat Titans. Despite the heartbreaking defeat, Rahane remained optimistic, stating that they "don't have a lot to lose." He emphasized that the team's hard work and dedication have been paying off, and they are just one step away from the knockout stages. Rahane acknowledged that the loss was a tough pill to swallow, but he remained focused on the present moment. "We played our hearts out," he said. "We gave it our all, and that's all we can do. We'll bounce back from this loss and come stronger in the next matches." With a positive attitude, Rahane is looking forward to the challenges ahead, vowing to give it his all in the next contest.
