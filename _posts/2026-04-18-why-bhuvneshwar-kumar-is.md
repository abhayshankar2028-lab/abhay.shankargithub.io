Bhuvneshwar Kumar, the pace bowling stalwart of the Indian cricket team, has solidified his position as the IPL's undisputed powerplay king. With an impressive array of variations, he has consistently impressed fans and pundits alike with his ability to unsettle batsmen in the powerplay overs. His most notable achievement in this regard is his ability to extract wickets from the opposition's best batsmen.

Kumar has a reputation for being one of the most effective bowlers in the IPL, and his ability to rotate the ball has been particularly notable. His ability to bowl yorkers and changing the pace has made him a formidable force in the IPL powerplay stands. With a career-best 30 wickets in 15 matches at an average of 20.66, Kumar's impressive statistics have cemented his status as the IPL's king of the powerplay.
