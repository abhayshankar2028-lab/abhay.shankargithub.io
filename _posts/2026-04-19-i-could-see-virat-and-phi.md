![‘I could see Virat and Phil Salt not happy with the ball…’: KL Rahul on Chinnaswamy pitch](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/kl-kohli.jpg)

In a recent match, KL Rahul expressed his frustration with the ball's bounce at the Chinnaswamy Stadium. The Indian batter felt that the ball kept bouncing higher than expected, making it difficult for him to control the game. 

When asked about the issue, Rahul stated, "I could see Virat and Phil Salt not happy with the ball... It's like they're not getting the bounce they're expecting. It's frustrating, you know?" Rahul's teammate Phil Salt also shared a similar sentiment, stating that the ball was too high for them. 

The lack of bounce in the ball has affected the batting lineup, and Rahul feels that it's putting pressure on the team. The umpires have been called in to review the ball's bounce, and it's likely that they will make necessary adjustments to ensure that the ball behaves as expected.
