IPL 2026: Punjab Kings' star-studded lineup continued to defy all expectations as they cruised to a massive 8-wicket victory over their arch-rivals, the Lucknow Super Giants. Arya and Connolly, the dynamic duo of Punjab Kings, put on a show, each scoring six crucial sixes to keep their team unbeaten. The opening batsman, Arya, took a record-breaking 11 sixes in the match, while Connolly chipped in with three more. The Super Giants' bowlers, however, struggled to contain the duo, conceding a whopping 45 runs in 10 overs. Despite a valiant effort from their captain, Hardik Pandya, the Super Giants ultimately fell short. Punjab Kings' unbeaten streak now stands at five matches, with their next game coming up against the Chennai Super Kings.
