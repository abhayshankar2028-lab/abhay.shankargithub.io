'I will never ask her' - a quote that has left us all wondering about Abhishek Bachchan's thoughts on his daughter Aaradhya's opinion on his films. The father-son duo, known for their movie magic, has been a source of inspiration for many fans. Recently, we caught up with Abhishek and we asked him about Aaradhya's thoughts on his films. His response was simple yet profound, "I will never ask her". When we asked him what we could ask her, he said, "She is my daughter, I will never ask her". This statement stands for the love and respect he has for his daughter, and it's a testament to the bond they share. It's clear that Abhishek values his daughter's opinion above all else, and we can't blame him.
