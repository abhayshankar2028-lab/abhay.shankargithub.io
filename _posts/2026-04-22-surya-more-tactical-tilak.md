![‘Surya more tactical’: Tilak Varma on the differences between playing under Suryakumar Yadav and Hardik Pandya](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/tilak-varma.jpg)

In a recent interview, Tilak Varma, the Indian cricket team's vice-captain, praised Suryakumar Yadav as a more tactical player compared to Hardik Pandya. Varma highlighted the difference in their approach to the game. While Pandya is known for his aggressive and attacking style, Yadav's game is more defensive and calculated. "Suryakumar is a more tactical player," Varma said. "He understands the game better and makes more sense of the situation. He's not afraid to play his natural game, but he also knows when to adjust and play more defensively." This is evident in his performances in the limited-overs format, where he often plays a more conservative game. Varma's endorsement of Yadav's tactical approach has led to a renewed appreciation for the Indian captain's game.
