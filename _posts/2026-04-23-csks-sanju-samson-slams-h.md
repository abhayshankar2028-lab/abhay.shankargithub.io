CSK's Sanju Samson has been in phenomenal form this season, with two stunning centuries under his belt. In the recently concluded match against MI at the Wankhede stadium, Samson once again delivered a breathtaking innings. He smashed a blistering 100 off just 57 deliveries, taking his tally to 5 IPL centuries overall. The Indian opener's century was a masterclass of batting, with his footwork and shot-making skills on full display. His partnership with Harishankar Durant helped CSK to post a formidable score of 226/4, which MI was unable to even get close to. Samson's 100 was a highlight of the match, and his 5 IPL centuries now put him at the top of the charts. His batting form continues to be a key factor in CSK's success this season.
