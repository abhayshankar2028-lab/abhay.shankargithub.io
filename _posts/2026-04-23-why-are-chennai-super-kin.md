IPL fans, get ready for a shocking sight - Chennai Super Kings players are continuing to wear black armbands in their matches against Mumbai Indians. This unusual trend started a few weeks ago and has been a topic of discussion among cricket enthusiasts.

The black armbands are a subtle nod to the team's predecessor, the Chennai Super Kings, which folded in 2021. By wearing the armbands, the current team is paying tribute to their predecessors and maintaining a sense of continuity.

However, some fans have taken to social media to express their disappointment and frustration with the decision. "It's a cheap attempt to draw attention to the team," one fan tweeted. Another fan replied, "It's just a bunch of players showing off their pride. What's wrong with wearing a simple red or blue armband like every other team?" As the IPL season continues, it remains to be seen if the black armbands will become a regular feature of the Chennai Super Kings' uniform.
