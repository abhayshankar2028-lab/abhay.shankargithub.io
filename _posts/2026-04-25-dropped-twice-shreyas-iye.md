In a thrilling encounter, Shreyas Iyer led the Punjab Kings to a record-breaking chase, leaving the Delhi Capitals reeling. The 31-year-old Indian batsman, who has been in phenomenal form, was dismissed twice but still managed to guide his team to a convincing victory.

Iyer's determination and leadership were on display as he played a crucial role in the Punjab Kings' triumph. Despite being bowled twice, he refused to give up and instead, used his experience to steady the innings. His partnership with his openers, Shikhar Dhawan and Rajarsi Dixit, was instrumental in setting up the record-breaking chase.

The Punjab Kings' victory marked a significant win for the franchise, who will look to build on this momentum in the remaining matches. Iyer's performance will be remembered as one of the most impressive ones in recent times, and he will surely be in the mix for the Indian cricket team's World Cup squad.
