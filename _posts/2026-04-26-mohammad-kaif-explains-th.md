![Mohammad Kaif explains the malaise of missed catches in IPL: ‘It’s a tradition of every year’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/Nair-miss.jpg)

In a recent interview, former Indian cricketer Mohammad Kaif expressed his frustration with the missed catches in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The 39-year-old legend believes that the tradition of missed catches has become a norm in the league, rather than a rare occurrence. Kaif, who played for several teams in the IPL, stated that the lack of fielders in the outfield has contributed to the high number of missed catches. "It's a tradition of every year," Kaif said. "We have not changed our fielding system enough. The same thing happens every year, and it's frustrating to watch." Kaif's comments highlight the need for the IPL management to review and improve the fielding strategy to minimize the number of missed catches.
