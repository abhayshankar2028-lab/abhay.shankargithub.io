In a candid conversation with Indian cricket fans, former India cricketer MS Dhoni expressed his admiration for KL Rahul's intent debate against Virat Kohli. When asked about Rahul's willingness to take on Kohli in the written test match, Dhoni said, "When you can play like that, when you can think on your feet and take on the best in the world, that's when the real cricketer emerges." Dhoni commended Rahul's mental toughness and ability to adapt to different situations, stating that it's a quality that sets great cricketers apart. The former captain's words are a testament to Rahul's growth as a batsman and his capacity to perform under pressure. Dhoni's endorsement of Rahul's intent and mental strength has been a welcome boost to the young opener, who has been in the spotlight for his vocal debates with Kohli.
