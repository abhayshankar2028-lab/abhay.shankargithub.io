In a thrilling match of the Indian Premier League, Delhi cricket team found themselves in a dire situation. With their lowest powerplay score yet, Delhi was on the brink of elimination. However, a stunning comeback from the bowling duo of Jasprit Bumrah and Avesh Khada, led by the explosive presence of Josh Hazlewood and Bhuvneshwar Kumar, proved to be too much for the Delhi team.

The pair reduced Delhi's score to 34/8, with Bumrah taking three wickets and Kumar picking two. Despite a valiant effort from Delhi captain, Shikhar Dhawan, and the rest of the team, the Delhi team was left to ponder what could have been. Kohli, however, stole the show, scoring an unbeatable 74 not out, as Delhi managed to post a respectable 218/8 in their innings. Kohli's heroics once again stole the limelight, leaving Delhi to wonder what might have been.
