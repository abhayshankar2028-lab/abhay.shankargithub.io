In a stunning turn of events, Michael Hussey's impressive batting, and the decision to bowl first by the Delhi Capitals, completely overshadowed the Indian bowling duo of Jasprit Bumrah and Bhuvneshwar Kumble. The Delhi Capitals started strongly, with captain Simon Katwarra eventually being dismissed for a meek 25. However, it was the bowling duo that stole the show. Bhuvneshwar Kumble and Jasprit Bumrah duo took 3 wickets in just 6.4 overs, reducing the total by 107 runs. This paired with Michael Hussey's aggressive batting, reduced Delhi's lowest powerplay score to 135/6. The bowling duo's performance proved to be the turning point in the match. As a result, Delhi were bowled out for 138 runs, with the batting duo putting up a valiant effort. It was a masterclass performance from the Indian bowlers, while Hussey stole the show with his blistering batting.
