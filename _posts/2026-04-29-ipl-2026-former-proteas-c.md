IPL 2026: Former Proteas Captain Blames Mumbai Indians Form on Three Key Bowlers

The 2026 Indian Premier League has officially kicked off, and the Mumbai Indians have been making waves with their impressive performances. However, former Proteas captain Faf du Plessis has come forward to express his disappointment with the team's batting line-up.

Du Plessis attributed the Mumbai Indians' poor batting to their ineffective bowling units. "The key bowlers for Mumbai Indians are Jasprit Bumrah, Rashid Khan, and Hardik Pandya," he stated. "They have been consistently producing pressure on our batsmen, and it's a significant factor in their poor batting last season."

The former Proteas captain's sentiments have sparked a lively debate among cricket fans, with some defending the Mumbai Indians' bowling prowess and others blaming the team's batting issues.
