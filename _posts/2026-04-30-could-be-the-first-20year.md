In a remarkable rise, Indian cricketer Vaibhav Sooryavanshi could be set to sign his first 20-year contract with the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB). The talented young all-rounder has been impressing fans and teammates alike with his outstanding performances. RCB's batting coach, Jos Buttler, has lauded Sooryavanshi's skills, stating that he could be a game-changer for the team. Meanwhile, RCB's fast bowler, Stuart Broad, has also expressed his admiration for the young player, calling him a "bright future". Sooryavanshi, who made his T20 debut just last year, has been a key player for RCB in the Indian Premier League. Could this be a turning point for Sooryavanshi's career, marking his first major contract? Only time will tell.
