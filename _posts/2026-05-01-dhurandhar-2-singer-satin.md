**Satinder Sartaj Praises Ranveer Singh, Dedicates Dhurandhar 2**

In an exclusive interview, singer Satinder Sartaj expressed his admiration for actor Ranveer Singh. The singer praised the actor's dedication to his craft, stating that "Ranveer Singh is not just an actor, he's a perfectionist. He puts in the toughest work and it shows in his performances."

Sartaj also paid tribute to composer Aditya Dhar, who composed the music for Dhurandhar 2. "Aditya Dhar's music is like a symphony. It complements the song and makes it even more beautiful," said Sartaj. The singer's praise for both Ranveer Singh and Aditya Dhar is a testament to the hard work and dedication that goes into creating hit Bollywood songs.
