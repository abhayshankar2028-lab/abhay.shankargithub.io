title: "Dhurandhar 2 singer Satinder Sartaj praises Ranveer Singh, salutes Aditya Dhar: ‘Tough work’"
---

"Satinder Sartaj has expressed his heartfelt admiration for Bollywood actor Ranveer Singh, highlighting his exceptional acting skills and dedication. In a recent interview, Sartaj praised Singh for his "tough work" and praised the entire team behind his movie 'Dhurandhar 2.' Sartaj also expressed gratitude towards music director Aditya Dhar for creating the captivating soundtracks for the film. 'Ranveer's performance in his role is truly remarkable, he has given his all to the film,' Sartaj said. He also praised the entire team, including the producers and the cast, for their hard work and dedication. The praise from Sartaj is a testament to the hard work and dedication that goes into making a successful Bollywood movie. 'Dhurandhar 2' has indeed been a huge success, and it's clear that the entire team has put in their heart and soul to make it happen.'
