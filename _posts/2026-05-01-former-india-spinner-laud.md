![Former India spinner lauds Shubman Gill’s Gujarat Titans captaincy, batting against Royal Challengers Bengaluru](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/05/Shubman-Gill-6.jpg)

In a recent interview, former Indian spinner Anil Kumble praised Gujarat Titans skipper Shubman Gill's exceptional captaincy skills. Kumble, who played 132 Tests for India, commended Gill's ability to bring about a sense of calm and stability amidst pressure. "Shubman is an extremely capable captain. He's one of the best I've seen, and I think he has a lot of potential for further success," Kumble said. Kumble specifically praised Gill's leadership during the Royal Challengers Bengaluru match. "He's a great leader and a very organized captain. He's got the right blend of experience, youth, and energy, and that makes him an ideal fit for the Gujarat Titans." With Gill at the helm, the Titans seem poised for a strong campaign.
