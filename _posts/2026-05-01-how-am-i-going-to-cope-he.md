**A Heartfelt Tribute to the Dimaagri Dada**

In a poignant moment of vulnerability, Hema Malini has opened up about her struggle to cope with the loss of her iconic co-star and husband, Dharmendra. The actress took to social media to share a heartfelt tribute, stating that Dharmendra's passing has left her feeling "lost and heartbroken". She expressed her gratitude for the memories they shared, and the love that only Dharmendra could provide. "His laughter, his smile, his love... everything about him will always be with me," she said. The actress also acknowledged the pain and struggle of coping with grief, stating that it's a difficult but ultimately rewarding process. Hema Malini's tribute has touched the hearts of many, and serves as a reminder of the enduring love and legacy of these two beloved Bollywood legends.
