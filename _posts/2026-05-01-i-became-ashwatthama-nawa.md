"I Brought Ashwatthama to Life: An Unforgettable NSD Holi Experience," - Nawazuddin Siddiqui

I still remember the day NSD (Nawazuddin Siddiqui) brought Ashwatthama to life during the Holi celebrations. It was a magical night that I'll cherish forever. He recreated the iconic scenes from the epic Mahabharata, immersing us in the world of ancient India. The way he effortlessly brought the character to life, making us believe he was indeed immortal, was nothing short of awe-inspiring.

As the bhang made him invisible, he careened through the streets, dodging our attempts to catch him. The crowd roared with laughter, and I was thrilled to be a part of it. It was a night to remember, a testament to Nawazuddin's incredible talent and passion for his craft.
