I've got the latest scoop on the music industry, and it's all about the talented Dhurandhar 2 singer Satinder Sartaj! In an exclusive interview, Satinder Sartaj expressed his gratitude and admiration for Ranveer Singh, the lead actor in the film. He praised Singh's hard work and dedication, crediting him for bringing a new energy to the film. "Ranveer Singh's tough work ethic is an inspiration to us all," Satinder Sartaj gushed. The singer also paid tribute to Aditya Dhar, the director of the film, calling him a masterful storyteller. "Aditya Dhar has crafted a story that will touch the hearts of audiences worldwide," Satinder Sartaj said. With his unique voice and heartfelt praise, Satinder Sartaj is undoubtedly a force to be reckoned with in the music industry.
