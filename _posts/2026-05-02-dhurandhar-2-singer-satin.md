title: "Dhurandhar 2 singer Satinder Sartaj praises Ranveer Singh, salutes Aditya Dhar: ‘Tough work’"

I'm thrilled to share that music legend Dhurandhar 2 singer Satinder Sartaj has been spotted in the company of Bollywood superstar Ranveer Singh at a concert. And what better way to pay tribute to the man who poured his heart and soul into this song than to reveal his admiration for the talented actor? Satinder Sartaj took to social media to express his gratitude, calling Ranveer Singh 'the real deal' and complimenting Aditya Dhar, the mastermind behind the song's creation. It's clear that Satinder Sartaj values the hard work and dedication that went into crafting Dhurandhar 2, and his praise for Ranveer Singh and Aditya Dhar is a testament to the power of music in bringing people together. A true celebration of music and its magic.
