![Experts point to overusage of slower balls from Jasprit Bumrah as reason behind his poor form: ‘gone over the top’](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/05/bumrah.jpg)

The Indian cricketer Jasprit Bumrah has experienced a significant decline in his bowling performance in the past few matches. Experts point to the excessive use of slower balls in his delivery as a major cause. Bumrah's high-speed deliveries have been clocking at speeds above 90 km/h, which can be detrimental to batsmen. According to cricket experts, the excessive use of slower balls has made Bumrah's batsmen accustomed to the high speed, leading to a decrease in their overall ability to withstand the pace.

As a result, batsmen are now taking risks by stepping up to the crease early, which in turn has led to Bumrah's poorer performances. The experts believe that Bumrah needs to find a balance between delivering fast and accurate deliveries to regain his form.
