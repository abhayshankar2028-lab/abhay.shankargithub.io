In a thrilling game, Ian Bishop reflected on the standout performance of Mitchell Starc, who returned to the Delhi Capitals lineup with thumping results. "He brought some firepower for them, which was exactly what they needed," Bishop praised. Starc, who faced opposition from some of the best batters in the league, showed incredible skill and variation in his bowling. He bowled a perfect game in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and took 6 wickets for 12 runs in 18 overs. Bishop highlighted Starc's ability to knock out batsmen with his pace and precision, making him an invaluable asset for the Capitals. His return to form has been a turning point for the team, and fans are eagerly anticipating his next outing. With his incredible bowling skills, Starc is undoubtedly a key player for Delhi Capitals.
