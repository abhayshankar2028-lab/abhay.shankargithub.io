I Meditated 24/7 for Two Years: India's First VJ Ruby Bhatia Opens About Her Spiritual Journey

In a profound revelation, Indian television VJ Ruby Bhatia spoke about her extraordinary decision to leave behind her lucrative career as a VJ to dedicate herself to her spiritual journey. Bhatia, who rose to fame in the early 2000s with her popular show "Tez Mazaa" on MTV India, announced her departure from the industry a year ago. After two years of introspection and self-discovery, Bhatia has found solace in meditation and yoga. "I meditated 24/7 for two years," she shared, "and discovered a deeper sense of purpose and meaning. I'm no longer chasing fame; I'm on a spiritual path that's guiding me forward." Bhatia's courage and conviction have inspired millions, and her story serves as a testament to the power of self-discovery and living a life of purpose.
