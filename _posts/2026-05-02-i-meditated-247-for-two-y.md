"I Meditated 24/7 for Two Years: India's First VJ Ruby Bhatia Opens Up on Leaving Fame Behind"

In a powerful statement, Bollywood's first-ever VJ Ruby Bhatia has left the spotlight behind to pursue spirituality. Her journey began two years ago when she dedicated her life to meditation and yoga. The 32-year-old, who rose to fame after competing in the third season of Bigg Boss, is now using her platform to share her experiences and connect with others seeking inner peace.

"I wanted to leave the fame behind and focus on what truly matters to me," Ruby shared in an exclusive interview. "Meditation has been a game-changer for me. It has helped me calm my mind and connect with my inner self. I'm not saying I've achieved enlightenment, but I feel more connected to myself and others. I'm excited to see where this journey takes me and how I can inspire others to do the same."
