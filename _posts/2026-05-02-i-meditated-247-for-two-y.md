I meditated 24/7 for two years, a journey that changed the course of my life. It was a defining moment that forced me to question my priorities. As a popular VJ in India, my days were dominated by work, fame, and fortune. But I felt suffocated by the constant scrutiny and pressure. That's when I made the bold decision to leave it all behind and focus on my inner self.

I meditated 24/7 for two years, surrendering to the stillness and quiet that lies within. It was tough, but it was also liberating. I discovered a sense of peace and clarity that I never knew was possible. Today, I'm living a more authentic life, one that's not governed by the demands of fame. My journey is a testament to the power of introspection and self-discovery. I'm living a more meaningful, purpose-driven life.
