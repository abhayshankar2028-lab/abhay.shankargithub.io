I wasn't desperate to shake hands either: Salman Ali Agha on handshake snub

Former Pakistani cricketer Salman Ali Agha has sparked controversy by his reaction to a handshake incident with Indian cricketer Ravichandran Ashwin. In a recent interview, Agha was asked about the incident where he refused to shake hands with Ashwin, prompting criticism and debate among fans and cricketers alike. When asked directly about the incident, Agha stated, "I wasn't desperate to shake hands either."

The incident sparked a heated debate, with some accusing Agha of being defensive and others defending his actions. When questioned about his stance, Agha refused to elaborate, instead focusing on the incident itself. His response has left many wondering about his motivations behind the incident.
