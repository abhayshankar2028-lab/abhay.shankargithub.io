I'm not getting married, having kids': Malaika Arora breaks silence on family plans. In an exclusive interview with a popular entertainment magazine, Malaika Arora opened up about her family plans. The Bollywood beauty revealed that her son Arhaan, who is seven years her junior, has expressed his wishes of not having children. Arhaan asked Malaika not to plan for grandkids, saying "forget about grandkids". Malaika responded with a gentle yet firm tone, saying that she was on the verge of having children and doesn't want to disappoint her family and fans. "I'm not getting married, having kids," she said. The unexpected revelation has left fans eagerly waiting for Malaika's future plans.
