The India Women's Cricket Team has announced the 15-member squad for the upcoming ICC World T20 Asia Cup 2027. While Harleen Deol, the experienced wicket-keeper, has been left out of the squad, Amanjot Kaur, the talented all-rounder, has been replaced by Priya Kushwara. The team, led by captain Smriti Mandhana, boasts an impressive lineup that includes seasoned players like Smriti, Jhulan Goswami, and Mithali Raj. The squad also features promising youngsters like Ritu Kaur and Sana Mirza. With the World T20 Asia Cup just around the corner, India is expected to put in a strong performance. This squad is a testament to the team's rebuilding efforts and its commitment to becoming a stronger unit.
