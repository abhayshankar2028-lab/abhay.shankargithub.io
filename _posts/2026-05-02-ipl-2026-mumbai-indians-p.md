IPL 2026 preview: Mumbai Indians look to salvage playoff hopes after thrilling loss to Chennai Super Kings. The Mumbai Indians' play-off push has been all but over since their stunning 8-wicket win over Chennai Super Kings in the 5th match of the IPL 2026. Indian pacer Ahsan Malik and all-rounder Suryakumar Yadav put on a show with Bharat's highest individual score of 66, while Kolkata's Sunil Narine and Mumbai's Kieron Pollard sealed the win with a 3-wicket haul.

The win has snapped Mumbai's hopes of making it to the playoffs, but the team can still salvage a spot with a win in their next match. The Indians will now focus on bowling out the remaining teams to secure a playoff spot. The win has also boosted Sunil Narine's batting form, and he is now expected to be a key player in the team's future matches.
