But, let's not forget, they have a decent T20 team that has consistently performed well. With players like Ambati Rayudu, Kieron Pollard, and Hardik Pandya, they have a mix of experience and talent that can take them to the top.

Despite their names, Mumbai Indians have shown that they can compete against the best teams in the league. They have a strong trading card market and a passionate fan base that supports them through thick and thin. While their names might not be the most comfortable to chant, they are certainly memorable. The Mumbai Indians name is a reflection of their determination and grit, and their T20 team is just a part of what makes them stand out.
