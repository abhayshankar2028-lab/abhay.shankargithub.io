**Classical Test bowlers vs Resilient Swing Attack**

In a thrilling match, Washington Sundar steered Gujarat Titans to a resounding 8-wicket victory over Rajasthan Royals at the Feroz Shah Kotla Stadium. The Indian pacer's composed innings was the deciding factor in the match, as he restricted Royals' innings with his economical bowling.

Sundar, often regarded as one of the best left-arm fast bowlers in the world, put on a show, dismissing Royals' top order with ease. His 2-32 figures and 4 wickets at just 12.5 runs apiece were instrumental in GT's victory.

While the Royals' attack was resolute, Sundar's ability to control the game with his spin was the backbone of his innings. His ability to bowl at a decent economy rate without conceding too many runs also made him a valuable asset for GT. Sundar's performance will likely be remembered as one of the standout moments in a thrilling Test match.
