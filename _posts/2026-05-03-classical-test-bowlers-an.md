**Classical Test Bowlers Power India Past Punjab**

In a thrilling match at the Eden Gardens, Indian spinners took to the field, showcasing their classical skills to lift the hosts to a 6-wicket victory over a defiant Punjab team. Washington Sundar, the young and in-form opener, was instrumental in the Indian innings, scoring an elegant 58 runs off 90 deliveries. His partnership with Rohit Sharma was a masterclass in composure, as they rode the Indian bowlers' attack to secure a full-house win.

The Pakistan spinners, led by Haris Randhawa, put up a valiant effort, but ultimately fell short against the calmer, more composed bowling of India's fast bowlers. As the sun set on the day, India celebrated a historic victory, with Sundar being hailed as the hero of the match.
