![‘Don’t think the price tag has got to do anything with it’: Bharat Arun on Rishabh Pant’s form](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/05/Rishabh-Pant-3.jpg)

In an exclusive interview, cricket legend Bharat Arun highlighted the crucial factor in Rishabh Pant's resurgence: his mental toughness. "Rishabh Pant has not changed at all, he's still the same bloke who's been writing the book on mental resilience," Arun stated. When asked if the pressure of being the wicketkeeper has impacted his form, Arun firmly replied, "Don't think the price tag has got to do anything with it. Rishabh Pant's form is not dependent on his price tag. It's his ability to perform under pressure that matters." Arun went on to emphasize that Pant's incredible career trajectory is not solely due to his wealth, but rather his exceptional skillset. This unwavering confidence and mental fortitude have catapulted Rishabh Pant to be one of the world's top wicketkeepers.
