I'm excited to share with you a remarkable story of self-discovery and transformation. India's very own VJ Ruby Bhatia recently broke free from the shackles of her illustrious career as a television jockey to explore her spiritual side. After meditating for 24 hours a day for two years, Ruby has released a stunning message, stating that she's traded in fame for fulfillment. "I've always been driven by my passion for music and content creation," she said. "But now, I'm focused on living my life on a higher plane." With a newfound sense of purpose, Ruby has embarked on a journey of spiritual exploration, seeking answers to life's greatest questions. Her decision to leave the spotlight behind has inspired many, showing that sometimes, the true meaning of success lies in pursuing one's inner truth.
