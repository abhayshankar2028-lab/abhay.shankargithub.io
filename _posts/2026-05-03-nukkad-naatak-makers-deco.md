**Unveiling the Secrets Behind "Nukkad Naatak"**

In a stunning feat of perseverance and determination, the makers of the critically acclaimed indie film "Nukkad Naatak" have successfully decoded and released the movie despite hidden costs of Rs 70 lakh. The film, directed by debutant Benjamin D'Souza, tells the story of a young woman's journey of self-discovery in the city of Mumbai. Despite being a low-budget production, the team managed to secure funding from several investors and even crowdfunding platforms. The film's success is a testament to the power of indie cinema and the tireless efforts of the filmmakers. We caught up with the lead actress, Aishwarya Narkar, to talk about the making of the film and what inspired her to take on the challenging project.
