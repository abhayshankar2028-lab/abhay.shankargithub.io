**Babil Khan Returns with a Heartfelt Promise**

Bollywood's beloved director Babil Khan has made a triumphant return to film sets after a grueling period of mental health struggles. The talented filmmaker has been open about his personal battles with depression and anxiety, and it's clear that he's been seeking solace and support.

In a heartwarming move, Khan has promised his fans that he'll be working tirelessly to overcome his struggles and return to his creative best. "I've been on an incredible journey of self-discovery, and I'm grateful for the love and support of my fans," Khan said in a statement. "I promise to keep pushing myself, and I'm excited to share new and exciting projects with you all soon. Thank you for your understanding and patience – I'm back!" Khan's fans are eagerly awaiting his return, and we can't wait to see what's in store for him next.
