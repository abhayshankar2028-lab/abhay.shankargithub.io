**Classical Test bowlers with the mettle to test GT**

In a thrilling match at the Feroz Shah Kotla Ground, Washington Sundar shone out of the bunch as the Indian bowlers conjured up a composed innings that propelled the Gujarat Titans to a stunning victory over the resilient Punjab Kings.

Sundar, who scored a century on his test debut, was the backbone of the Titans' innings. He played a patient 76 from 89 deliveries, his lines and lengths dictating the proceedings. The Punjab attack struggled to contain the Indian opener, who was eventually joined by Ravindra Jadeja, who too scored a useful score.

The Titans' bowlers, led by Jasprit Bumrah and Bhuvneshwar Kumar, then showed their mettle with a potent combination that saw the Punjab batsmen struggling to gain a foothold. In the end, it was Sundar's composed innings that sealed the deal, as the Giants cruised to a convincing 10-wicket victory.
