"Pooja Dadwal: The Unlikely Journey of a Bollywood Starlet"

It's astonishing to think that Pooja Dadwal, the gorgeous heroine from Salim Khan's 1990 classic, Tum Mere Ho, started her journey in the film industry at just 16 years old. Despite her glamorous debut, Dadwal's early life was marked by struggles. She was forced to drop out of college due to financial constraints, and her family's financial situation worsened. Undeterred, she took up a part-time job to earn a few hundred rupees and eventually started her own tiffin service, a humble yet essential service that provided meals to those in need. Today, Dadwal's rags-to-riches story serves as a testament to the power of hard work and determination. Her journey is a reminder that success is never far away, even in the most unexpected circumstances.
