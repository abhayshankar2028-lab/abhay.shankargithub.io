"Pooja Dadwal's Unlikely Journey: Survival Story of a Bollywood Heroine

In a remarkable tale, Pooja Dadwal, the 16-year-old heroine of Salman Khan's film 'Bajranga Mahaadweep', has embarked on a battle of survival. Despite being thrust into the limelight, she continued to pursue her passion for fashion. However, the harsh realities of her life took a toll on her. Dadwal's story is a testament to the power of resilience and determination. With barely enough funds to support her education, she ran a tiffin service to make ends meet. Her selfless efforts eventually earned her recognition, but more importantly, it showcased her strength and character. This inspiring tale is a reminder that success is not solely defined by fame, but by the courage to face challenges head-on."
