**The Over-reliance of CSK on Sanju Samson**

The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) have been a dominant force in the Indian Premier League (IPL) for years, but their reliance on star player Sanju Samson can be seen as overblown. Samson's impressive batting skills have made him a key player in the team's success, but in recent years, he has shown signs of burnout and injury concerns.

CSK captain MS Dhoni has been vocal about Samson's importance, but the team's reliance on him has led to fatigue and inconsistencies in their batting lineup. The absence of key players like Ravindra Jadeja and Kedar Jadhav has exposed the team's vulnerability. While Samson is still a top-class batsman, it's time for the CSK to diversify their options and find alternative solutions to their batting woes.
