**Are Chennai Super Kings Over-Dependent on Sanju Samson?**

As the 2023 Indian Premier League (IPL) season comes to a close, fans are left wondering if the Chennai Super Kings (CSK) have been over-reliant on their talented, but often injury-prone, batter, Sanju Samson.

While Samson is undoubtedly one of the most exciting and destructive batsmen in the league, his inconsistent form has raised questions about the team's reliance on him. Injuries to key players like Ravindra Jadeja and MS Dhoni have left a significant void in the CSK lineup, making it difficult for Samson to maintain his impressive form.

Will CSK be able to find a solid replacement for Samson, or will the team continue to rely too heavily on him? The answer lies in the upcoming season, where Samson will once again find himself in the spotlight, and the CSK will need to find a way to balance his game with more consistent performances from their other batters.
