**The Unlikely Partnership: Bobby Deol's Journey with Anurag Kashyap**

In a fascinating tale of Bollywood's vibrant film industry, it's widely acknowledged that Bobby Deol's participation in Anurag Kashyap's critically acclaimed film, Bandar, was not without its challenges. The actor, known for his roles in Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge and Sholay, was hesitant to join the project. However, it was his friend and veteran actor, Dharmendra, who stepped in to persuade Deol to take a chance. According to sources, Dharmendra expressed his support and encouragement to Deol, urging him to embrace the role and go with the flow. This heartfelt support ultimately led to Deol's successful participation in Bandar, which has since become a hidden gem of Indian cinema. The story serves as a testament to the power of friendship and support in overcoming creative differences.
