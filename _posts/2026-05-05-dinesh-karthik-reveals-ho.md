Dinesh Karthik has recently opened up about how Virat Kohli encouraged him to consider a coaching role with Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB). In an interview, Karthik revealed that the Indian captain played a significant role in convincing him to take up the opportunity. "Virat Kohli was one of the first people who approached me and said, 'Dinesh, you would probably fit in quite nicely' as a coach with RCB," Karthik shared. Kohli's endorsement was likely instrumental in Karthik's decision to join the RCB coaching staff. With Kohli's backing, Karthik felt more confident in his decision, and he has since been instrumental in helping the team achieve success. Karthik's experience as a prolific opener and his ability to play a crucial role in the team's strategy have made him a valuable asset to RCB.
