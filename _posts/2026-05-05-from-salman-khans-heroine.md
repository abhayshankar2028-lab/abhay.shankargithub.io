Title: From Heroine to Entrepreneur: Pooja Dadwal's Inspiring Journey

Pooja Dadwal, the 16-year-old heroine from Salman Khan's blockbuster film Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge, has come a long way since then. What started as a humble endeavor to earn some pocket money from her part-time job turned into a remarkable entrepreneurial journey. Dadwal started running a tiffin service, providing delicious meals to her friends and family at an unbeatable price of just a few hundred rupees. Her hard work and determination paid off, and she soon expanded her business to cater to a larger clientele. Today, she's a successful entrepreneur with a loyal customer base and a bright future ahead. Dadwal's story is a testament to the power of perseverance and the importance of pursuing one's passions, even in the face of adversity.
