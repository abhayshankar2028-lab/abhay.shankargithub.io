With a warm smile and a gentle nod, Karan Johar acknowledged Shah Rukh Khan's unparalleled talent, dedication, and kindness. As the crowd witnessed the gesture, it was clear that Karan Johar's love and respect for Shah Rukh Khan knew no bounds.
