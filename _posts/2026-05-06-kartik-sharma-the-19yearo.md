Kartik Sharma, a 19-year-old all-rounder from Bharatpur, has made headlines with his impressive performances on the cricket field. Despite being one of the younger players in the Indian squad, Sharma has consistently impressed with his skills, earning him a spot in the national team.

Sharma's impressive batting and bowling abilities have caught the attention of former Indian captain MS Dhoni, who has hinted at considering him for the World Cup. Dhoni has publicly praised Sharma's hard work and dedication, stating that he was surprised by the teenager's potential. With Sharma's impressive form continuing, it remains to be seen if he will earn a spot on the national team, but one thing is for sure - he is certainly keeping Dhoni on his toes.
