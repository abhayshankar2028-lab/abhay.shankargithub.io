I'm Shekhar Suman, a renowned Bollywood blogger. In a recent interview, I shared a heartwarming story about how Shah Rukh Khan gifted me a luxurious watch. It was a beautiful reminder of our time working together on the hit film 'Chalte Chalte' (2003). According to sources close to the star, Shah Rukh Khan sent me a very expensive watch as a token of appreciation.

The director of 'Chalte Chalte,' Nitesh Tiwari, revealed that Shah Rukh Khan had gifted the watch to me as a big cheque for our collaborative effort. I was truly touched by his kindness and generosity. This moment not only reminded me of the positive impact of our movie but also the incredible bond I shared with Shah Rukh Khan during the making of 'Chalte Chalte'. It's a testament to the true spirit of Bollywood.
