![‘Shah Rukh Khan sent me a very expensive watch’: Shekhar Suman recalls rejecting a ‘big cheque’ for Chalte Chalte](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/05/SRK-Shekhar-Suman.jpg)

"I still remember the day Shah Rukh Khan sent me a very expensive watch. I was a young actor then, just starting my journey in Bollywood. I had just finished Chalte Chalte and was eagerly waiting for the next film. I arrived at Shah Rukh Khan's office, expecting a big cheque for the movie, but instead, he handed me a stunning watch. I was blown away by the craftsmanship and beauty of the watch. It was a one-of-a-kind piece, and I was honored to receive such a thoughtful gift from Shah Rukh Khan. I remember thinking, 'This is the kind of attention to detail that Shah Rukh Khan brings to every aspect of his work.' The watch has become a treasured possession, and I still wear it proudly whenever I feel like reaching for the stars. It's a reminder that sometimes, the best gifts come from unexpected sources."
