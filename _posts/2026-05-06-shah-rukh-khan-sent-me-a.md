**The Extravagant Gift of Shah Rukh Khan**

In a heartwarming story, Bollywood actor Shekhar Suman revealed that Shah Rukh Khan sent him a stunning watch as a token of appreciation for his role in the hit film "Chalte Chalte". Suman shared the experience in a recent interview, saying that the actor had gifted him a diamond-studded watch worth a whopping INR 10 lakhs. However, Suman expressed his gratitude by saying that he would never forget the simple yet thoughtful gesture. "I still remember the day Shah Rukh Khan gave me the watch," Suman said, visibly moved. "It was a big cheque for Chalte Chalte, but his watch was so thoughtful and extravagant that I couldn't resist being touched by it." Suman's genuine emotion is a testament to the actor's kind heart and generosity.
