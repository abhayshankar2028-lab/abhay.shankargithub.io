**A Forgotten Era: Amitabh Bachchan's Resignation**

In the annals of Indian cinema, one name stands tall - Amitabh Bachchan. However, there's another tale behind the star's rise to fame, one that's often overlooked. Decades before Vijay, Bachchan's career trajectory was marred by scandals that led to his resignation. In the 1970s, Bachchan infamously feuded with the press and his film "Anand" was criticized for its alleged plagiarism. These controversies, combined with his outspoken nature, made him a polarizing figure. As the years went by, Bachchan's star continued to rise, but his personal life and public image took a hit. His resignation from politics in 1978 was a result of these scandals. Despite this, Bachchan's legacy remains intact, and his story serves as a reminder that even the most charismatic stars can fall.
