**Nora Fatehi in Hot Water: National Commission for Women to Hear Her Out**

In a shocking turn of events, Bollywood actress Nora Fatehi has been summoned by the National Commission for Women (NCW) to clear her name over the 'Sarke Chunar' controversy. The incident, which took place at a party in 2020, alleged that Fatehi had a close relationship with a young woman who was subsequently harassed by her. The NCW has summoned Fatehi to provide an explanation and address the allegations. This development comes just days after Fatehi was arrested in connection with the case. Fatehi's team has been tight-lipped about her decision to attend the hearing, but sources close to the actress claim that she is ready to clear her name. This is a significant move as the NCW has been investigating Fatehi's involvement in the case. The actress's reputation is on the line, and it remains to be seen how she will respond to the allegations.
