I still remember the day Shah Rukh Khan gifted my home a stunning diamond-studded watch, a true masterpiece. It was a rare opportunity to own such an exquisite piece, but he did it with an ulterior motive. In an exclusive interview, Shah Rukh shared how he once gifted me a 'big cheque' during the filming of Chalte Chalte. My heart still skips a beat recalling the excitement and gratitude I felt that day. He told me that he wanted to reward me for my hard work and dedication, but I never suspected that he had a penchant for luxury watches. Little did I know, my heart would soon be racing with anticipation as Shah Rukh offered me the watch. What a thought-provoking experience - one that made me realize the true value of friendship.
