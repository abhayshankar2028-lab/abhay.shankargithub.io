"Exclusive Interview: The Bollywood Bubbly's Take on Daadi Ki Shaadi

I had the pleasure of sitting down with the talented Alia Bhatt, who recently wrapped up her latest project along with her co-stars Neetu Kapoor and Riddhima Sahni. The drama packed special was Daadi Ki Shaadi, where the three ladies bring the heat as the eccentric and lovable Daadi family.

Alia, who played the lead role, praised her co-stars for their exceptional talent and dedication. 'Neetu and Riddhima are absolute perfection. They completely immersed themselves in their characters and brought the required energy to the set,' she gushed. The actress also praised her co-star, Shaandaar actress Riddhima, for her 'talented' portrayal. 'Riddhima's character is an absolute delight. She brings the required sass and charm to the role,' Alia added. The two stunning actresses are set to star in the upcoming film, 'Jambo', which promises to be an exciting ride."
