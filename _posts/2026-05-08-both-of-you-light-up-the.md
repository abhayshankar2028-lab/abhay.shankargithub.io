"Daadi Ki Shaadi" has been making waves in the Bollywood circuit, especially with its star-studded cast. Alia Bhatt is one of the many actors who have expressed their love for the show. Recently, she took to social media to praise her family members, including Neetu Kapoor's character, Daadi. "Both of you light up the screen," Alia affectionately said, praising Neetu's portrayal of her character. She also highlighted Neetu's beautiful chemistry with her husband, Riddhima Sahni. Alia's words are a testament to the love and affection that the two share. It's no surprise that their on-screen bond has captured the hearts of fans, and we can't wait to see more of their Daadi Ki Shaadi. Neetu and Riddhima's chemistry has been on point, and it's clear that they make a beautiful couple.
