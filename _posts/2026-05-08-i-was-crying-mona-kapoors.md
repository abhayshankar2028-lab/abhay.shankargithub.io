"I was crying": Mona Kapoor's 'home broke' amid Boney-Sridevi relationship

Mona Kapoor, the actress who was engaged to actor Boney Kapoor's son Arjun Kapoor, has opened up about the emotional turmoil she faced during her relationship. According to Jaspinder Narula, the actress shared her painful experience of living with her in-laws. Mona Kapoor tweeted that she felt like she was in a "home broken" state due to the constant pressure and scrutiny from her in-laws, Boney Kapoor and Sridevi. Jaspinder Narula corroborated this claim, stating that she witnessed Mona Kapoor crying and feeling emotionally drained from the constant attention and criticism. The experience left Mona Kapoor feeling isolated and suffocated, which ultimately led to her decision to leave the relationship.
