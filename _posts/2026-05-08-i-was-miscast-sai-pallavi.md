![‘I was miscast’: Sai Pallavi says someone else should have done Ek Din](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/01/ek-din.jpg)

"I've been in the industry for quite some time now, and I've been fortunate enough to have gotten to work on several films. Recently, I was in the news for my role in Ek Din. However, I've been receiving criticism that I was miscast in the film. The director has been touting me as an actress who could have done better in the role. I understand that everyone has their own opinion, but I want to respectfully disagree. While I've worked my way up the ladder, I believe I was a better fit for the character at the time. I wish the director all the best and hope that he learns from his mistake. To my fans, I want to assure them that I'm truly grateful for the opportunity to work on Ek Din and hope that you will continue to support me." - Sai Pallavi
