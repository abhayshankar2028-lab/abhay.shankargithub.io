**IPL: KKR bowlers Fall Short Against Delhi**

In a 38-run chase, Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) bowlers were unable to stem the Delhi Capitals (DC) innings, ultimately losing by 9 wickets. The Delhi Capitals, led by David Warner, put up a solid performance with Warner scoring 88, while Babar Azam chipped in with 41 off 64 deliveries. However, the KKR bowlers, with Shardul Thakur and Mayank Agarwal, struggled to contain the Delhi attack, particularly Finn Allen, who converted his 35-ball cameo into six crucial fours and a six.

Allen's aggressive batting was the turning point in the match, as he took the attack to the DC bowlers and helped his team achieve a commanding 9-wicket victory. The loss will be a significant blow to KKR's hopes of making the playoffs, but they can still salvage their season with a win in their next match.
