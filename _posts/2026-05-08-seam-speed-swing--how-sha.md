As the 2023 Indian Premier League approaches, the slow and steady approach has paid off for Lucknow Super Giants (LSG). The trio of Shahid Afridi's left-arm seamer, Jasprit Bumrah, and the batting duo of Ishan Kishan and Mayank Agarwal, along with the middle-pace bowler, Mohammed Shami, has formed a formidable pace pack.

Shami's occasional raw pace has proven to be a game-changer, taking wickets at crucial intervals. His ability to swing the ball both ways has also been a significant factor in LSG's success. Mayank Agarwal's impressive batting has been a key factor in LSG's top-order dominance, while Ishan Kishan's aggressive batting style has given the opposition trouble.

This combined pace, paired with Shami's raw pace, has made LSG a formidable opponent in the IPL. With a strong core of bowlers and batsmen, LSG is poised to create a winning formula that will continue to challenge their opponents.
