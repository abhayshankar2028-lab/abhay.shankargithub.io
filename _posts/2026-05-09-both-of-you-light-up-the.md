**A Masterclass in Family Drama: Alia Bhatt Shines in Daadi Ki Shaadi**

In a recent television debut, Neetu Kapoor's eldest daughter, Riddhima Sahni, took the Bollywood scene by storm with her stunning performance as the doting and loving dadi in her upcoming family drama, Daadi Ki Shaadi. Alia Bhatt, who plays the role of Riddhima's sister, is no stranger to dramatic roles, but her portrayal of the dadi's sharp wit and sassiness left viewers in awe. With her impeccable acting skills and razor-sharp comedic timing, Alia Bhatt truly shines alongside her family members. Whether she's teasing her sister or doling out advice, Alia brings a level of authenticity and charm that makes Daadi Ki Shaadi a must-watch for any family drama fan. Both of you light up the screen with their heartwarming performances!
