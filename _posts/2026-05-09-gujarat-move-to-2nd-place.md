Gujarat's thrilling 77-run victory over Rajasthan has catapulted them to the second spot in the league table, with a 1-1 record. The match was a nail-biter, with both teams giving their all. Gujarat's Jofra Archer and Rashid Khan were instrumental in their team's success, taking 4 and 3 wickets, respectively, to help their side secure the win. Gujarat's star batsman, Ishan Kishan, also scored a valuable 44 runs, while Mohammed Shami chipped in with 3 crucial wickets. The performance was a testament to the team's resilience and determination. With this win, Gujarat have now moved into second place behind the table-toppers, Mumbai. The win sets the stage for a crucial match against Rajasthan next week.
