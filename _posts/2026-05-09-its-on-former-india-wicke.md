![‘It’s on’: Former India wicketkeeper backs KKR to storm into playoffs after fourth straight win](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/05/KKR-DC-5.jpg)

"It's on" for the Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) after their fourth consecutive win in the Indian Premier League (IPL). As former India wicketkeeper, Nitish Rana, aptly put it, "It's on" for the team to storm into the playoffs. With their impressive performance, KKR looks to make a strong push for the title. The team's head coach, Stephen Cook, seems to agree, stating, "We've got the momentum on our side, and we're looking to build on that momentum to make a deep playoff run." Rana added, "We know we have the firepower in our squad, and we're looking to unleash it on the opposition." The pressure is on for the team to perform under pressure, but Rana's words are music to the ears of KKR fans. Can the team rise to the challenge and make a deep playoff run? Only time will tell.
