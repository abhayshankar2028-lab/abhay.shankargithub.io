**Esports on the Edge: Hayden on Sooryavanshi**

In the highly anticipated clash between Gujarat Titans and Rajasthan Royals, Indian cricketer Ravichandran Ashwin played an incredible 30 runs at a strike rate of 300. His dominance in the middle order was a force to be reckoned with. However, his team's performance was marred by a lack of energy in the lower order, particularly Roy's batting.

Ashwin's innings was a masterclass in batting, as he remained unbeaten despite being on a high score. His partnership with Ishan Kishan was particularly impressive, showcasing the duo's ability to rotate the strike and take the game away from the opposition.

While Ashwin's performance was a highlight of the match, it's clear that the Titans needed more from their lower order to compete with the Royals.
