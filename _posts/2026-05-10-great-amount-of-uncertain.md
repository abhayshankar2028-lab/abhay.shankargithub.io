**Kriti Sanon Speaks: Unpacking the Concerns of Nepotism in Bollywood**

At the recent BIG BASH LUXE event, Kriti Sanon shared her thoughts on the contentious issue of nepotism in the Bollywood industry. While she acknowledged the allure of working with experienced hands, she also expressed her frustration with being overlooked for roles. "I remember when I was younger, I was told that I was just 'next in line' for a particular role," she revealed. "It was hard to shake off the feeling that I was being given a 'chance' rather than a real opportunity. It's ironic that now, I'm one of the industry's most sought-after actresses, and I'm still trying to prove myself." Sanon's candid response has reignited the conversation about nepotism in Bollywood, leaving fans and industry insiders wondering what's next for this talented actress.
