"It was a big lull": Kiara Advani's words of encouragement to her fellow actors still ring true. The actress, who played the role of MS Dhoni's wife Sakina in the biopic 'Dhoni', recently opened up about the struggle she faced in Bollywood. Despite the success of the biopic, Kiara expressed that it didn't end her struggle in the industry. "It was a big lull, but we didn't give up," she said in an interview. Kiara's words of encouragement to her fellow actors echo the experiences of many others who have faced similar struggles. The actress's dedication to her craft and her refusal to give up in the face of adversity serve as a testament to the power of perseverance. Her words are a reminder that success is not just about achieving fame, but also about staying true to oneself.
