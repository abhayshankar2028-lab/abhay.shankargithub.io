"RCB 2/0 (10 overs) vs MI: Rajat Patidar's Post-Match Analysis

In a thrilling two-wicket win over Mumbai Indians, RCB had their hands full against a formidable MI side. However, head coach Rajat Patidar was quick to deflect any praise, stating that his team "didn't deserve to win" this match. Patidar was visibly disappointed as he expressed his frustration with MI's bowling effort. "We played poorly from start to finish," he said. "Our batting was exposed, and we couldn't capitalize on our chances. We need to work on our discipline and patience to achieve our goals." Patidar's candid comments suggest that the team still has much to learn from their contest against MI. Will RCB be able to rise to the challenge and make a strong comeback in the upcoming matches?"
