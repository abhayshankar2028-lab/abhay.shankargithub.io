Rajat Patidar's recent performance for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League has been a topic of discussion among cricket enthusiasts. In a post-match interview with ESPN Cricinfo, Patidar expressed his disappointment with the outcome of the match against Mumbai Indians (MI). Despite a strong batting effort from Patidar, RCB was bowled out for just 177 runs. Patidar stated, "We didn't deserve to win this match. Our batting was not great, while MI's defense was good. We need to work on our fielding and bowling to win matches." The defeat has been a setback for RCB, but Patidar remains confident about his team's future. With a strong squad and a solid strategy, RCB is likely to bounce back in the next match.
