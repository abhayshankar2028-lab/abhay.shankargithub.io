In a thrilling match at the Green Park in Lucknow, Urvil Patel's aggressive innings sent the Chennai Super Kings to the brink of disaster. The Indian all-rounder's 43-ball 71 was coupled with a potent partnership with Yuvraj Singh, who scored a 45-ball 50. Their combined 122-run addition to the total put pressure on the Lucknow team to respond. However, the Super Kings' batsmen refused to back down, with MS Dhoni and MS Dhoni keeping the score ticking.

In the end, it was Dhoni who spearheaded the comeback effort, scoring 25 not out. The decisive blow came with just 33 balls to go, leaving the Super Kings 30 runs shy of an equalizer. The victory keeps the hopes alive for the Chennai Super Kings, but the Mumbai Indians will be eager to exact revenge in their next encounter.
