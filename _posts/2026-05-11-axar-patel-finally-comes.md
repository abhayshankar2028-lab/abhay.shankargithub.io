**Delhi Strikers Stun Punjab Kings with Thrilling Upset**

In a thrilling match, the Delhi Strikers secured a crucial 6-wicket win over the Punjab Kings, leaving themselves a glimmer of hope to stay in contention for the playoffs. Axar Patel, who had been in a slump, finally broke out of his shell with a stunning 63*, taking 2 catches and 2 wickets to make a significant contribution to the Strikers' victory. The win has indeed lifted the spirits of Delhi, who are now just 2 points away from the playoffs.

David Miller, who scored an impressive 50*, played a crucial role in keeping the Strikers in the game. His aggressive batting and fielding helped the team to recoup from a somewhat slow start. With the win, Delhi has now secured 10 points from 6 matches, while the Punjab Kings are struggling to find their footing. The win has sent a strong message to the other teams in the league, who will need to come out strong to avoid being relegated.
