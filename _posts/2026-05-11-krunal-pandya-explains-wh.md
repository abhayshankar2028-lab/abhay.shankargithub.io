In a jaw-dropping display of grit and determination, Krunal Pandya batted on despite severe cramps in Royal Challengers Bangalore's (RCB) run chase against Mumbai Indians (MI). Pandya's decision to continue batting was met with a mixture of cheers and boos from the RCB camp, but his teammate and captain, Virat Kohli, refused to give up. Pandya, who has been struggling with injuries, emphasized that his cramps were nothing compared to the will to win. "I knew I had to bat on, even if it was tough," Pandya said. Kohli added, "Krunal's body is weak, but his spirit is stronger. We'll be happy with a draw, no matter what." Pandya's courageous display has sparked a heated debate about the importance of sportsmanship in cricket. Will his actions inspire other players to follow his lead? Only time will tell.
