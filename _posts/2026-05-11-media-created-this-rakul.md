**Media Created This: Rakul Preet Singh & Sara Ali Khan Deny Sidelining Wamiqa Gabbi**

The recent controversy surrounding Wamiqa Gabbi's sudden departure from the popular Hollywood production 'Bigg Boss: Dusk' has left fans buzzing. However, things have taken a surprising turn as Rakul Preet Singh and Sara Ali Khan, both of whom were involved in the project, have come forward to deny any involvement in the alleged sidelining of Wamiqa.

In a series of interviews, both actresses claimed that Wamiqa was not the one who left the show, and they were not aware of any issues that led to her sudden departure. The trio was seen as one of the most promising and talented teams in the production, and their absence has left fans wondering what went wrong. Both Rakul and Sara have maintained their innocence, and it remains to be seen who will be the next to come forward to clear their names. Only time will tell.
