**Rakul Preet Singh and Sara Ali Khan Unite Against Media 'Sideling' Wamiqa Gabbi**

In a shocking incident, several media outlets have been accused of 'sideling' actress Wamiqa Gabbi, sparking numerous reactions from actresses and fans alike. Rakul Preet Singh and Sara Ali Khan have joined the chorus of support for the beleaguered actress, emphasizing that the media's behavior is unacceptable.

Both actresses have come forward to express their solidarity with Gabbi, stating that she deserves respect and deserving of a fair coverage. "This is not the first time we've seen media uninformed and biased towards celebrities," said Rakul. "Their actions have no place in the entertainment industry," added Sara. With their words, it's clear that the actresses are committed to supporting Gabbi and breaking the media's grip on her story.
