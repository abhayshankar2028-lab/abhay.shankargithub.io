**Shahid Kapoor's Mother Neelima Azeem Speaks Out: "Toxic Narratives"**

Neelima Azeem, the mother of Bollywood superstar Shahid Kapoor, has taken a strong stance against the "toxic" narratives surrounding her son's personal life. In an interview, she revealed that she is "fed up" with the constant scrutiny and gossip surrounding Shahid's three failed marriages. The actress expressed her frustration, stating, "I don't even drink" and "I don't want to be a part of this toxic culture." She emphasized that her family's values and upbringing are a source of strength, and that she will not let the media dictate their lives. Neelima's words serve as a reminder that celebrities deserve respect and support, rather than being shamed and judged by the media. Her statements are a powerful message to her son and fans alike.
