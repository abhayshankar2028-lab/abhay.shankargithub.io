**100% Bowler & 100% Batter: Madhav Tiwari Sets the Standard for Delhi Capitals**

In an unprecedented display of all-rounder brilliance, Delhi Capitals' new hero Madhav Tiwari has emerged as the perfect blend of bowler and batter. The 25-year-old all-rounder has been instrumental in turning the game around for his team, with an impressive record of 24.5 wickets in 12 matches.

Tiwari's batting prowess has been equally impressive, with 3 centuries in his first 10 innings. He has also consistently contributed with crucial partnerships, showcasing his ability to play both technically and tactically. With Delhi Capitals looking to build momentum, Madhav Tiwari's all-rounder credentials have set the bar high. As the franchise looks to navigate the challenges of the upcoming season, Madhav Tiwari's unique skillset has raised the bar for all Indian all-rounders.
