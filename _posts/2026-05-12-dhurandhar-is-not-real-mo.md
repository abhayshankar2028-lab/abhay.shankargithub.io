**"Dhurandhar is not real": Mohan Kapur's Heartfelt Op-Ed**

In an extraordinary display of Bollywood fervor, Mohan Kapur, the creator of the iconic film "Dhurandhar", has sparked a heated debate with his recent statement. The renowned filmmaker expressed his dismay at the overwhelming praise he received for his life's work, stating that "Dhurandhar" is "not real". Kapur's poignant observation was met with shock and surprise from fans and industry insiders alike. "Dhurandhar" is a poignant tale of love, loss, and redemption, crafted with unwavering dedication and a deep understanding of human emotions. Kapur's words serve as a poignant reminder that true artistry lies in the nuances of the human experience, not the superficial glitz of a film. As the creator of "Dhurandhar", Kapur's words are a heartfelt tribute to the power of storytelling.
