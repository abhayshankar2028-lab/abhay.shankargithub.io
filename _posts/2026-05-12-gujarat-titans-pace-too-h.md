**Gujarat Titans Pace Too Hot to Handle for Sunrisers Hyderabad**

The Gujarat Titans continued their dominance in the Indian Premier League (IPL) with a convincing 9-wicket win over the Sunrisers Hyderabad. The match was marked by a blistering pace attack that left the Sunrisers struggling to cope. The Titans' fast bowlers, Jofra Archer and Rashid Khan, were instrumental in dismantling the Hyderabad batting line-up. Archer's 3/32 and Rashid's 2/15 were the standout performances, but it was the 12-wicket haul by Rashid that sealed the deal for the Titans. The Sunrisers' bowlers were unable to contain the pace, and as a result, they slipped further down the ranks. With the win, the Titans now sit at the top of the table, while the Sunrisers are in last place. The Titans' impressive form has set the stage for a thrilling final showdown against the Delhi Capitals.
