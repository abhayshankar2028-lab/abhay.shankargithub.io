In a stunning display of cricketing acumen, Shreyas Iyer delivered a scathing post about Punjab's loss against Delhi Capitals (DC). Iyer's comments highlighted the team's poor performance, lack of discipline, and inability to execute their plans. He stated, "I don't have to beat around the bush, he didn't work, he was not in form... He didn't show up, he didn't contribute." Iyer's sharp words reflected on the team's captain, Harbhajan Singh, who was also left at the receiving end. The post also pointed out the team's failure to adapt to the game's dynamics and their inability to adapt to the conditions. Iyer's words were laced with a bitter taste, but they perfectly encapsulated the team's performance. The post will undoubtedly spark a heated debate among cricket fans, with many questioning Punjab's ability to compete at the highest level.
