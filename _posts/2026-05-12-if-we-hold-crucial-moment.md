**Delhi Capitals' Regrettable Misses: An Exclusive Analysis with Axar Patel**

In a thrilling season, the Delhi Capitals have had their fair share of heartbreaking moments. However, as we reflect on their missed chances, one player stands out - Axar Patel. The talented all-rounder has been instrumental in the team's success, but the extent of his contribution has often been overlooked. In an exclusive interview, Axar shared his thoughts on the Capitals' downfall.

"Keep saying that we hold crucial moments, we can win," Axar emphasized. "We have to believe in ourselves. We must learn to manage our expectations and not get too high when we're behind. Sometimes, we take a chance, and it doesn't work out. But when we do, we capitalize on them and move forward." With Axar's words, we see a glimmer of hope for the Capitals' future success.
