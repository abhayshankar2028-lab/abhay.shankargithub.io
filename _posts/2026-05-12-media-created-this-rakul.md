**Breaking News: Rakul Preet Singh and Sara Ali Khan Speak Out Against Sidelining Wamiqa Gabbi**

In a shocking turn of events, Bollywood celebrities Rakul Preet Singh and Sara Ali Khan have come forward to deny the allegations of sidelining Wamiqa Gabbi. The actress was recently dropped from the popular TV show "The Kapil Sharma Show" after performing just one episode. However, sources close to the issue claim that Wamiqa's popularity was at an all-time low, leading to her being "dropped" from the show.

Rakul Preet Singh and Sara Ali Khan have publicly denied these allegations, stating that they never knew about Wamiqa's performance and were not aware of any plans to drop her from the show. Both actresses have expressed their support for Wamiqa and have assured her that they will continue to stand by her.
