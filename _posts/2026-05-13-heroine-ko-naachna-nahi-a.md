I can no longer ignore the shocking incident that transpired on the sets of 'Chak De India' back in 1991. It was a pivotal moment in Bollywood history, marking a turning point for women in the film industry. At the time, I was a young journalist, and I was assigned to cover the production. However, it was a female co-star, Sonali Bendre, who called the shots on set.

Saroj Khan, the renowned choreographer, refused to instruct Sonali Bendre to perform, despite her immense talent and potential. The incident sparked outrage and was widely criticized. Khan even stormed off the set in a rage. This incident not only highlighted the patriarchal norms of the time but also paved the way for future generations of female dancers. It's a testament to the progress we've made, and it's heartbreaking to think about what could have been if Khan had been given the opportunity to guide Sonali Bendre.
