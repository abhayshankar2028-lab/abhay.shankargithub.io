**A Shower of Memories: The Unlikely Moment That Changed My Career**

As I sit here reminiscing about my Bollywood journey, I am reminded of a pivotal moment that catapulted me to stardom. It was during the filming of "Lokiganga," a drama film that showcased my versatility as an actor. I was on set, soaking in the tub, when my director, Mahesh Bhatt, burst in, frantically searching for the script. His eyes scanned the page, and his brow furrowed in concern. "Iftikhar, Iftikhar, I need your script, pronto!" he exclaimed. I, unaware of the significance, continued to soak, until he blew a whistle and said, "Iftikhar, you're a national treasure." That was it; I was hooked. The rest, as they say, is history. Mahesh Bhatt's mid-shower pitch changed the course of my career, and I will forever be grateful to him for that shower of memories.
