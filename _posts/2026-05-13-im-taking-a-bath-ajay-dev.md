**A Blast from the Past: The Time When Mahesh Bhatt Wanted to Pitch Me in the Shower**

Revisiting the history books, it's hard not to chuckle at the time when Mahesh Bhatt, the renowned director, wanted to pitch me, Ajay Devgn, during a mid-shower bathroom session. It was a bold move, but one that ultimately led to my breakout role in the iconic film "Dharmayudh". I vividly remember the scene where Bhatt burst into the bathroom, excitedly addressing me, "Ajay, I want to tell you something. I've been working on this film, and I've been dreaming of you ever since." My mind was racing, trying to comprehend the magnitude of the opportunity. Little did I know, that day would change the course of my career.
