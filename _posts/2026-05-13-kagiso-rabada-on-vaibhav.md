Kagiso Rabada, the 24-year-old fast bowler from South Africa, has been making waves in the cricket world with his fearsome bowling skills. Recently, he made his debut for the Indian national team, and one of the standout performances was against Vaibhav Sooryavanshi. During the match, Rabada took 3 wickets for 46 runs, showcasing his exceptional speed and control. Sooryavanshi, a talented young batsman, was caught out by Rabada in the 40th over, ending his innings. Rabada's fearless approach to the game was evident, as he did not shy away from facing the danger zone. His ability to deliver at both ends will be crucial in India's quest for victory. Rabada's impressive performance has earned him a spot in the Indian team's squad for the upcoming series.
