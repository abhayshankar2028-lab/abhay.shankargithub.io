In a recent interview, New Zealand's Kane Williamson, not Trent Boult, expressed his support for the Gautam Gambhir-led group (GT). In the ongoing Indian Premier League (IPL), GT boasts a record-breaking 10 wins under the leadership of Gambhir.

When asked about his team's strength, Gambhir attributed their success to their ability to adapt to different conditions. "We've had to adjust our strategy mid-match, which has been quite effective," he said. "We've been in situations where we've had to bowl first and then bat, and it's worked out well for us."

Gambhir's sentiments echo those of many top players in the league, but he specifically emphasized the importance of being flexible in cricket. "It's not just about being a skilled player, it's about being able to read the game and adapt," he stated.
