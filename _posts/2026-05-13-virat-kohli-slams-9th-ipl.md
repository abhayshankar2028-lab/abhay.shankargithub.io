**Virat Kohli Extends Record, Slams 9th IPL Century**

In a thrilling match against the Kolkata Knight Riders, Virat Kohli continued his dominance in the Indian Premier League by slamming his ninth IPL century. The Indian captain's 120 not out helped his team, Royal Challengers Bangalore, secure a convincing 8-wicket victory.

Kohli's century was a masterclass display of batting, as he faced 126 deliveries with ease. He thoroughly dominated the Knights' bowlers, opening the batting with Rohit Sharma and later coming in at number 3. His aggressive approach paid off, as he took advantage of the opposition's bowlers to create a strong foundation for his team's innings.

Kohli's 9th IPL century will undoubtedly go down in history, cementing his status as one of the greatest batsmen in the IPL.
