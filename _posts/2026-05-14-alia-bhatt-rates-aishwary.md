![Alia Bhatt rates Aishwarya Rai’s look from Cannes 2002 as ‘most iconic’; meets F1 driver Charles Leclerc](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/05/aishwarya-alia.jpg)

In a recent interview, Bollywood star Alia Bhatt praised Aishwarya Rai's stunning look from the 2002 Cannes Film Festival. The actress reportedly cited Aishwarya's bold, yet elegant gowns as the most iconic of the event. Alia reflected on how the outfit made her feel, stating that it was as if she was walking on air. She also expressed her admiration for Aishwarya's confidence and poise, which truly left a lasting impression. After her conversation with Aishwarya, Alia had the opportunity to meet F1 driver Charles Leclerc at a charity event. Initially, Charles was hesitant, but after being impressed by Alia's warm smile and charming demeanor, he struck up a conversation. Their brief reunion was filled with laughter and excitement, showcasing the true power of kindness and human connection.
