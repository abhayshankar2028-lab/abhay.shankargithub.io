**"A Royal Response: Alia Bhatt Speaks Out"**

Last night, Alia Bhatt's attendance at the prestigious Cannes Film Festival generated quite a buzz, with many critics questioning her ability to handle the glamour of the red carpet. One of them, in particular, made a bold claim, saying that one noticed her at the festival. The question on everyone's mind was, was that a genuine observation or a clever marketing ploy?

Alia Bhatt has now responded, sending a strong message to her critics. In an interview, she revealed that her presence at Cannes was purely coincidental and that she was there to promote her upcoming film. She even went on to say, "You noticed me? You noticed me! If you didn't, that's because you weren't there." This response is a clear indication that Alia Bhatt is not one to be underestimated, and her confidence is palpable.
