**WTC at Stake: India to Send A-Team to Sri Lanka**

The World Test Championship (WTC) is just around the corner, and with it comes the pressure to secure a spot in the India - Australia - New Zealand (IAN) group. India's bowling attack has been in a slump, but recent performances have shown promise. With a plethora of talented red-ball regulars, including JASPRIT SINGH, KUALA RAMSEEN, and PRABHAT CHANDRA, the Indian A-Team is well-equipped to take on the best bowlers in the world.

Their mission is to send three to four players to Sri Lanka, where they will face the IAN squad. The key to success lies in their ability to take wickets and control the game. India's squad has the skill and experience to take on the best, but can they overcome their recent struggles and deliver? The stage is set for an epic showdown, and fans around the world are eagerly anticipating the action.
