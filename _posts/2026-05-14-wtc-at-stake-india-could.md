**WTC at Stake: India Eyes Sri Lanka Bumper Budget**

The World Test Championship (WTC) is just around the corner, and India's cricket team is eager to secure a spot in the tournament. With a bumper budget of $175 million, India is looking to send a strong team to the tournament, including a mix of red-ball regulars. The key players, including Shreyas Iyer, Ravindra Jadeja, and Jasprit Bumrah, are likely to be part of the A team. However, India's coach Ravi Shastri has hinted at a more aggressive approach, with a focus on getting as many as three to four red-ball regulars into the squad. The team will be looking to capitalize on a strong Sri Lankan team, led by Dhanushka Perera and Nipun van der Groen. The stakes are high, and India must deliver to make sure they retain their WTC spot.
