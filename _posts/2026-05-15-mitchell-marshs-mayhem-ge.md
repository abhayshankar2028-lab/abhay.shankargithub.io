**LSD Stunned by Marsh's Masterclass**

A thrilling 3-wicket haul from Mitchell Marsh has sent the Lowland Stock Exchange (LSG) to the brink of making it to the playoffs. Marsh's sensational display saw him dismiss openers Glenn Maxwell and Moeen Ali, before then removing Dwayne Bravo and Sam Curran to seal the win. The LSG batsmen, who had struggled for form in recent matches, were left stunned as Marsh's form finally clicked. The Foxes' star all-rounder has consistently delivered in big games, and this win cements his status as one of the world's best. With Marsh's 3-wicket haul and LSG's new wicket patterns, they are now firmly in the playoff mix. The win will also help to dent CSK's hopes of making it to the playoffs, leaving them to regroup and recharge for the latter stages of the season. Can LSG continue their form? Only time will tell.
