"Sharmila Tagore's Advice to Saif Ali Khan: 'Wake Up' For Bollywood Acting

Sharmila Tagore, the legendary actress, recently dropped some interesting insights on Saif Ali Khan's acting abilities. In an interview, she shared her observations about her former co-star Saif, stating that he has the potential to become a great actor, but needs to wake up and take his craft seriously. Tagore said, 'Saif Ali Khan asked me to wake up, I told him, "Not sounding like an interesting actor." I wanted to make him believe that he's capable of becoming a great actor, but he didn't want to take it seriously.' Tagore's words of wisdom serve as a reminder to all aspiring actors to take their craft seriously and strive to improve their performances. It's a valuable lesson from one of Bollywood's most respected stars."
