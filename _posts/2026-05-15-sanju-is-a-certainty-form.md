In a recent statement, former India head coach Ravi Shastri has expressed his confidence in Rohit Sharma's successor, Rishabh Pant. However, he has also praised a different player, Shreyas Iyer, stating that he believes Rohit's other team, the T20I captain position is for Rishabh Pant. Shastri's statement comes after Pant's impressive performances in India's recent T20I series against Australia. The former head coach believes that Rishabh Pant's batting skills are better suited for the T20I captaincy role. However, Shastri's assertion that Rohit is the next T20I captain isn't entirely new, as many have been speculating about a similar transfer in recent times. Nevertheless, Rishabh Pant's impressive performances have earned him the spot, making him a strong contender to be considered for the role.
