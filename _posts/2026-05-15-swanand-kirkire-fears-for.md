**The Soul of Bollywood Music: A Concern of Swanand Kirkire**

As the founder of the iconic music label, Swanand Kirkire, I've always been deeply invested in the soul of Bollywood music. The melodies, the lyrics, the rhythm – all of it is a reflection of the human experience. But amidst the chaos, a growing concern has been brewing: AI-generated music. Swanand Kirkire fears that the increasing use of AI algorithms may compromise the essence of Indian music.

I remember the countless hours I spent crafting "O Ri Chiraiya" with my team, pouring our hearts and souls into every note. The result was a work of art that resonated with audiences worldwide. I'm worried that as AI-generated music becomes more prevalent, it will dilute the very soul of Bollywood music. The thought of machines replicating our masterpieces is a fear that keeps me up at night. One day, can we truly call ourselves the masters of music?
