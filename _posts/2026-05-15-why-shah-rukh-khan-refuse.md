**A Heartwarming Lesson in Etiquette: Shah Rukh Khan's Timeless Wisdom**

In an era where social media has become an integral part of our lives, it's not uncommon to see celebrities taking to FaceTime with their loved ones. However, when it comes to interacting with children, things can get a bit more complicated. Take Shah Rukh Khan, the King of Bollywood, for instance. In 2008, his daughter, Aaradhya, was facing harassment on social media, and Shah Rukh took a stand. Following a wise rule of etiquette, he refused to FaceTime the child, instead choosing to contact her parents and express his concerns.

This decision not only protected Aaradhya but also sent a powerful message about the importance of respecting children's privacy and boundaries. Shah Rukh's selfless act is a shining example of how one can make a positive impact without sacrificing their personal boundaries. A lesson that continues to inspire us all.
