**Aamir Khan Sets the Record Straight: Ghost Directing Claims Ruined**

Renowned Bollywood actor Aamir Khan has issued a scathing response to the recent allegations of ghost directing on his upcoming film, claiming once again that he is the sole creative force behind the project.

Rumors had circulated that Khan had approached several directors to helm his film, only to have them turn him down. Insiders alleged that the actors were hesitant to take on the role, fearing they would be overshadowed by Khan's star power. However, Aamir Khan is quick to dismiss these claims, stating that he would never put someone else's name on his project without their consent. "If I was directing, why would I put someone else's name?" he asked. Khan's team has denied these allegations, and it remains to be seen whether the rumors will be proved true.
