In a thrilling match, Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) secured a hard-fought victory over Gujarat Titans (GT) on Saturday, securing their spot in the IPL 2026 playoffs. Opener Shreyas Iyer and skipper Kusal Perera delivered crucial knocks as KKR's batting line-up managed to stay afloat after a solid top-order start. However, it was Ravi Ashwin's controlled innings that yielded the most significant impact. The middle-order pair of Nitish Rana and Shubman Gill also showed their mettle, as they chipped in with crucial contributions. Meanwhile, GT's slow bowler Rashid Khan was castled on multiple occasions, forcing KKR to dig deep. In the end, it was Ashwin who played the most crucial role, scoring 60 not out in a crucial partnership with Perera to secure a thrilling draw and stay alive in the playoffs.
