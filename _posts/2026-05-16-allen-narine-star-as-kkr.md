**IPL 2026: Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) Stun Gujarat Titans (GT)**

In a thrilling match, the Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) edged out Gujarat Titans (GT) by 8 wickets to stay alive in the Indian Premier League (IPL) 2026. The home team dominated the proceedings with a strong batting display led by captain Shreyas Iyer, who scored a century. The spin duo of Kuldeep Yadav and Anuj Malhotra also played crucial roles in KKR's victory. GT's bowlers struggled to contain the KKR batsmen, who posted a competitive total of 218/4. With 9 balls to go, GT were down to 112/4. Kuldeep Yadav then sealed the win with a six off the last ball. The victory has kept KKR in the running to make the playoffs.
