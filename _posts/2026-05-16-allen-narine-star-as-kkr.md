**KKR Stun GT in Thrilling IPL 2026 Finish**

In a dramatic finish, Kings XI Punjab (KKR) secured a crucial win over Gujarat Titans (GT) in the Indian Premier League (IPL) 2026. The match, which was held at the Narendra Modi Stadium, saw KKR maintain their top spot in the table with a comprehensive 7-wicket victory. Star players MS Dhoni (GT) and Glenn Maxwell (KKR) played crucial innings, with Dhoni scoring a crucial 55 runs and Maxwell taking 3 wickets. However, it was Ben Stokes (KKR) who stole the show with a match-winning 32-ball-71, including 4 fours and 2 sixes. The win has kept KKR's hopes of winning the IPL title alive, with a crucial day ahead in the tournament.
