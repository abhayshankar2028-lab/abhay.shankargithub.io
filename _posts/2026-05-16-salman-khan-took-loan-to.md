**The Unlikely Rolex Affair: How Salman Khan Showed His True Colors**

In a shocking revelation, sources close to the matter have claimed that Bollywood superstar Salman Khan took a loan of Rs 9 lakh to buy a Rolex watch for the late Salim Khan, his late uncle. The incident has left many wondering how such a large loan was sanctioned, especially considering Salman's reputation as a philanthropist. When asked about the loan, Salman allegedly retorted, "Do you think you're some king? I'm the one who's giving this gift, not you." While this may seem like a tongue-in-cheek remark, it hints at Salman's more generous side. It's worth noting that this incident has sparked a lot of debate about Salman's philanthropic efforts, and it remains to be seen how this incident will affect his reputation.
