**Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) Stun Gillette Giants to Stay Alive in IPL 2026**

In a thrilling IPL match, KKR defeated Gillette Giants in a nail-biting encounter at Eden Gardens. The win maintains KKR's hopes of making it to the playoffs. The star-studded KKR team, led by captain Virat Kohli, showcased their impressive batting skills as they scored 204/6 in 32 overs. Rovaiya Narine, the team's speedster, took 2 crucial wickets to secure the win. Narine's 42-run strike partnership with Kohli helped the team to chase down the target with ease. The Giants, on the other hand, struggled to find their rhythm, losing their last 4 wickets in the final over. With this win, KKR stays alive in the competition, and they will now face the Delhi Capitals in the next match.
