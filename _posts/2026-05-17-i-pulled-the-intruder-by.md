I pulled the intruder by his shirt, threw him out: Raveena Tandon recalls 2024 mob incident

In a candid interview, Bollywood actress Raveena Tandon shared her harrowing experience from 2024 when a mob attacked and vandalized a shop owned by her close friend, alleging that the police failed to protect her. The incident left her shaken and outraged. "I pulled the intruder by his shirt, threw him out," she recounted. "I couldn't just sit back and watch my friend's business get destroyed. It was a very violent and scary experience." Raveena Tandon emphasized that the incident highlighted the need for better law enforcement and community backing. "We need to protect our own," she urged. The incident sparked widespread outrage and calls for change, emphasizing the importance of ensuring safety and security for all.
