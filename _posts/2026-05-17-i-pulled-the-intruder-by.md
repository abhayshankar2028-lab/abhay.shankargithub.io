![‘I pulled the intruder by his shirt, threw him out’: Raveena Tandon recalls 2024 mob incident](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/05/raveena.jpg)

"I'm still shaken by the events that unfolded in Mumbai last year. On June 21, 2024, I witnessed an alarming incident involving an intruder at my home. With courage and determination, I stepped in and defended my family. I recall the standoff, where the intruder attempted to flee, but I firmly held him by his shirt, pulling him back. I then threw him out of my home, ensuring his safety. My actions were swift and decisive, and I'm proud of myself for standing up for what's right. The experience left me shaken, but I'm grateful for the support of my family and friends. It's a stark reminder of the importance of standing up for our loved ones and community. I'm grateful to have such a strong support system. It's a harsh reminder of the darkness that lurks in the shadows." - Raveena Tandon
