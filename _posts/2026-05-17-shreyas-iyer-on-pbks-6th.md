Shreyas Iyer's frustration is palpable as the 6th consecutive loss drops PBKS to the bottom of the table. The 25-year-old Indian batter has been in the spotlight for his incredible form, but it seems like his team is yet to match up to his expectations. "I'm going to see the sunrise," Iyer tweeted, a message of defiance in the face of adversity. The loss has exposed the team's weaknesses, and Iyer's social media post is a reflection of his growing dissatisfaction. Despite his talent, PBKS seem to be struggling to overcome their mental blocks. With several key players out due to injuries, it's clear that the team needs a fresh start. Iyer's words are a call to action, urging his teammates to regroup and refocus, as the situation continues to deteriorate.
