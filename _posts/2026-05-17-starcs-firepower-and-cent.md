In a crucial T20I, Delhi Capitals secured a thrilling 7-wicket win against their title rivals, Sunrisers Hyderabad. The turning point in the match came when Australian pacer Josh Hazlewood (7/22) took two wickets for just 32 runs in the 32nd over. However, it was the opposing team's top order that proved to be the deciding factor. Indian sensation Jasprit Bumrah (2/0) and Suryakumar Yadav (2/1) formed a deadly duo that allowed Delhi to score at a blistering rate.

The duo's partnership, combined with Ravindra Jadeja's aggressive batting, enabled Delhi to accumulate 136 runs in 20 overs. Despite a valiant effort from Hyderabad's middle-order, Delhi Capitals held firm to secure a winner. Hazlewood's heroics helped seal the win, securing a crucial 1-run advantage in the ICC T20I points table.
