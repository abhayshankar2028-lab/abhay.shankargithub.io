**Bollywood's Unlikely Butler: Raaj Kumar's 'Jaani' Charm**

In a bizarre incident, filmmaker Raj Kumar has called everyone 'Jaani' after his adorable dog, Moji. It's a quirky move that has left many in the industry scratching their heads. But, Raj Kumar insisted that his affectionate pet was indeed tougher than his co-star, Amitabh Bachchan. "He's moody, just like me," Raaj Kumar said with a chuckle. While Bachchan's larger-than-life persona often overshadowed his co-stars, Raj Kumar's 'Jaani' moniker seems to be a fitting tribute to his furry sidekick's unique personality. The 'Jaani' treatment has also sparked a new trend in Bollywood, with several actors adopting the term to describe their pets. As for Moji, it seems like he's the real 'Jaani' of the house.
