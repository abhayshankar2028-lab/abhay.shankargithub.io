**"The Future of cricket lies in innovation"**

By Ravi Rathor, RR batting coach: "The concept of a 'slower ball' is indeed unconventional, but I'm convinced it can't be a weakness for the Sooryavanshi team. As we continue to evolve and adapt our game, I believe this approach can be a crucial part of our arsenal. The key is to understand the psychology of the batsman and the bowler, and to use this knowledge to our advantage. With the right strategy, a slower ball can be a formidable tool for the batsman, allowing them to play their shots with confidence. I'm eager to see more bowlers experiment with this concept and see how it can help our team succeed. Let's give it a try and see where it takes us."
