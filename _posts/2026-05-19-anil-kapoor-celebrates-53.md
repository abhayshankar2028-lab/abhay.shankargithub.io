I'm thrilled to share the special news that Anil Kapoor, the renowned Bollywood actor, is celebrating 53 years of togetherness with his loving wife, Sridevi. What's more, he's made the most of this milestone by hosting an intimate ceremony, which he described as a "simplest wedding, done in 30 minutes." The ceremony, held at his home, was a heartfelt and emotional affair that was attended by close friends and family members. Anil Kapoor took to social media to share the news, stating that the couple's love has stood the test of time and that they are "blessed" with each other. The couple's 53 years of togetherness are a testament to their enduring love and commitment to each other.
