"Unforgettable Pep Talks: A Tribute to Shah Rukh Khan's Influence on Kolkata Knight Riders

In a recent interview, Australian cricket legend Brett Lee reminisced about an unforgettable moment from the Kolkata Knight Riders' journey. Lee recalled the time Shah Rukh Khan, then the team's captain, delivered a pep talk before each match, leaving an indelible mark on the team's mentality. Lee described Khan as an "absolute gentleman" who would often spend hours in the team's dressing room, providing words of encouragement and guidance. The team's fortunes would often soar after Khan's motivational speeches, as he instilled in them a sense of unity and purpose. Lee's tribute to Khan's influence on the team is a testament to the power of sportsmanship and the lasting impact of a legendary personality."
