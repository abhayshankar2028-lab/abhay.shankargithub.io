IPL Magic Unfolds: Brett Lee on Shah Rukh Khan's Pep Talk

In a candid conversation with the cricket world, Australian all-rounder Brett Lee revealed that Bollywood star Shah Rukh Khan's pep talks were an integral part of the Indian Premier League's (IPL) success. When asked about the moments that turned the tide of the season, Lee shared that Khan's words of encouragement were often the turning point in their matches. "He's an absolute gentleman," Lee said with a smile. Khan's pep talks were known for being memorable and heartfelt, often going beyond cricket strategies. Lee recalled one particular instance when Khan used to tell the team to "think like a batsman" and "play like champions." It was these words of wisdom that often helped India turn the tables against their opponents. Khan's influence is undeniable, and it's no surprise that he remains an integral part of the IPL magic.
