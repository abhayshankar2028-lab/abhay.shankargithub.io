IPL 2026 Playoff Scenarios:

The stage is set for a thrilling conclusion to the 2026 season, with the playoffs reaching their climax. As the playoffs open, here's a brief overview of the key scenarios:

Reliance Royal Challengers Bangalore (RR) win over Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) would set up a crucial playoff spot for Punjab Kings (PBKS). A win would also guarantee a spot in the playoffs, making them a strong contenders for the top-four position.

The situation is different for Delhi Capitals (DC) and Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR). While a win would secure a spot in the playoffs, it would not guarantee qualification. However, a draw would push them into the playoffs, making them a significant contender.

The final scenarios would depend on how the teams perform throughout the season.
