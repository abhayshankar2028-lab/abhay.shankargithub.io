**The Paws-itive Punisher: Raaj Kumar's Jaani Razzle Dazzle**

In an attempt to add some fun to his public appearances, Bollywood actor Raj Kumar recently found himself in a humorous spot. During a recent event, he addressed his fans as 'Jaani', a term he gave to his pet dog. To his surprise, many fans started responding to him as 'Jaani' too, and the internet was abuzz with the news.

It turns out that Raj Kumar's dog, named Jaani, was as moody as its owner, and Raj Kumar was just trying to channel its spirit. While some might say it's a clever ploy to get a rise out of his fans, others just think he's being kind and affectionate. Either way, Raj Kumar's Jaani has won our hearts, and we're rooting for the two - a dog and its owner, united in their adorable antics.
