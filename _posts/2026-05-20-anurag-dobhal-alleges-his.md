**Breaking News: Anurag Dobhal Speaks Out Against Allegations**

Actor Anurag Dobhal has made a shocking revelation, claiming that his father, Rajendra Dobhal, falsely accused him of filing a "fake complaint" against him after a near-fatal car crash in 2018. According to sources close to the matter, Rajendra lodged a false complaint with the police, stating that Anurag had caused harm to his neck and head. The incident occurred when Anurag was just 35 years old, and he had sustained serious injuries. Anurag claims that he has been vindicated after his father's alleged false claims were revealed. Anurag's statement comes as a relief, and he has been praising his family members for standing by him during this challenging time. This is a significant development in the ongoing saga of Anurag Dobhal's family drama.
