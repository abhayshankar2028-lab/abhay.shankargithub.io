Ashwin, India's all-rounder and former Chennai Super Kings captain, has reacted to the team's shocking exit from the 2026 IPL tournament. In an emotional interview, Ashwin expressed his disappointment and frustration with the decision behind the team's elimination. "This is not Dhoni's CSK," Ashwin said. "I've known Dhoni for a long time, and I'm not sure what went wrong this time. We gave our all, but it was not enough. I think we underestimated some of the rival teams. We need to take a hard look at ourselves and figure out what went wrong. I'm hurt that my team didn't make it to the final," Ashwin concluded. The 29-year-old all-rounder's candid reaction has sparked a heated debate among cricket fans, with some siding with him and others defending CSK's decision-making process.
