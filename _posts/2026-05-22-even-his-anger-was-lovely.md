**The Unbridled Passion of a Legend: Bobby Deol**

Bobby Deol's latest film, "Lutoya", is a poignant reminder of his unrelenting passion for acting. Speaking to me about his father, Dharmendra, Deol pours his heart out, revealing the depth of his love and respect for the legendary actor. "Even his anger was lovely," Deol confesses, as he reminisces about his father's fiery temper. Dharmendra's unbridled energy was a driving force behind Deol's early career, propelling him to become one of the most talented actors in Bollywood. However, it was his kindness, generosity, and unwavering support that left a lasting impact on Deol's life. As he looks back on his father's legacy, Deol's words convey the profound impact he hopes to leave on his own career and the lives of those he loves.
