**"Unbridled Emotion: Bobby Deol Opens Up About His Tribute to Father Dharmendra"**

In an emotional conversation with us, actor Bobby Deol shared his heartfelt tribute to his late father, Dharmendra. The 59-year-old actor spoke fondly of his father's unwavering support and guidance throughout his life. "Even his anger was lovely," Bobby revealed, chuckling. "My father was a strong-willed man who never backed down from a fight. He taught me the importance of standing up for what I believe in." Bobby expressed his gratitude towards his father for being a constant source of inspiration and comfort. "Dharmendra was a reminder that family is not just about blood ties, but about the love and support we share with each other." Bobby's heart-wrenching tribute to his father is a testament to the profound impact he had on his life.
