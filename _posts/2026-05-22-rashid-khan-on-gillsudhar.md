**Rashid Khan's Unyielding Support for Gill-Sudharsan: A Success Story**

In a recent interview, Rashid Khan, the all-rounder, expressed his unwavering support for Gill Sujan and Sudharshan, the Indian women's cricket team. Khan praised their ability to adapt to different playing conditions and strategies, stating, "They don't change their game." This statement is particularly noteworthy, given the challenges faced by women's cricket teams worldwide. Khan's endorsement is a testament to the team's resilience and determination. The team's captain, Gill Sujan, has indeed shown remarkable leadership and skill, guiding her team to numerous victories. Khan's words of encouragement serve as a reminder that, with hard work and dedication, teams can overcome adversity and achieve success.
