![Superfan whose life Dhoni saved says he’ll be heartbroken if hero calls it day](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/05/Rambabu.jpg)

In an emotional scene, a devoted fan of Indian cricket legend MS Dhoni walked into the media center, visibly shaken. He had been watching Dhoni's India tour with unrelenting fervor, and the captain's retirement news had left him feeling lost.

"MS Dhoni saved my life," he said, his voice cracking with emotion. "If he calls it day, I'll be heartbroken. He's not just a hero to me, he's a family member. I've followed his journey from the beginning, and I've seen him grow from a young boy to a World Cup-winning captain. I've cried with him, cheered with him, and I'll miss him even more if he stops playing."

The fan's words echoed the sentiments of countless fans worldwide, who have grown attached to Dhoni's larger-than-life personality and on-field heroics.
