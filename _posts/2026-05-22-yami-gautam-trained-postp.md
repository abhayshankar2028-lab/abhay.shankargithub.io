**The Unlikely Superstar: Yami Gautam's Post-Pregnancy Journey**

In a remarkable display of dedication, Bollywood actress Yami Gautam has been training post-pregnancy, much to the surprise of her toddler who clings to her feet. The actress, who welcomed her first child with husband Rohit Shetty, has been working tirelessly to regain her strength and flexibility. While her tiny tot watches eagerly, Yami has been pushing herself to new heights. In a candid interview, the actress shared, "I wanted to prove to myself and everyone that I'm not weak after pregnancy. I'm training hard, and it's amazing to see the progress." Dhurandhar's Rizwan, who witnessed Yami's training, expressed his admiration, saying, "Yami is an inspiration to all. I'm proud to have seen her grow stronger and more determined." With her toddler by her side, Yami Gautam is an unlikely superstar in the making.
