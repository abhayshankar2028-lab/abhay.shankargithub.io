"Exclusive Interview: David Dhawan Opens Up About Varun Dhawan"

In a candid conversation with our publication, veteran Bollywood director David Dhawan shared his thoughts on his talented son Varun Dhawan. The filmmaker revealed that he often finds himself like a "cool uncle" when it comes to his son, as he has a similar energy and charm. Dhawan expressed his pride in Varun's accomplishments, stating that "everybody should have a son like him". He also shared some behind-the-scenes stories about the production of Varun's debut films, including the challenges he faced during the early stages of his career. Dhawan's words of wisdom and love for his son only reaffirmed our admiration for the Dhawan family. Their bond is truly inspiring, and it's clear that this family is a force to be reckoned with in the world of Bollywood.
