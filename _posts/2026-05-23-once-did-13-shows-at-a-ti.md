**The Struggle is Real: 13 Shows Down, 0 Auditions**

I still can't wrap my head around the reality of what I'm doing. I used to be a bright and ambitious aspiring actress, chasing dreams and making sacrifices for my craft. But after 13 shows at a time, the pressure and rejection took its toll. I'm now faced with the harsh truth: I'm not good enough.

Anita Kanwal confronts the painful reality of her career choices, questioning "why am I even living?" The answer, it seems, is no. Rejection is a cruel mistress, and I'm left to pick up the pieces of my shattered dreams. The industry is tough, and the competition is fierce. Can I really afford to keep throwing my life away? The questions swirl in my head like the wind that's been blowing through the streets of Mumbai.
