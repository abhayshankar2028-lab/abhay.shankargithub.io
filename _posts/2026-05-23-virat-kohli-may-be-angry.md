Cricket blogging by Rohit Sharma

Virat Kohli and Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) were left to ponder the what-ifs after a crushing 71-run loss to Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The team was led by a visibly frustrated Kohli, who was seen getting into an argument with the fielding coach on the field. However, we've seen Kohli bounce back from adversity before, and I think he'll be just fine.

Defending RCB's loss, former India captain Ricky Ponting weighed in on social media, saying, "Virat's a great player, but you can't win every match. Winning is just about executing the game plan and making smart decisions. You win, you lose. But you don't get ahead of yourself." I couldn't agree more, Ricky. You can only win if you learn from your losses and come back stronger.
