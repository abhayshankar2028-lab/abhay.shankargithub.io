**The Anatomy of a Collapse: How Punjab Kings Went from Favourites to Also-Rans**

The 2022 Indian Premier League season was a rollercoaster ride for the Punjab Kings, who were once considered one of the top teams in the league. However, their rise to the top was short-lived. A combination of poor performances, injuries, and a string of unfortunate events led to their downfall. Their batting lineup, which had been in top form for most of the season, began to collapse, and their bowling attack started to unravel.

As the season progressed, the Kings struggled to find consistency, and their opponents began to exploit their weaknesses. Despite a few late-season salvages, they were ultimately relegated to the also-rans list. The Punjab Kings' collapse serves as a reminder that even the most talented teams can fall victim to bad luck and poor decision-making.
