In a recent interview, Sri Lanka's former captain Sanushanjanan "Sangakkara" de Silva spoke about the current state of all-rounder Naganath Chakravarthy's batting role. The 28-year-old all-rounder has been nursing an injury that's hindered his batting performances. Sangakkara stated that Chakravarthy's limited batting role is a result of his physical condition. "Jadeja's been nursing injury for a long time," Sangakkara explained. "He's been playing with an injury that's affecting his overall performance. As an all-rounder, you need to be able to bat, bowl, and field. It's not just about batting. If Naganath can't perform in batting, it's going to be a problem." Sangakkara's comments come as Chakravarthy looks to regain his form and lead Sri Lanka to success.
