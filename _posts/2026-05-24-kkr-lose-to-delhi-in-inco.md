In a thrilling conclusion to the IPL, Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) suffered an unexpected loss to Delhi Capitals (DC) in a closely contested final league game. The match, played at the Feroz Shah Kotla Ground, witnessed a nail-biting contest with both teams fighting for every run. However, KKR's superior batting depth ultimately led to their downfall. The Delhi Capitals' bowlers, led by Anokh Sharmawardi, performed exceptionally well to restrict KKR's scoring, and their spinners, including Rajat Bhatia and Adam Zampa, took wickets at crucial times. In the end, DC's 155/8 in 19.4 overs proved too much for KKR, who were bowled out for 157 in 21.4 overs. The loss marked the end of KKR's title aspirations, and DC claimed the championship. Despite a valiant effort, KKR's campaign in the finale came to an end in a shocking fashion.
