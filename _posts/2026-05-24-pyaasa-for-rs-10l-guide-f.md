**Rare First Release Posters: A Valuable Treasure trove**

When it comes to Bollywood, there's a certain allure to owning rare first release posters of iconic films. These posters are not only a testament to the era's grandeur but also a reflection of the talent that shone through on screen. Here's a guide to help you navigate the world of vintage posters.

**Pyaasa (Rs 10L+)**
Pyaasa, a 1957 classic starring Dilip Kumar and Rajan Haksar, is a prime example of a rare first release poster. These posters, adorned with intricate designs and vintage colors, can fetch up to Rs 10,000 (approximately $1,500 USD) or more. To spot one of these gems, you'll need to scour the markets, attend film festivals, or buy from reputable dealers.

**Guide to Indian Filmmaker Posters (Rs 5L+)**
For those on a tighter budget, Indian filmmaker posters are an exciting find. Expect to pay anywhere from Rs 5,000 to Rs 50,000 (approximately $700 to $7,000 USD) for a high-grade poster. These posters often feature iconic characters or scenes from notable films, making them a valuable collectible.
