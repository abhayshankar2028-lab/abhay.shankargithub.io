**3 Brownies for Breakfast: Sara Ali Khan Reveals Her Unconventional Morning Routine**

As Sara Ali Khan shares her inspiring story, we can't help but crave a brownie for breakfast. The actress, who's an avid foodie and fitness enthusiast, took us back in time to her student days when she had an unusual morning routine. As a busy actress, Sara used to indulge in three brownies for breakfast, which became her go-to morning treat. "I used to have three brownies for breakfast, one for every day of the week," she reminisces. Her friends and family thought she was being 'mindless', but Sara attributes her unusual habit to her busy schedule. Despite her hectic film career, Sara remains committed to her fitness and healthy eating habits. Who knew that a brownie could be the key to a productive day?
