**Not a Ban**: FWICE's Ashoke Pandit Clarifies Action Against Ranveer Singh Over Don 3 Exit

In a recent statement, Bollywood's Family, Weddings & Engagement Industry (FWICE) President Ashoke Pandit has clarified the controversy surrounding Ranveer Singh's exit from the upcoming film, Don 3. The actor's decision to leave the project, citing creative differences, was met with criticism from fans and colleagues. Pandit's clarification suggests that the decision was not a ban, but rather a deliberate creative choice. "There was no ban, just a professional decision," Pandit said. The industry body emphasized that the decision was taken with discretion and respect for the filmmakers. The clarification comes as a relief to fans and actors alike, who had been divided on the matter. Pandit's words have injected a sense of calm into the industry, assuring that creative differences are taken with respect.
