IPL 2026 Absence Sparks Debate, Yash Dayal Opens Up

In a recent interview, Indian cricketer Yash Dayal has finally broken his silence on his absence from the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) squad in the IPL 2026. The 26-year-old all-rounder had been missing from the team's practice sessions and training exercises for several weeks, sparking rumors of a potential rift with coach Simon Katilelana.

Dayal, however, has vehemently denied any issues with the team or his coaches, stating that he should be there for the entire season. "I should be there," he said. "I've been working hard, and I don't want to be a part of any drama. I'm not trying to hurt anyone; I just want to play cricket at the highest level." Dayal's comments have sparked a heated debate among fans, with some feeling he's not pulling his weight and others defending his absence.
