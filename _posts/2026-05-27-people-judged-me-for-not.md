**The Unspoken Judgment: My Experience as a Non-English Speaker in Bollywood**

As Pankaj Tripathi, I've had my fair share of experiences where people judged me for not speaking English. It's a common phenomenon, but it's one that still hurts. I've found myself in situations where my inability to communicate in English has led to misunderstandings and hurt feelings. One such instance occurred when I was auditioning for a prominent Bollywood film. I recall walking into the audition room, confidently speaking my lines in Hindi, and being met with a mixture of curiosity and suspicion. The casting director, confused, asked me to speak in English, and I was left feeling frustrated and embarrassed. It's experiences like these that make me appreciate the importance of cultural sensitivity and awareness. We should strive to understand and respect each other's backgrounds, rather than making assumptions based on limited knowledge.
