In a stunning revelation, Bollywood actress Shahana Goswami recently opened up about her unconventional relationship preferences. The 30-year-old actress revealed that she has engaged in multiple open relationships, but categorically stated that her experiences are never casual. According to Goswami, her openness is not about seeking excitement or variety in her love life, but rather a genuine desire for human connection and emotional intimacy. "I've had my fair share of casual relationships, but I never feel like they're truly fulfilling," she stated. Goswami's statement highlights the complexities of modern relationships and the need for open communication in matters of the heart. Her words serve as a reminder that love is not just about physical intimacy, but also about emotional and intellectual connection.
