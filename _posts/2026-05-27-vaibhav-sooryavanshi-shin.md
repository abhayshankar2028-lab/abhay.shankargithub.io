![Vaibhav Sooryavanshi shines as RR eliminate SRH to reach IPL 2026 Qualifier 2](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/05/sooryavanshi-express-photo-kamleshwar-1.jpg)

In a thrilling IPL 2024, Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) defeated Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) in a nail-biting match, securing their spot in the IPL Qualifier 2. Vaibhav Sooryavanshi, the RCB skipper, stole the show with his outstanding performance. He scored a blistering 92 runs off 65 deliveries, including six fours and a six, to lead his team to victory.

Sooryavanshi's impressive display sealed a convincing 18-run win for RCB. His leadership and batting prowess were instrumental in securing the pole position for the Qualifier 2. With this victory, RCB will face off against Mumbai Indians in the Qualifier 2 showdown. Sooryavanshi's impressive performance has solidified his position as one of the top batsmen in the league.
