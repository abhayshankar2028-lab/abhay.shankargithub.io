![Vaibhav Sooryavanshi shines as RR eliminate SRH to reach IPL 2026 Qualifier 2](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/05/sooryavanshi-express-photo-kamleshwar-1.jpg)

Vaibhav Sooryavanshi's phenomenal display sealed a thrilling victory for Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) as they defeated Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) in the Indian Premier League (IPL) Qualifier 2 match. With a batting score of 157/4, RCB chased down the target with ease, led by Sooryavanshi's 78 not out. The Indian bowler was instrumental in RCB's triumph, taking 3/34 in 22.1 overs. Sooryavanshi's dream run to become the first player to score 10+ fours in a single innings was a testament to his exceptional batting skills. RCB's victory will surely propel them to the top spot in the IPL standings, with a 1-point edge over Kolkata Knight Riders. This win cements Sooryavanshi's status as one of the top players in the league, and fans eagerly await his next innings.
