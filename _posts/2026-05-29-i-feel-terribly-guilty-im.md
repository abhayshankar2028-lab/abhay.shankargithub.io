![‘I feel terribly guilty’: Imtiaz Ali on fans leaving careers after watching Tamasha](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/05/Tmasha.jpg)

"I'm still trying to process what happened to the crew and cast of Tamasha. What was supposed to be a grand finale for the film turned into a devastating blow. Fans were left feeling terribly guilty after watching the film, and the disappointment was palpable. I think it's natural to feel this way, especially when a film is as emotionally resonant as Tamasha. The mix of romance, drama, and music was expertly woven, and the characters were so well-developed that it was hard not to become invested. But the fans' reaction was a harsh reality check. I remember seeing tweets and messages pouring in from fans, expressing regret for leaving their careers to watch the film. It's a harsh reminder that even the most talented individuals can't escape the pressures of the industry. It's a bittersweet experience, and one that I'll remember for a long time."
