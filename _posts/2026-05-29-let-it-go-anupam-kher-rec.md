**A Reunion of Sorts: Anupam Kher Reflects on Naseeruddin Shah's Apology**

The Bollywood community was left stunned last week when Naseeruddin Shah publicly apologized to Anupam Kher for their past spat. The delay in this apology was met with warmth and appreciation by Kher, who expressed gratitude for Shah's honesty. In an exclusive conversation with us, Kher reminisced about their years-long feud, stating, "It was a very bitter spat, but I've learned to let go and move forward." Their candid conversation shed light on the underlying issues that led to their disagreement. Shah's apology was a masterstroke, bringing closure to the long-standing feud. Kher's emotional response highlights the power of forgiveness and the importance of moving forward, even in the face of adversity. Their story serves as a reminder that sometimes, letting go can be the key to healing.
