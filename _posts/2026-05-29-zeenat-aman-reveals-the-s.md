**Zeena Khan's X-Factor: Unraveling the Mystery Behind 'So Millennial Coded' Instagram Handle**

Actress Zeenat Aman recently opened up about the origin of her Instagram handle, 'SoMillennialCoded'. I figured 'so' added a touch of whimsy and playfulness to my handle." The 'so' code also represents the "social media culture" Zeenat is a part of, where humor and irony play a big role. Her fans seem to appreciate the cleverness of the handle, and it's clear that Zeenat's Instagram handle has become an integral part of her brand.
