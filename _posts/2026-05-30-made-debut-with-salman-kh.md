**From Bollywood to Hospitality: Rajeev Verma's Journey from Royalty to Ramelan Hotel**

In a shocking turn of events, Bollywood star Rajeev Verma made his debut as a hotelier with the launch of Ramelan Hotel in Bhopal, the capital of Madhya Pradesh. This is a significant departure from his previous ventures in the film industry. Verma, known for his iconic roles in films like "Koi... Kya Kaho?" and "Mujhse Aish", has traded in his glitzy stardom for a more humble role. According to sources, Verma's decision to leave the film industry was not taken lightly, as he had been working tirelessly for over a decade. "I wanted to explore a new passion and give back to society," Verma said in an interview. With Ramelan Hotel, Verma is focusing on providing quality hospitality to the city's residents.
