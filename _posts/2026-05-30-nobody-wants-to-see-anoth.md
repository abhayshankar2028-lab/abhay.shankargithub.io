**"Arun Govil on the Backlash: 'Nobody Wants to See Another Ram'?"**

As we dive into the world of Hindi cinema, it's not uncommon to see actors facing backlash for their choices. Take Ranbir Kapoor's decision to play Rama in the recent adaptation of the Ramayana. While many were pleased with the choice, others were less than enthusiastic.

In an exclusive interview, Arun Govil, the director of the film, shared his thoughts on the backlash. "The truth is, I don't think Ranbir was the right choice for Rama," he said. "Nobody wants to see another Ram." Govil's sentiments echo those of many fans, who felt that Kapoor's portrayal of the legendary hero was marred by his inexperience. While some might disagree, Govil's candid assessment highlights the often-debated nature of casting in Bollywood films.
