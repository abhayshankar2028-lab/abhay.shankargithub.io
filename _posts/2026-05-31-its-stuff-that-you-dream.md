![‘It’s stuff that you dream of’: Virat Kohli after guiding RCB to 2nd title](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/06/Kohli-132.jpg)

In a post-match interview after Royal Challengers Bangalore's (RCB) 2nd title win, Virat Kohli reflected on the journey that has led them to this moment. "It's stuff that you dream of," he said, his eyes sparkling with emotion. "Two years of hard work, dedication, and teamwork have finally paid off. The feeling of lifting the title is an incredible one, and we're all over the moon." Kohli praised his teammates for their outstanding performance, saying, "We were all in this together, and it was a collective effort. We played for each other, and it showed." With this championship win, RCB has cemented their status as one of the top teams in the league, and Kohli's leadership has been instrumental in their success.
