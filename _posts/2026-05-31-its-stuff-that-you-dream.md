![‘It’s stuff that you dream of’: Virat Kohli after guiding RCB to 2nd title](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/06/Kohli-132.jpg)

"It's a bittersweet feeling for Virat Kohli and the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) as they come close to their second title. We caught up with Kohli after their thrilling victory, and he spoke candidly about the journey that led to this moment.

'When we started this season, our goal was to win the title,' Kohli said. 'We knew it wouldn't be easy, but we had a great squad and a lot of hard work put in. We've come a long way, and it's an incredible feeling to know that our efforts have paid off.'

Kohli praised the team's dedication and teamwork, saying, 'We've been through ups and downs, but we've stuck together and supported each other. This is an amazing feeling, and I'm so proud of every single one of them.'

RCB's title win is a testament to Kohli's leadership and the team's resilience."
