**Bhuvneshwar Kumar's Wobbly Seam: The Key to IPL Resurgence**

In a recent interview, former Indian seamer Bhuvneshwar Kumar revealed that his wobbly seam was a crucial factor in the team's successful resurgence in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The 32-year-old pacer had struggled with consistency in his early years, but over the past two seasons, he has found his rhythm, particularly with his wobbly seam. This unique delivery, which drops sharply at 90km, is perfect for slow-moving batters, who are often caught off guard. Kumar's wobble has been instrumental in his success, as he has become one of the top wingers in the league. His newfound confidence and seam control have also led to a significant decline in his injury record, cementing his place as one of the top bowlers in the IPL.
