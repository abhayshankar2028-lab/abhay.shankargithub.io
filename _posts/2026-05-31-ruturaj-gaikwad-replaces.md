![Ruturaj Gaikwad replaces injured Riyan Parag in India A squad for SL tri-series](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/05/Rutu-Parag.jpg)

Ruturaj Gaikwad has been drafted into the India A squad for the upcoming Sri Lanka Tri-Series against Australia. This announcement comes after Riyan Parag, who suffered a knee ligament injury, failed his medical test. Gaikwad, an Unmukt Narine and Bhuvneshwar Kumar protégé, will replace Parag in the squad. With his impressive batting form in the Indian domestic circuit, Gaikwad's inclusion in the squad is expected to boost India's chances in the upcoming series. As a skilled opening batsman, Gaikwad is known for his aggressive and destructive batting style, which could make him a valuable asset to the Indian A team. This move is seen as a positive step by the BCCI, who are eager to see Gaikwad's potential in the A-team.
