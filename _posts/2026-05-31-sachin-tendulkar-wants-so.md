**Sachin Tendulkar's Test Future Uncertain**

In a recent interview, Indian cricket legend Sachin Tendulkar expressed his desire to play Test cricket again, but his plans are being put on hold. When asked about his future in the format, Tendulkar said he wants to play Test cricket but is not sure if he will be called up. While he appreciated the opportunity to play in ODIs and T20Is, Tendulkar feels he needs to prove himself in Tests again. "I have played enough Test cricket, and I want to play again," he said. However, he also emphasized that the selectors will ultimately decide his fate. Tendulkar's statement has sparked debate among fans and pundits, with many wondering if he will be given another chance in the Test format.
