TigerPrint Films' TP Aggarwal is taking a bold move to challenge the Indian government's ban on the Ranveer Singh film. The filmmaker has filed a petition in the Supreme Court, seeking a review of the ban on the movie. Aggarwal's petition contended that the ban is based on a misleading report by a filmmaker and that it has caused severe losses to the film industry. The Supreme Court had initially granted interim relief to Ranveer Singh's film, allowing it to start production despite the ban. However, the court later lifted this order, allowing the film to resume production. Aggarwal's petition now seeks a review of this order.
