IPL 2026 has officially kicked off, and the excitement is palpable. One player who has left a lasting impression so far is Harbhajan Singh's protégé, Adam Buttler. The English batter has been instrumental in Nottinghamshire's thrilling campaign, including their thrilling comeback win over Northamptonshire. When asked about the impact of the new generation of players, Buttler gushed about Rohit Sharma's Sooryavanshi's (his batting coach) influence on the team. "He was the IPL this year," Buttler said with a grin. "His coaching, his leadership, everything was outstanding. We learned so much from him and it's been an incredible ride so far. The young players have grown exponentially, and we're excited to see what the future holds." With Buttler's praise, it's clear that Sooryavanshi's coaching has made a significant impact on the team's performance.
