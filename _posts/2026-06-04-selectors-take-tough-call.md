Selectors have taken a tough call on Suryakumar Yadav's selection for India's T20I series against Australia. The skipper's impressive form in the series was marred by his limited T20I innings, with the latter being a shocking decline from his usual standards. The selectors had to make a difficult decision after considering other talented players like Ishan Kishan and Umesh Yadav. Suryakumar's performances in the series, where he scored just 18 runs in 6 matches, were a concern for the selectors. His batting average in the series was just 3.75, which was a concern for his future in the T20I format. The selectors' decision to drop Suryakumar has left the fans and the players wondering what could have been done differently.
