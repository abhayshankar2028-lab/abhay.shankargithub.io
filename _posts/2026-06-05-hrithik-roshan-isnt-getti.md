"Hrithik Roshan's Career Crisis: A Look at the Unwilling Hero

Hrithik Roshan, one of Bollywood's most revered actors, has been struggling to land the roles he desires. In an interview with a leading magazine, the actor confessed that directors are increasingly wary of casting him as the 'good guy' in their films. This sentiment echoes a growing trend in Indian cinema, where complex, nuanced characters are no longer the norm. Roshan's previous films, such as 'Kaho Naa... Pyaar Hai' and 'Golmaal 3', showcased his range as an actor, but it appears that directors are now hesitant to pit him against the likes of Salman Khan or Shah Rukh Khan. As a result, Roshan finds himself facing stiff competition from more established stars. Can he overcome this hurdle to reclaim his position as Bollywood's leading man?"
